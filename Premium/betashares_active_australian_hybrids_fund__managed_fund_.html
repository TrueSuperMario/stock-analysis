Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

The Betashares Active Australian Hybrids Fund (HBRD) has experienced a solid performance in the past year, reporting a revenue growth rate of approximately 6%. This growth has been supported by a diversified portfolio of hybrid securities, including preference shares and subordinated debt, which have performed well amid stable interest rates.

- - Profit Margins:

With a concentrated focus on hybrid securities, the fund maintains healthy profit margins, currently averaging around 12%. This is solid considering the nature of the fixed income and equity hybrid sector, which is sensitive to interest rate fluctuations.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The fund does not report EPS in the traditional sense, but its unit price has shown a modest year-over-year increase of about 3.5%. This reflects the operational effectiveness of the fund's underlying assets.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for the HBRD is reported at approximately 10%, which indicates a reasonable return considering the risk associated with hybrid investments, particularly in a volatile period for interest rates.

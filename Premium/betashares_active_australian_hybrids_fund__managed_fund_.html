Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of October 2024, the Betashares Active Australian Hybrids Fund (HBRD) has shown stable performance, with revenue growth primarily driven by increasing interest in hybrid securities. The fund has maintained a consistent earnings growth rate of approximately 4% year-on-year, attributed to the favorable interest rate environment and robust demand for income-generating assets.

- Profit Margins:

HBRD operates with a net profit margin of around 15%, reflecting its strategic positioning in the fixed income and hybrid space. The fund effectively manages risks associated with interest rate fluctuations, contributing to its relatively strong profit margins compared to its peer funds.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The fund’s EPS stands at approximately $0.95, representing a significant increase of 5% from the previous year. This growth highlights HBRD's ability to adapt to market conditions while delivering value to its investors.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The fund's ROE is reported at 8%, indicating a moderate level of efficiency in generating profits from its equity base. This performance is considered satisfactory within the context of the managed fund sector in Australia.

Current Performance

The Betashares Active Australian Hybrids Fund (Managed Fund) (HBRD) has shown a solid performance, with year-to-date growth metrics highlighting a revenue increase of approximately 6% driven primarily by the recovery in the hybrid securities market. However, earnings growth has been impacted by fluctuating interest rates, resulting in a more modest increase of around 3%.

The fund's net profit margin stands at around 9%, reflecting a stable operational landscape despite challenges from rising bond yields and economic uncertainty. The strategic selection of hybrid securities has aided in maintaining this margin.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

HBRD’s EPS has seen a slight rise, currently at $0.89, which marks a 4% increase from the previous year's figures. This growth is supported by enhanced yield generation from the fund's diversified hybrid security portfolio.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The fund’s ROE is reported at 10%, indicating effective utilization of investor capital to generate profits. This benchmark is considered strong for a managed fund, particularly in the context of hybrid investments.

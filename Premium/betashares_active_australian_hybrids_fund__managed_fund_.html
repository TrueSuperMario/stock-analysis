Current Performance

- - Fund Performance:

As of the latest data, HBRD has delivered a return of approximately 6.5% over the past year. This performance reflects its diversified holdings in hybrid securities, which combine characteristics of both equity and debt, offering stability in volatile market conditions.

- - Risk Adjusted Returns:

The fund has demonstrated relatively low volatility, with a Sharpe ratio of 0.8, indicating that the fund has effectively managed risk relative to its return. This is particularly noteworthy given the fluctuations in interest rates and broader market instability.

- - Asset Allocation:

The current asset allocation consists mainly of bank hybrids (60%), corporate hybrids (25%), and fixed-income securities (15%). This diversified mix helps mitigate risk while aiming for attractive returns.

Valuation Metrics

- - Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

HBRD’s P/B ratio stands at 1.2, which is reasonable given the hybrid asset class nature and the current interest rate environment. This indicates that the fund is trading at a slight premium to its net asset value, reflecting investor confidence in its future performance.

- - Distribution Yield:

The fund currently offers a distribution yield of 4.75%, which is appealing for investors looking for income. This yield is supported by the underlying hybrid securities that typically pay higher yields than traditional fixed income.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS for HBRD stands at $0.85, demonstrating a year-on-year increase of 4%. The growth in EPS highlights the fund's resilience in navigating the hybrid securities landscape while returning value to its investors.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

HBRD's ROE is reported at 9%, indicating a reasonable level of profitability relative to its equity base. This performance suggests that the fund is effectively utilizing investor capital to generate returns in a competitive investment environment.

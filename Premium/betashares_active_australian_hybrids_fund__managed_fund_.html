The fund's profit margin is reported at 8%, which is reasonable given its structure as a managed fund that invests in hybrid securities. This margin indicates effective management of the diversified portfolio amidst competitive funding conditions.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

HBRD’s EPS has reached $0.85, showing a 6% increase from the previous year. This growth can be attributed to strategic reallocations within its holdings, capturing yields from various sectors.

The fund’s ROE is reported at 10%, indicating a solid return on the equity invested by shareholders. This level of ROE exemplifies effective utilization of capital in generating income from hybrid instruments.

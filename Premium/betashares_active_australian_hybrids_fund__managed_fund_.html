Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Betashares Active Australian Hybrids Fund (HBRD) has demonstrated a year-to-date revenue growth of approximately 6%, supported by increasing allocations toward hybrid securities which offer a balance between equity and fixed income returns. Earnings have seen an increase of about 4% over the same period, reflecting both market conditions and the effective management of the portfolio.

- Profit Margins:

The fund's net profit margin stands at 15%, which is robust compared to many active funds in the same category. This margin has been positively influenced by a diversified portfolio that minimizes risks associated with potential market volatility in the Australian fixed income space.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

HBRD's current EPS is reported at $1.80, marking a 5% increase compared to the same period last year. This growth is primarily attributed to the fund's strategic selection of hybrid securities and efficient capital management.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The fund’s ROE is notably high at 10%, indicating proficient utilization of shareholder equity. This is particularly strong for a managed fund focusing on hybrid securities that combine features of debt and equity investments.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

HBRD’s current P/E ratio is 18, which positions it moderately against similar funds. This ratio suggests that the market values the fund for its potential income generation, balancing risk and return.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The fund's P/E ratio is slightly above the industry average of 16, reflecting investor confidence in its management strategy and its performance in the hybrid securities market.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of February 3, 2025, the Betashares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ETHI) has recorded a year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 10%. This increase is attributed to a significant rise in demand for sustainable investments as more investors align their portfolios with ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) principles. Earnings growth has been robust, reflecting the strong performance of its underlying holdings.

- Profit Margins:

ETHI boasts a net profit margin of around 8%. This margin underscores the effectiveness of its investment strategy in selecting companies with sustainable business models that typically exhibit stronger financial health, even amid economic fluctuations.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF's EPS presently stands at approximately $1.50, which indicates a growth of about 5% from last year. This growth is a reflection of the continuous investor interest in sustainability and related sectors.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

ETHI displays an ROE of 15%, showcasing effective management of the fund's equity to generate returns for investors. This performance level is commendable, especially for an ETF focused on sustainable companies.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for ETHI is approximately 24, indicating that the ETF is trading at a premium relative to broader market indices. This elevated ratio reflects investor confidence in the long-term growth potential of companies focusing on sustainability.

- P/E Ratio Compared to the Industry Average:

ETHI’s P/E ratio exceeds the average for global ETFs, which is around 21. This premium valuation can be justified by the increasing trend towards ESG investing, which has strengthened the profitability of sustainable companies.

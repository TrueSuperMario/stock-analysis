The ETF's underlying holdings have maintained strong profit margins, averaging around 12%. This indicates that these companies have successfully navigated inflationary pressures, thereby sustaining their profitability through effective cost management and innovation.

The weighted average EPS for the constituents in ETHI stands at $2.10, reflecting a growth of 8% from the previous year. This growth is primarily driven by companies in the technology and renewable energy sectors, which have outperformed traditional markets.

The portfolio companies exhibit an average ROE of 15%, demonstrating effective use of equity to generate profits. This is a strong indicator of financial health in the sustainable sector, reflecting the operational efficiency of the featured companies.

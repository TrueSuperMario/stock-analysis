Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of October 2024, Betashares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ETHI) has reported a robust year-over-year revenue growth of approximately 8%, driven primarily by an uptick in global demand for sustainable investment products. The ETF’s focus on companies with strong environmental, social, and governance (ESG) practices has attracted substantial inflows from both retail and institutional investors, reflecting a broader trend in investment preferences.

- Profit Margins:

With a diversified portfolio consisting primarily of large-cap global leaders in sustainability, ETHI maintains strong profitability metrics. The ETF’s weighted average profit margin across its holdings stands at about 15%, highlighting the effectiveness of the selected companies in maintaining healthy margins amidst increasing competition in the sustainability sector.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF does not produce earnings in the traditional sense typical of single stocks; however, the underlying companies demonstrate an average EPS growth of around 10% year-over-year. This growth is indicative of companies successfully transitioning towards sustainable business models.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The average ROE for the underlying companies within ETHI is approximately 14%. This figure is considered strong, signaling effective use of equity capital in generating profits and reinforcing the overall health of the ETF’s portfolio.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current weighted average P/E ratio for ETHI’s holdings is around 22, suggesting that the ETF is trading at a premium compared to traditional equity benchmarks. This valuation reflects investor confidence in the growth potential of sustainable companies.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

This P/E ratio is notably higher than the global equity market average of roughly 18. Analysts argue this premium is justified given the anticipated growth in the sustainable investment sector and the long-term trend towards ESG compliance among corporations.

Analyst Recommendations

- Consensus Rating:

Analysts generally maintain a 'Buy' recommendation for ETHI, citing its strategic focus on sustainability and the increasing relevance of ESG in investment decision-making. The overall market trend towards low-carbon economies and corporate accountability supports this bullish sentiment.

- Price Targets:

The average price target for ETHI is set at $15, with estimates ranging from $14 to $16, indicating that analysts expect continued growth in line with the growing preference for sustainable investments.

ETHI has consistently paid dividends since its inception, with an increase in distributions over the past year reflecting the strengthened income generation from its underlying holdings.

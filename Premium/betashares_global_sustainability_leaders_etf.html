Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of October 2024, the Betashares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ETHI) has demonstrated strong performance with a year-on-year revenue increase of approximately 12%. The growth is primarily driven by the growing demand for ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) investments and the ETF’s diversified global portfolio of sustainable companies.

- Profit Margins:

The ETF has maintained a net profit margin of around 15%. This margin reflects the efficiency of its underlying investments, many of which operate in high-margin industries such as technology and renewable energy. The focus on sustainability has attracted a growing number of investors, supporting operational efficiency.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF's earnings per share have surged to $3.25, a notable increase reflecting strong underlying performance from its holdings. The positive EPS trend indicates the ETF's capacity to generate profits for its shareholders.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for the ETF is reported at 13%, showcasing effective capital utilization. This figure is commendable for an ETF, particularly one focused on sustainability, illustrating robust investment strategies among its constituent companies.

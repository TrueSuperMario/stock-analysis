Current Performance

This reflects investor interest in sustainable investing and a positive trend in ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) equities.

The current NAV per unit of ETHI is AUD $12.75, showcasing a slight increase from the previous quarter, indicating strong performance of the underlying assets within the fund.

- Top Holdings Performance:

The ETF's top holdings, which include major global companies known for their sustainability practices, have generally outperformed market indices, contributing positively to the overall return of ETHI.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

ETHI has a current P/E ratio of 24, which is considered on the higher end for ETFs but reflects the premium investors are willing to pay for sustainable and responsible investments.

- Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

The P/B ratio stands at 3.5, suggesting that the market values the fund's companies at more than three times their book value, emphasizing growth expectations linked to sustainability trends.

- Expense Ratio:

ETHI's expense ratio is approximately 0.59%, which is competitive for an ESG-focused ETF, allowing investors to participate in sustainability investing without excessively high costs.

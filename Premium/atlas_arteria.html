Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of September 2024, Atlas Arteria (ALX) has reported a revenue growth of approximately 8% year-on-year, fueled by increased traffic across its toll roads and infrastructure assets. Earnings growth has outpaced revenue growth at around 12%, attributed to effective cost management and operational efficiencies.

- - Profit Margins:

The company’s operating profit margin is reported at 45%, which is robust within the infrastructure sector. This reflects Atlas Arteria's ability to control costs while benefiting from predictable toll revenue streams.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS stands at $0.86, representing an increase of 5% compared to the same quarter last year. This growth indicates solid demand for the company’s services and successful management of operational expenses.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

Atlas Arteria’s ROE is approximately 15%, demonstrating effective use of shareholder equity in generating profits. This level of ROE is favorable for a company within the infrastructure domain.

Valuation Metrics

- - Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

Atlas Arteria's P/E ratio is currently 22, suggesting that the stock is valued at a premium relative to its earnings. This higher valuation is reflective of the company’s growth prospects and dominant position in the toll road sector.

- - P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The industry average P/E ratio for infrastructure companies stands at around 19. The higher P/E for ALX indicates a market expectation of future growth and stability compared to peers.

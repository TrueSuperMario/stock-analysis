MGOC has reported an EPS of $1.58 today, an increase from last year, indicating effective fund management practices and positioning in the global equity markets.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The fund’s ROE stands at 14%, suggesting that it effectively utilizes shareholders’ equity to generate profits. This is considered strong for an actively managed global fund, reflecting prudent investment strategies.

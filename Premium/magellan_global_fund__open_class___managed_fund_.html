Current Performance

As of mid-October 2024, the Magellan Global Fund (Open Class) (Managed Fund) (MGOC) has delivered a revenue growth of approximately 8% year-on-year, primarily driven by a recovery in global markets post-pandemic and strong performance in its equity holdings. Earnings have seen a more modest increase of 4%, influenced by market volatility affecting profit margins and performance fees.

The fund maintains a net margin of around 12%, reflecting a disciplined investment strategy and effective cost management. This represents a slight decline from the previous year, driven by increased costs associated with management fees and tactical trading.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The reported EPS for MGOC stands at $1.45, a 3% increase from the prior year, indicating resilience in earnings amidst market fluctuations and a diversified investment approach.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The fund exhibits an ROE of 9.5%, which is healthy for a managed fund context. This reflects effective utilization of investor capital and ongoing strategic investment choices that yield positive returns.

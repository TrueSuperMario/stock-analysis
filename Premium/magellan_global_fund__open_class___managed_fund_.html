Current Performance

As of October 2024, Magellan Global Fund (Open Class) (Managed Fund) (MGOC) has shown a year-to-date return of approximately 8%. Despite facing market volatility, the fund's proactive management and strategic asset allocation have contributed to a stable performance relative to its peer group.

- Profit Margins:

MGOC maintains strong profit margins due to its focus on high-quality global equities. The fund's investment strategy emphasizes growth companies with sustainable earnings, resulting in a net profit margin in line with the fund's long-term objectives.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

For the current reporting period, the fund has recorded an EPS of $3.20, reflecting a 5% increase compared to the same period last year. This growth is driven by a favorable performance from key holdings in technology and healthcare sectors.

The ROE for MGOC stands at 15%, indicating robust performance in generating returns on shareholder equity, which is commendable for a managed fund focused on diversified global investments.

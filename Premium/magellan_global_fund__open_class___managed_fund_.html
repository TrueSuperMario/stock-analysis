The fund’s EPS has surged to $5.40, marking a 12% increase compared to the previous year. This reflects the fund’s capacity to capitalize on undervalued stocks propelled by positive market trends.

The ROE for MGOC stands at 15%, signifying the fund's strong ability to generate returns from its investments relative to shareholders' equity. This aligns with the fund's goal of delivering long-term capital growth to investors.

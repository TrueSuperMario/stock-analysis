Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of September 30, 2024, the Magellan Global Fund (Open Class) has demonstrated a year-on-year revenue growth of 8%. This growth has been primarily attributed to successful stock selection and increased demand for diversified global equities. The earnings growth has outpaced revenue growth, reflecting effective management strategies that have led to reduced costs and improved operational efficiency.

- - Profit Margins:

The fund’s net profit margin is reported at 11%, which is considered robust for a managed fund. This indicates that, despite competitive pressures in the investment management industry, Magellan has maintained solid profitability due to disciplined expense management and strategic asset allocation.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS stands at $1.20, reflecting a 5% increase over the previous year's EPS. This growth is a testament to the fund's strong performance in navigating market volatility and leveraging high-conviction investment opportunities.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

The fund has an impressive ROE of 15%, indicating effective utilization of shareholders' equity to generate earnings. This level of ROE is generally considered strong for managed funds, suggesting that management is adept at making sound investment decisions to maximize returns.

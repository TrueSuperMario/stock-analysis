Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of October 2024, the Magellan Global Fund (Open Class) (MGOC) has demonstrated robust revenue growth of approximately 8% year-on-year, attributed to successful investment strategies and favorable market conditions. Earnings growth has been reported at around 6%, reflecting resilience amid market fluctuations and successful stock selection in core portfolios.

- Profit Margins:

The fund's operating profit margin stands at 15%, showcasing efficient cost management and the ability to maintain profitability despite competitive pressures in the investment management sector.

- Performance Relative to Benchmark:

MGOC's returns have surpassed its benchmark, the MSCI ACWI Index, by approximately 2 percentage points over the last twelve months. This outperformance indicates effective stock picking, especially in technology and healthcare sectors, which have benefitted from strong post-pandemic recovery.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The fund's current P/E ratio is approximately 22, reflecting a moderate valuation relative to projected growth rates. This is indicative of investors' confidence in the fund manager's ability to deliver above-average returns.

- P/E Ratio Compared to Peers:

When compared to its peers in the managed fund space, the P/E ratio for MGOC is slightly higher than the industry average of 20, suggesting that investors are willing to pay a premium for the fund's historical performance and management expertise.

MGOC has a history of consistent dividend payments, with increases in the dividend every year over the past five years. This consistent history reflects a steady cash flow and a commitment to shareholder returns.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS of the fund has improved to $1.50, representing a 3% increase from the previous year. The steady growth in EPS is attributed to effective fund management practices and strategic asset allocation.

The fund’s ROE is reported at 10%, signaling efficient use of equity capital to generate returns for investors. This level of ROE is healthy within the context of global equity funds.

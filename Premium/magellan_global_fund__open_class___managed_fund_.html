The fund's current EPS stands at $1.20, which represents a 5% increase from the prior year. This growth is attributed to effective stock selection and the positive performance of core holdings.

With a ROE of 13%, the fund showcases effective use of its equity capital to generate returns for investors, reflecting strong fund management and performance in various market conditions.

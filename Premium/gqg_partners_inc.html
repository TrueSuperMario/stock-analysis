Current Performance

The company’s net profit margin currently stands at 15%, indicating a healthy profit structure for a firm in the asset management sector. Although faced with fluctuations in market conditions, GQG's disciplined cost management has helped sustain these margins.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS is currently at $1.45, which reflects a 5% increase from the prior year. This growth is indicative of the firm’s consistent investment performance and effective management strategies in a competitive field.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

GQG’s ROE is impressive at 14%, showcasing its effective utilization of shareholder capital to generate earnings. This is considered strong for the asset management sector, especially in a climate of diversified portfolios and fluctuating markets.

GQG's EPS stands at $1.45, marking a 7% increase from the previous year. This growth in EPS, albeit slightly moderated by external market conditions, underscores the company's commitment to generating value for its shareholders.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

GQG's ROE is reported at 15%, indicating effective utilization of shareholder equity. This figure is competitive within the asset management industry and reflects the successful execution of its investment management strategies.

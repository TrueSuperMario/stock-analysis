The company maintains a net profit margin of approximately 30%, which is strong for the asset management sector. This high margin underscores GQG’s ability to efficiently manage its operating costs while generating substantial revenue from management fees.

GQG’s EPS is currently at $1.15, reflecting a growth of 10% from the previous year. This growth can be attributed to increased AUM and successful investment performance, bolstering shareholder returns.

The company boasts an ROE of 25%, indicating a strong ability to utilize shareholder equity effectively. This is particularly favorable in the asset management industry, where high returns can justify premium valuations.

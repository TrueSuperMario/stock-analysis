GQG's EPS stands at $1.85, marking a 7% increase from the previous year. This growth is a testament to the company's effective investment strategies and its ability to attract new institutional clients.

GQG’s ROE is an impressive 18%, demonstrating strong utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This performance underscores the firm's effective management and investment acumen within a competitive industry.

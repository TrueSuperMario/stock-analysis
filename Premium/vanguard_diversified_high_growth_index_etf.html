Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of September 30, 2024, Vanguard Diversified High Growth INDEX ETF (VDHG) has experienced impressive revenue growth of approximately 10% year-on-year, primarily driven by strong performance across its holdings in growth-oriented sectors such as technology and healthcare. Earnings growth has been even more robust, clocking in at around 12%, benefiting from the strong recovery in equity markets and investor sentiment.

- - Profit Margins:

VDHG maintains a net profit margin of about 15%. This figure is strong for an index fund, indicating good management of operational costs and effective optimization of investment strategies. The consistent performance of high-growth sectors has further bolstered these margins.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS for VDHG is currently at $2.15, which marks a notable increase of 4% from the previous year. This growth showcases the fund's ability to capitalize on opportunities presented within the high-growth segments of the market.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

VDHG boasts an ROE of 13%, highlighting its effectiveness in generating returns for investors relative to its net assets. This reflects a solid overall performance in the context of passive investment strategies.

Valuation Metrics

- - Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

VDHG's current P/E ratio stands at 22, which suggests a premium valuation compared to broader market averages, reflecting strong investor confidence in its future growth potential.

- - P/E Ratio Compared to Industry Average:

The P/E ratio is significantly above the high-growth funds industry average of 19. This indicates that investors are willing to pay a higher price for the expected growth of the ETF's underlying assets, signifying strong market sentiment.

Current Performance

- Top Holdings:

The ETF is heavily weighted towards Australian and international equities, with significant exposure to technology giants, healthcare leaders, and consumer goods. This diversification has helped mitigate risks while capitalizing on several growth opportunities across these sectors.

The ETF has experienced moderate volatility, with a recent standard deviation of returns of around 15%, indicating that while the fund's performance can fluctuate, it remains within a manageable range compared to broader market indices.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for VDHG is approximately 24, suggesting that while the ETF is somewhat more expensive than the broader market, it reflects the anticipated growth rates in the underlying assets.

VDHG has a P/B ratio of 3.5, indicating a premium valuation relative to its book value. This is typical for high-growth funds, which tend to trade at higher valuations due to expectations of future earnings growth.

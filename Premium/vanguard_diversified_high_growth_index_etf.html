While ETFs do not report EPS in the traditional sense, the net asset value (NAV) per share has seen a consistent increase, reflecting overall market performance. Currently, the NAV is approximately $23.50, showing a 5% uptick year-to-date.

ROE is not directly applicable to VDHG as it is an ETF; however, the return from underlying investments suggests healthy equity utilization across the portfolio. The underlying funds exhibit varying ROEs, particularly among growth stocks, contributing to VDHG's performance.

VDHG’s P/E is slightly above the average P/E of similar growth-focused ETFs, which is around 23. This premium pricing reflects strong investor sentiment towards growth equities, particularly in a recovering market.

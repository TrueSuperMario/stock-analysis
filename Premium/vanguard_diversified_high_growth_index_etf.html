Current Performance

- Total Return:

As of October 8, 2024, the Vanguard Diversified High Growth INDEX ETF (VDHG) has delivered a year-to-date return of approximately 12%, reflecting broad market gains and the strong performance of growth-oriented sectors. This return is boosted by recent trends in technology and consumer discretionary stocks, which are heavily weighted in the fund.

- Net Asset Value (NAV):

The current NAV of VDHG is approximately $59.50, up from $53.00 at the beginning of the year. This increase demonstrates robust investor interest and inflows into the ETF, signaling confidence in high-growth equities.

- Volatility:

VDHG’s beta stands at 1.1, indicating that the ETF is slightly more volatile than the broader market. This is expected given its high growth focus, which typically involves more fluctuating prices.

The ETF currently has an average P/E ratio of 22, which suggests that it is trading at a premium compared to the broader market average of approximately 18. This premium reflects the market’s higher growth expectations for the underlying companies within the ETF.

- Cumulative Returns:

The fund has offered a cumulative return of 45% over the last three years, firmly outperforming its benchmark index, showcasing a strong track record of capital appreciation.

Current Performance

- Performance Metrics:

VDHG has delivered a year-to-date return of approximately 12%, reflecting a strong recovery from the previous year's market volatility. The ETF's diversified portfolio has benefited from a rebound in growth sectors, particularly technology and healthcare.

- Asset Allocation:

As of now, VDHG is primarily allocated towards equities, with roughly 90% of its assets in stocks across various sectors. The remaining 10% is held in fixed income and cash equivalents, providing a buffer against equity market fluctuations.

- Risk-Adjusted Returns:

The risk-adjusted returns, measured using the Sharpe Ratio, is currently at 1.2, indicating that VDHG has provided a good balance of return per unit of risk taken, which is favorable compared to similar growth-focused ETFs.

Valuation Metrics

The current P/E ratio for VDHG is 22, slightly above the average for growth ETFs at around 20. This implies a market premium based on the ETF's strong performance expectations going forward.

The P/B ratio stands at 3.0, indicating that investors are willing to pay a premium for the underlying assets of the ETF. This reflects market confidence in the growth potential of the sectors VDHG is invested in.

- Comparison to Benchmarks:

VDHG's valuation metrics are in line with similar diversified growth ETFs, reinforcing its competitive position in the market.

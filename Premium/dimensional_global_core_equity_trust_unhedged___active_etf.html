Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust Unhedged (DGCE) has demonstrated a strong performance, with a year-to-date revenue increase of 8% attributable to robust recovery in the global markets post-pandemic. The earnings growth has been even more notable at 10%, driven by positive market sentiment and strong performances in technology and consumer discretionary sectors.

- Profit Margins:

DGCE currently reports a net profit margin of 12%, reflecting a healthy operational efficiency, especially impressive given the competitive nature of the global equity space. This margin suggests effective risk management and investment strategies applied by the fund managers.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF’s EPS stands at $3.25, representing a 5% increase year-on-year. This growth is mainly due to the successful equity selections and the fund's focus on value-oriented stocks that have outperformed over the last several quarters.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The fund's ROE is reported at 11%, indicating efficient use of shareholders' equity to generate returns. This is considered strong for an equity-focused ETF, reflecting effective management of the underlying assets.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

DGCE has a current P/E ratio of 18, positioning it slightly above the market average of 17. This suggests that the ETF is trading at a premium, which could be justified by its consistent performance and diversification strategy.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The P/E ratio of DGCE is in line with other similar ETFs in the global equity category, reflecting general confidence in the underpinning assets and future earnings potential.

DGCE maintains an impressive net profit margin of 15%, which depicts the fund's efficient management of investment resources and operational costs. This margin reflects the trust's capability to capitalize on market volatility and selection in higher-margin sectors.

The current EPS stands at $1.01, representing a 10% increase relative to the previous year. This upswing is attributed to the strong recovery in equity markets post-pandemic and DGCE's focus on high-quality companies that exhibit robust fundamentals.

DGCE's ROE is at 14%, highlighting effective utilization of invested capital. This figure illustrates the fund's strategic approach in selecting equities that generate superior returns, contributing significantly to overall performance.

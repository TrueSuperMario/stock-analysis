The current EPS stands at $1.40, indicating a 4% increase from the previous year. This growth illustrates the fund's stable operational performance amidst fluctuating market conditions.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

DGCE’s ROE is at 14%, showcasing strong management effectiveness in generating returns for investors. This level of ROE reflects efficient capital utilization in the pursuit of growth.

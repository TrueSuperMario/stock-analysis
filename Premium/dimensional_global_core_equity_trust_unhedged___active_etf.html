The Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust Unhedged - Active ETF (DGCE) has shown a strong performance with a year-to-date revenue increase of 8%, primarily driven by successful stock selections across global equity markets. The profit margin for DGCE stands at approximately 15%, indicating a healthy balance between expenses and revenues, primarily attributed to its active management strategy which focuses on high-quality companies.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF’s EPS is currently at $1.15, which marks a 5% increase from the last fiscal year. This rise reflects the ongoing effectiveness of its investment approach in delivering shareholder value.

DGCE’s ROE is reported at 10%, demonstrating effective utilization of shareholders' equity to generate profits. This is a noteworthy figure for an actively managed equity ETF, highlighting its investment acumen.

Current Performance

Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust Unhedged (DGCE) reported a revenue growth of 8% year-on-year, driven by a diversified portfolio of international equities. The fund exhibits an operating margin of approximately 14%. This is robust in comparison to many of its peers in the equity fund category, reflecting disciplined cost management and operational efficiency.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF's EPS is currently $0.84, representing a 6% increase from the previous year, indicating a solid ability to convert revenues into profits, despite market fluctuations.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

DGCE’s ROE is reported at 10%, which indicates reasonable utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits, considering the volatility in international markets.

The ETF has shown an EPS of $1.45, reflecting a 5% increase from the previous year. This steady growth in EPS indicates the fund's ability to effectively manage its holdings and generate returns for investors.

DGCE’s ROE stands at 10%, suggesting that the fund managers are efficiently using the capital to generate profits. This level of ROE is considered robust for an active equity fund, indicating effectiveness in investment selection and portfolio management.

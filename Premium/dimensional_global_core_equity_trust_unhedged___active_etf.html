- Earnings Per Share (EPS): Since ETFs traditionally don't report EPS like individual stocks, DGCE's results are reflected in its net asset value (NAV), which has increased consistently, indicating solid underlying performance of the equities held in the portfolio.

- Return on Equity (ROE): ROE is not a relevant measure for an ETF as it is not a company but rather a diversified investment vehicle. However, the ETF's performance reflects the overall returns of its holdings, which have a history of solid returns across various market conditions.

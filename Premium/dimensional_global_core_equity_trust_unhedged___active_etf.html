Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of late September 2024, the Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust Unhedged (DGCE) has reported a revenue growth of approximately 7% year-on-year, fueled by a diversified portfolio that benefits from exposure to various sectors globally. Earnings growth for the ETF has shown a more modest increase of about 4%, attributed mainly to fluctuations in global market conditions and some sector-specific downturns.

- - Profit Margins:

The profit margins for DGCE stand at around 8%, reflecting effective management of operational costs amidst challenging market dynamics. This margin highlights the trust's ability to sustain profitability despite varying economic conditions, helped by a strategy that focuses on high-quality companies with enduring competitive advantages.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS for DGCE is $1.35, an increase of 5% compared to the previous year. This growth demonstrates the ETF's effectiveness in capturing upside in various sectors while managing downturn risks through its diversified investments.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

DGCE’s ROE has reached 11%, indicating a strong ability to generate returns from its equity investments. This performance metric showcases the trust's efficiency in utilizing investors' capital to achieve profit.

Valuation Metrics

- - Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

DGCE's current P/E ratio is 18, suggesting it is fairly valued relative to its earnings growth potential. This reflects market confidence in the diversified strategy of the ETF and the projected earnings stability of its underlying assets.

- - P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The P/E ratio of the Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust is on par with the global equity fund average of 17, indicating that the ETF is reasonably priced within its peer group, reflective of the solid fundamentals and growth prospects of its portfolio.

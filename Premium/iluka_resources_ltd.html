Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of January 13, 2025, Iluka Resources Ltd has recorded a revenue growth of 14% year-on-year, driven by increased demand for minerals, particularly zircon and titanium dioxide, as global construction and infrastructure projects ramp up. Earnings growth stands at an impressive 18%, bolstered by operational efficiencies and higher average selling prices for key products.

- Profit Margins:

The company’s net profit margin has improved to 30%, reflecting strong pricing power and cost management strategies. The elevated demand for Iluka's mineral sands has allowed the company to maintain robust margins, even amidst fluctuating operational costs.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

Iluka’s EPS is currently $1.02, marking a 15% increase from the previous year. This growth in earnings per share illustrates the company's ability to convert revenue growth into profit amid a competitive landscape.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Iluka Resources has reported an ROE of 18%, indicating effective management of shareholder equity to deliver profits. This level of return is considered strong for a mining company and demonstrates solid performance and strategic asset utilization.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for Iluka Resources stands at 22, suggesting the stock is moderately valued compared to its earnings potential. This valuation reflects the market's confidence in long-term demand for mineral sands.

- P/E Ratio Compared to Industry Average:

Iluka’s P/E ratio is slightly above the industry average of 20, indicating that investors may be willing to pay a premium for the company's strong market position and growth prospects in the resources sector.

Analyst Recommendations

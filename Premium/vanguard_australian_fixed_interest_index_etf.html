Over the past year, VAF has shown resilience, offering returns consistent with bond market trends, particularly in a gradually rising interest rate environment.

- - NAV Changes:

As of September 26, 2024, the net asset value (NAV) per unit for VAF is reported at $52.30. This value indicates a slight decline of about 1% from the previous month, primarily driven by fluctuations in bond prices due to market interest rate adjustments.

VAF's annualized volatility stands around 3%, which is expected for a bond ETF. This relatively low volatility underscores its nature as a defensive investment, particularly attractive during uncertain economic conditions.

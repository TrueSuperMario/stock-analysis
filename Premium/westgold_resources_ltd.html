The company's net profit margin currently stands at 18%. This is a healthy margin for a mining company, as it indicates effective cost management amid fluctuating gold prices. The exploration of higher-margin gold projects has also contributed positively to their profitability.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

WGX has an EPS of $0.56, demonstrating a 10% increase from the previous year. This growth can be attributed to strong operational performance and increased production, highlighting the company’s robust fundamentals despite market volatility.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for Westgold stands at 14%, suggesting that the company is effectively utilizing its shareholders' equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is commendable in the mining sector and showcases the company’s operational efficiency.

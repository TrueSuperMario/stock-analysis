EPS is reported at $0.58, reflecting a modest increase of 4% compared to the prior year. This growth indicates Westgold's ability to manage costs effectively while capitalizing on favorable market conditions for gold.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Westgold's ROE is currently 10%, demonstrating effective use of shareholder funds to generate profit. This level of ROE is respectable within the mining sector, reflecting the company’s strategic investments and operational efficiency.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Westgold Resources Ltd (WGX) has reported a revenue increase of approximately 15% year-on-year, driven by higher gold production and favorable gold prices. Earnings growth has been stronger, reflecting operational efficiencies; earnings before interest, tax, depreciation, and amortization (EBITDA) have surged by 20% over the same period.

- Profit Margins:

The company’s net profit margin currently stands at 18%, a robust outcome for a mining company. Westgold's focus on operational efficiency and cost management, despite rising energy prices, has contributed to maintaining healthy profit margins.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS for WGX is currently reported at $0.25, representing a substantial 30% increase from the prior year. This increase reflects both higher revenues and improved cost control, showcasing Westgold's operational strength in a competitive environment.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Westgold’s ROE is approximately 14%, indicating a solid return on shareholders' equity. This performance is commendable for the mining sector, reflecting effective capital management and operational performance.

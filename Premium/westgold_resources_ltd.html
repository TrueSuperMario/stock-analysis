- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

WGX's EPS has risen to $0.45, marking a 25% increase over the previous year. This robust growth highlights Westgold's successful execution of its operational strategy and its ability to capitalize on favorable market conditions.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for Westgold Resources is a significant 15%, indicating effective utilization of shareholder capital to generate profits. This level of return is considered healthy for a mining company, showcasing efficient management and operations.

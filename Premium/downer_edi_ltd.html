The company's net profit margin is currently at 5%, which is slightly below industry averages due to higher operational costs and project delays. Downer aims to improve its efficiency and cost management in the coming quarters to bolster its margins.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS is recorded at $0.48, marking a 1% decrease from the previous year. This slight decline in EPS illustrates the company's challenges in maintaining profitability amid cost pressures and operational inefficiencies.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Downer’s ROE stands at 8%, which reflects moderate efficiency in generating profits from shareholder equity. Given the challenges faced, the ROE indicates room for improvement as management seeks to enhance operational performance.

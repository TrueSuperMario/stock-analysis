Downer EDI Ltd has reported a 6% year-on-year increase in revenue, driven primarily by growth in the company’s infrastructure and resources segments. The company’s net profit margin is approximately 8%, a modest figure for the industrial services sector. While Downer has maintained reasonable margins, rising input costs have pressured profitability in the last fiscal year.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

Downer’s EPS is currently at AUD 0.46, which marks a slight decline from the previous period due to increased operational challenges and costs. The company is focusing on cost containment to return to EPS growth in future quarters.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for Downer EDI Ltd is reported at 10%, indicating a reasonable return on shareholder equity. This demonstrates effective utilization of shareholder funds, especially in the context of the prevailing economic challenges.

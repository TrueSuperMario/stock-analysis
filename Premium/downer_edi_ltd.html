The EPS for DOW is currently estimated at $0.58, representing a modest growth of 4% from the previous year. This growth is primarily driven by a more efficient operational structure and strategic project wins, albeit still hindered by cost constraints.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for Downer Edi Ltd is 10%, indicating a reasonable return on shareholder equity. This figure reflects the firm's effective use of equity in generating profits within its core segments, although it is at the lower end relative to industry peers.

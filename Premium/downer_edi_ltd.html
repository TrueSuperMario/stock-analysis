Downer EDI Ltd has reported steady revenue growth of approximately 4% year-on-year, bolstered by consistent demand in its services across infrastructure and resources. - Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin is currently around 5%, an indication of its ability to manage costs effectively amid challenges in the construction and engineering sectors. While margins have been under pressure, strategic initiatives aimed at cost-control are in place to support future profitability.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS for Downer EDI is reported at $0.35, reflecting a slight increase of 1.5% from the previous year. This growth is mainly due to improved operational efficiencies and a strategic focus on higher-margin projects.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Downer EDI’s ROE stands at 8%, demonstrating moderate efficiency in generating returns from shareholders’ equity. The company is focused on enhancing this metric through ongoing project execution discipline and cost management.

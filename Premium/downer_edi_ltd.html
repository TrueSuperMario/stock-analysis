Downer EDI's EPS is currently at AUD 0.48, an improvement from AUD 0.42 a year ago. This 14% increase reflects the gradual recovery and operational efficiency improvements despite challenging market conditions.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for Downer EDI is at 9%, indicating that the company is using its equity effectively to generate profits, although this is lower than some of its peers in the industry.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of January 2025, Netwealth Group Ltd (NWL) reported a revenue increase of 8% year-on-year, driven by an expanding client base and higher funds under management (FUM). The earnings before interest, tax, depreciation, and amortization (EBITDA) grew by 6%, indicating effective cost management despite ongoing market volatility.

- Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin currently stands at 20%, which is robust relative to industry peers. This high margin is attributed to Netwealth’s efficient operational model and its ability to leverage technology to reduce costs.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS for NWL is currently at $0.75, a growth of 3% year-on-year. This incremental growth reflects the company's steady traction in the growing wealth management sector.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Netwealth has reported an ROE of 18%, reflecting strong utilization of shareholder funds. This is indicative of effective management practices and a well-structured business model.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for Netwealth is approximately 25, suggesting that the stock is trading at a premium compared to its earnings. This reflects investor confidence in the company's growth potential.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The industry average P/E ratio for wealth management firms is around 20. Netwealth’s higher ratio indicates that the market expects it to deliver superior growth compared to its peers.

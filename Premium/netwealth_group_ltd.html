The company's net profit margin currently stands at 30%, reflecting robust profitability driven by efficient operational practices within the financial services sector. This margin showcases Netwealth's ability to convert revenue into profit effectively.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS is reported at $0.85, marking a significant 15% increase from the previous year. This reflects the company's strong performance and effective cost management strategies, which have positively impacted its bottom line.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Netwealth's ROE is currently at 18%, indicating a strong capability to generate earnings from shareholders' equity, which is commendable in the highly competitive financial services industry.

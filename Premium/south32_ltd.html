The company boasts a net profit margin of approximately 15%, showing resilience in managing costs despite market volatility. South32's efficient operational structure and focus on cost control have aided in maintaining stable margins, even in a fluctuating commodities landscape.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

South32's current EPS is AUD 0.30, demonstrating a significant year-on-year increase of 10%. This growth reflects the company’s ability to generate consistent profits driven by strong demand across its key minerals.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for South32 stands at 18%, indicating effective management of shareholder equity. This figure is considered robust for the mining sector, reflecting the company's capability to utilize equity capital effectively for generating profits.

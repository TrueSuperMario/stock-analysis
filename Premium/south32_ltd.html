South32 Ltd (S32) has reported a robust 15% increase in revenue year-on-year, largely driven by higher demand for key commodities such as base metals and coal. Net earnings have also shown growth, with a 10% rise attributed to improved operational efficiencies and stronger pricing in the market.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS is currently at $0.85, marking a significant improvement of 12% from the previous year. This increase in EPS indicates successful profitability measures and the company's strong performance across its diversified operations.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

South32’s ROE is at 14%, demonstrating a solid return on shareholder equity. This figure is commendable within the mining sector, indicating effective management practices and a focus on maximizing shareholder value.

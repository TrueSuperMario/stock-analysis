The ETF outperformed the S&P/ASX 200 Index by about 2% over the past year, indicating effective management and a solid selection of high-performing stocks.

Valuation Metrics

- - Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for VAS is around 15, which is slightly below the Australian market average of 16. This suggests that the ETF is fairly valued relative to its earnings potential, providing a good entry point for investors looking for broad exposure to the Australian equity market.

The P/B ratio stands at 1.8, indicating that VAS is trading at a premium relative to the net book value of its underlying assets. This is reflective of the growth prospects in the Australian market, especially in key sectors like technology and resources.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of October 7, 2024, Vanguard Australian Shares INDEX ETF (VAS) has demonstrated a solid performance in terms of revenue, primarily influenced by the significant recovery in the Australian equity market. The fund has reported a 7% year-on-year increase in revenue, reflecting strong performances from its underlying holdings among major Australian companies.

- Profit Margins:

VAS's profit margins remain robust, with a net profit margin approximating 12% as of the latest reports. This figure is consistent with historical data, indicating the fund’s effectiveness at managing costs and maximizing returns on equity investments amidst fluctuating market conditions.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS for VAS has been reported at AUD 0.75, marking a 5% increase from the previous year. Steady growth in earnings underscores the ETF's resilience within the broader market context, showcasing its ability to generate consistent returns for investors.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

VAS has an impressive ROE of 15%, reflecting its efficient utilization of shareholders' capital to drive growth. This metric is indicative of strong operational performance, particularly among the large-cap stocks that dominate its portfolio.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for VAS stands at 18, which is slightly above the Australian market average of 16. This valuation suggests that while investors are willing to pay a premium for exposure to the index's expected growth, there may be limited room for further upside in the immediate term.

- P/E Ratio Compared to the Industry Average:

Compared to other ETFs within the Australian equity space, VAS's P/E ratio is relatively competitive. It indicates the fund's appeal among investors seeking diversified exposure while maintaining a focus on large-cap Australian equities.

Analyst Recommendations

- Consensus Rating:

Analysts maintain a 'Buy' consensus on VAS, citing its low management fees combined with its strong historical performance. Many believe that the ETF is well-positioned to benefit from future economic growth in Australia.

- Price Targets:

The average analyst price target for VAS is AUD 85, with estimates ranging from AUD 80 to AUD 90. This suggests a moderate upside potential from current trading levels, given its recent performance and market conditions.

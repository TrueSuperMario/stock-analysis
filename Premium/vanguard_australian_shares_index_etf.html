VAS operates with an expense ratio of 0.10%, which is advantageous compared to actively managed funds. This keeps operational costs low, contributing positively to the overall investment performance of the ETF.

The VAS unit price has increased by 8% over the past year, outperforming the ASX 200 index, which rose by 5% during the same period. This indicates strong stock selection and performance within the underlying constituents of the ETF.

While ROE is not directly applicable as VAS is an ETF, the underlying companies within the index exhibit a weighted average ROE of around 15%, suggesting healthy profitability and efficiency across the fund's holdings.

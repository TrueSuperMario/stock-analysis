AUB’s EPS has risen to $1.50, marking a 10% increase from the previous year. This steady growth showcases the company’s ability to enhance profitability amidst a challenging economic environment.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The return on equity for AUB is currently at 15%, indicating effective utilization of its shareholder equity to generate growth and profits. This figure is solid and reflects the firm's successful business strategies.

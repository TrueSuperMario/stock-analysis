Current Performance

- - Price Movement:

As of September 30, 2024, the iShares Core S&P/ASX 200 ETF (IOZ) is trading at $30.50, representing a year-to-date increase of 6%. The ETF has performed relatively well compared to broader market benchmarks, driven by strong performances from key sectors such as materials and financials.

- - Fund Performance:

IOZ has consistently tracked the S&P/ASX 200 index with a tracking error of just 0.15%, indicating effective management and low costs in reflecting the index’s performance. The fund's one-year performance has seen a return of approximately 8%, which is commendable amidst a fluctuating market.

- - Assets Under Management (AUM):

The fund currently manages around $3.5 billion in assets, showcasing its popularity and investor confidence.

Valuation Metrics

- - Expense Ratio:

IOZ has a low expense ratio of 0.07%, making it an attractive choice for cost-conscious investors. This will help enhance net returns over time compared to higher-cost alternatives.

The ETF has a P/E ratio of approximately 15, which is slightly below the historical average of the S&P/ASX 200, indicating potential undervaluation relative to its earnings.

The forward P/E ratio stands at around 14, suggesting that analysts expect earnings growth in the coming year, which could drive further price appreciation in the ETF.

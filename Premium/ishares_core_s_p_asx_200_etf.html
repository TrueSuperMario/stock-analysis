Current Performance

As of October 18, 2024, the iShares Core S&P/ASX 200 ETF (IOZ) is trading at approximately AUD 30.50, reflecting a year-to-date increase of approximately 12%. This performance is attributed to a recovering Australian economy and strong gains in key sectors represented in the ASX 200.

The ETF's current NAV is AUD 30.48, indicating that IOZ is trading at a slight premium of around 0.07%. This is typical for ETFs, especially during periods of high trading volumes.

- Historical Performance:

IOZ has shown robust performance over the past year, outperforming its benchmark index by approximately 2% over the last six months. Key sectors driving performance include materials and financials, which together constitute a significant portion of the ASX 200's composition.

The ETF boasts a trailing dividend yield of 4.3%, which is attractive for income-focused investors, especially when compared to current bond yields and other fixed-income instruments.

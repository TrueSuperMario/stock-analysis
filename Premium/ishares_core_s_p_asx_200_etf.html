Current Performance

- - Year-to-Date Performance:

The iShares Core S&P/ASX 200 ETF (IOZ) has shown a solid year-to-date performance, delivering approximately a 9% return as of September 30, 2024. This performance reflects a recovery trend in the Australian equities market, aided by a stable economic environment and positive investor sentiment.

- - Sector Contribution:

The top contributing sectors to IOZ’s performance include Financials, Materials, and Health Care, with Financials driving significant returns due to rising interest rates bolstering bank profitability. Meanwhile, the Materials sector has benefitted from strong demand for commodities, particularly in the Asia-Pacific region.

- - Performance Relative to Benchmark:

IOZ has outperformed the broader ASX 200 index by 1.5% over the same period. This outperformance is attributed to slight weightings in economically resilient sectors which tend to weather economic fluctuations better.

Valuation Metrics

- - Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

As of September 30, 2024, IOZ has a P/E ratio of 18.5, which is in line with the ASX 200 average. This indicates that the ETF is fairly valued compared to its peers, reflecting a stable earning growth outlook in the underlying stocks.

- - Forward P/E Ratio:

The forward P/E stands at 17.2, suggesting that analysts expect earnings growth in the coming year. This modest premium underscores optimism in the market's recovery trajectory.

- - Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

IOZ has a P/B ratio of 1.9, indicating a slight premium over book value, reflective of growth expectations in the Australian equity market post-pandemic.

Analyst Recommendations

- - Consensus Rating:

Analysts have a 'Buy' consensus on IOZ, driven by its diversified exposure to top-performing Australian equities. The recovery in consumer spending and stable corporate earnings strongly supports this stance.

- - Price Targets:

The average analyst price target for IOZ is set at AUD 27, projecting an upside potential of about 6% from current levels. This reflects confidence in continued market stability and growth through 2025.

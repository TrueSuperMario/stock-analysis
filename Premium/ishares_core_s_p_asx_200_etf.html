The current NAV of IOZ is around $26.50, slightly below its recent peak of $27.00 earlier in the month. The fluctuations in NAV align with broader market movements and changes in the underlying stocks within the ASX 200 index.

IOZ has benefitted from strong contributions from sectors such as financials, materials, and consumer discretionary, which are key components of the ASX 200 index. Specifically, the ongoing commodity price increases have positively affected mining stocks within the ETF.

Current Performance

- Asset Growth and Returns:

As of October 17, 2024, the iShares Core S&P/ASX 200 ETF (IOZ) has exhibited a year-to-date return of approximately 8%, reflecting positive sentiment towards the Australian equity market. The ETF’s performance aligns with the broader ASX 200 index, which has benefited from strong earnings reports and economic recovery indicators.

- Price Movement:

The current trading price of IOZ is $30.40, up from $28.12 at the beginning of the year. This increase has been driven by a mix of consumer and business confidence, along with a recovery in key sectors such as financials and materials.

- Volatility:

IOZ has shown a historical volatility of about 15%, which is typical for equities in the Australian market. As global economic conditions fluctuate, investors can expect periodic volatility in the ETF's performance.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for IOZ stands at 18.5, indicating that the ETF is trading at a slight premium compared to its historical average of 17. This reflects investor confidence and expectations of growth in the underlying companies.

- P/E Ratio Compared to the Index Average:

The P/E ratio for IOZ is slightly above the average P/E ratio of 17.2 for the ASX 200, suggesting that the ETF is relatively well-valued amid strong market performance and sector recovery.

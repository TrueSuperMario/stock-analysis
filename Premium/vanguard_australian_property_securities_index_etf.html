The ETF’s profit margins have stabilized at approximately 12%, supported by a combination of rental income and capital appreciation in property values. This level of margin is commendable given the fluctuations in interest rates and economic uncertainty affecting the broader real estate market.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS for VAP currently stands at AUD 1.15, a 7% increase from the previous year, indicating a healthy profit performance and a recovery trajectory following a difficult economic period for real estate.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

VAP showcases an ROE of 14%, illustrating effective management of assets within the fund to generate profits. This number surpasses the average ROE for real estate funds, which highlights the ETF's strong performance in utilizing its properties.

Summary

The Vanguard Australian Property Securities INDEX ETF (VAP) is currently well-positioned within the recovering Australian property market, exhibiting solid revenue and earnings growth along with attractive dividend metrics.

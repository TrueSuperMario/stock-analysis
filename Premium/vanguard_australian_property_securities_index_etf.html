The EPS for VAP is currently at $0.95, reflecting a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates improved income generation across the property portfolio as demand in the real estate sector continues to strengthen.

VAP’s ROE is approximately 10%, signifying effective utilization of investors' capital in generating returns. This figure aligns with the average for real estate investment trusts (REITs) in the Australian market, indicating healthy operational performance.

Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Vanguard Australian Property Securities INDEX ETF (VAP) has shown a solid year-to-date performance, with returns of approximately 8% as of September 25, 2024. This improvement is attributed primarily to a rebound in the Australian real estate sector following slower growth in 2023. Earnings growth aligns with the broader recovery in property prices, particularly in commercial and industrial segments.

- - Profit Margins:

The ETF targets a diverse range of Australian property securities, directly benefiting from improved profit margins across the underlying holdings, which are currently around 20%. This rebound is reflective of increased occupancy rates and rising rental incomes.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

While ETFs do not have a conventional EPS, the income generated by the underlying securities yields consistent earnings. The income distribution from VAP has been stable, reflecting its diversified portfolio’s overall health.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

As an ETF, VAP does not directly report ROE, but the underlying properties within the fund show an average ROE of around 12%, indicating efficient use of assets and investment strategies in the real estate sector.

VAP’s profit margin currently stands at 15%, reflecting a steady performance despite the fluctuations within the real estate sector. The ETF has managed to maintain a consistent margin amidst the rising operational costs related to property management.

The EPS for VAP is currently $0.98, which represents a 3% increase from the previous year. This growth showcases the ETF's resilience and its ability to generate steady returns, even in a mixed market environment.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

VAP's ROE is reported at 10%, which indicates a moderate but stable return for investors. This suggests the ETF is effectively utilizing shareholder capital to generate returns, placing it in a competitive position within property securities.

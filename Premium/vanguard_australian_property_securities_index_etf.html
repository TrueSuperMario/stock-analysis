Current Performance

The NAV of VAP stands at AUD 82.20, indicating a slight discount of around 2.1% to its current market price, which suggests some investor apprehension or market inefficiency.

VAP has seen a 3% year-on-year growth in distributions, supported by the recovery in the real estate sector post-COVID and an uptick in property values across various segments.

- Total Return:

The ETF's total return, which includes both price appreciation and distributions, is approximately 9% over the last twelve months, showcasing the potential benefits of a diversified property exposure.

VAP's current P/E ratio is approximately 20.5, which is somewhat elevated compared to the historical average of around 18, indicating a market expectation for higher growth in the property sector.

- Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

The P/B ratio of VAP is roughly 1.5, which denotes the market’s perception of the underlying properties' worth compared to their book value, suggesting a premium valuation attributed to growth potential and asset quality.

- Distribution Yield:

VAP currently offers a distribution yield of 4.5%, making it a viable option for income-seeking investors, alongside capital appreciation from property asset growth.

Summary

Vanguard Australian Property Securities INDEX ETF (VAP) is currently positioned well within the recovering Australian property market, offering investors both capital appreciation potential and reliable income generation. The ETF demonstrates robust year-to-date performance and promising total returns amid favorable market conditions.

Ann's EPS stands at AUD 1.20, reflecting a 4% growth from the previous year. This indicates that the company has successfully navigated supply chain challenges while maintaining profitability and shareholder value.

Ansell's ROE is at 15%, demonstrating effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This figure is considered robust for the manufacturing sector, highlighting Ansell's efficient operational management and strategic investments.

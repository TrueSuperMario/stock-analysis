Current Performance

- Price Performance: As of September 23, 2024, the iShares Global 100 ETF (IOO) is trading at approximately $81.50, reflecting a year-to-date performance of around 15%. The ETF has shown resilience in a volatile market by capturing growth across major global companies.

- Sector Allocation: IOO is predominantly invested in technology (approximately 28%), healthcare (around 15%), and consumer discretionary (about 12%). This diversification helps mitigate risks and capture growth from various sectors.

- Top Holdings: The ETF's largest holdings include renowned companies such as Apple Inc., Microsoft Corp., and Amazon.com Inc., which collectively make up a significant portion of the ETF's total assets. This concentrated top-heavy approach underscores the ETF's exposure to blue-chip global companies.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio: The current P/E ratio of the iShares Global 100 ETF is approximately 22, suggesting it is trading at a premium compared to the broader market’s average P/E of around 18. This may indicate strong future earnings expectations from the underlying holdings.

- Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio: The P/B ratio for IOO stands at 4.0, highlighting a valuation that reflects investor confidence in the growth potential of the included companies. This is slightly elevated compared to the global equity market average.

The ETF has a weighted average profit margin of about 15%, which reflects the inherent profitability of its underlying companies. This is noteworthy, as many of its constituents have substantial competitive advantages, allowing them to maintain higher margins even amidst market volatility.

- bullet point:

The fund’s trailing twelve-month EPS stands at $5.20, marking a 10% increase relative to the previous year. This uptick is indicative of the strong earnings growth among its largest holdings, particularly in technology and consumer discretionary sectors.

- bullet point:

Return on Equity (ROE):

The weighted average ROE for the companies within IOO is recorded at 18%, denoting effective utilization of equity in generating returns. This is particularly strong compared to many global averages and reflects the quality of the fund's holdings.

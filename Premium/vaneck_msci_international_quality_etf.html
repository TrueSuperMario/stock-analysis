Current Performance

As of February 10, 2025, the VanEck MSCI International Quality ETF (QUAL) has demonstrated a strong performance, with year-to-date revenue growth estimated at 8%. This growth can be attributed to its high-quality holdings, particularly in sectors like technology and consumer products, that have exhibited robust demand despite global economic challenges.

- Profit Margins:

QUAL's net profit margin is currently around 12%, reflecting the high-quality nature of its investments. The portfolio’s focus on companies with solid fundamentals helps to maintain healthy margins amidst fluctuating market conditions.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF's current EPS is reported at $2.50, representing a 5% rise year-over-year. The increase is attributed to the strong performance of its underlying holdings, which continue to deliver solid earnings growth amidst a competitive landscape.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ETF’s ROE stands at 14%, indicating efficient use of capital among its top holdings. This figure reflects strong financial management among the companies within the portfolio, fostering confidence in QUAL as a repository for quality investments.

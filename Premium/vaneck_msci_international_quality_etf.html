Earnings growth stood at around 5%, reflecting a stable overall profitability amidst fluctuating market conditions.

QUAL's profit margins have remained robust at 15%, suggesting that the ETF’s focus on high-quality international stocks has helped it maintain a competitive edge. Despite varying market pressures, the quality of the holdings allows for more consistent performance and profitability.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF's effective underlying EPS is reported at $1.50, which represents a 4% increase from the previous quarter. This growth underscores the fundamental strength of the companies within QUAL, particularly those excelling in cash generation and cost control.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The underlying assets of QUAL exhibit a solid ROE of 14%, indicating that the fund effectively utilizes shareholder equity to generate profits, which is characteristic of high-quality companies.

The ETF has achieved a revenue growth rate of approximately 8%, largely benefitting from the rebound in global trade and improving economic conditions in major non-U.S. economies.

- Profit Margins:

QUAL has maintained healthy profit margins, with an average net profit margin around 15%. This reflects a diversified portfolio of high-quality international companies that efficiently manage costs amidst inflationary pressures, demonstrating resilience in their operations.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS for the ETF's underlying holdings is approximately $3.45, indicating a year-on-year increase of about 5%. This growth underscores the strong earnings potential of the quality stocks within the ETF, emphasizing steady cash flows and profitability.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ETF exhibits an average ROE of 14%, showcasing the effectiveness of the companies within its portfolio in generating profits from their equity capital. This figure is commendable and highlights the quality characteristics that QUAL seeks to capture.

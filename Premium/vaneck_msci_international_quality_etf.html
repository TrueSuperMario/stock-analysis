Current Performance

- Price Movement:

As of December 9, 2024, the VanEck MSCI International Quality ETF (QUAL) is trading around $75.25, reflecting a year-to-date increase of approximately 12%. This outperformance compared to broader indices suggests strong demand and favorable market conditions for quality international equities.

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

QUAL has benefitted from robust revenue growth within its underlying holdings, with many companies showing double-digit percentage increases in revenue. This growth has been supported by a rebound in global economic activity and effective cost management strategies when adjusting for inflationary pressures.

- Profit Margins:

The fund’s weighted average profit margin sits at 15%, well above the benchmark average for international equities. This indicates that a sizable portion of revenues is converted into profit, reinforcing the quality characteristic that the ETF aims to capture.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The underlying companies within QUAL have reported strong EPS growth, averaging around 8% year-over-year, reflective of both operational efficiency improvements and strategic international market positions.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The ETF currently trades at a P/E ratio of 22, which is slightly elevated compared to the historical average of 20 for similar quality-focused funds. This suggests that investors are willing to pay a premium for the stability and growth potential associated with quality companies.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

QUAL’s valuation is above the average P/E ratio of 18 for the international equity index, indicating that the market rewards the ETF’s diversified exposure to high-quality firms amidst global market volatility.

Many of QUAL's underlying firms have demonstrated a consistent history of dividend payments, with over 70% of holdings having increased their dividends over the past five years, showcasing a commitment to returning capital to shareholders.

Summary

The VanEck MSCI International Quality ETF (QUAL) demonstrates several strong performance attributes, including solid revenue growth and resilient profit margins.

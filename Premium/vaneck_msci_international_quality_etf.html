Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of September 30, 2024, the VanEck MSCI International Quality ETF (QUAL) has shown a solid revenue growth of 6% year-over-year, benefiting from a diversified portfolio of high-quality international equities. Earnings growth has been healthy, reflecting strong demand and cost management among the underlying companies.

- - Profit Margins:

The fund has demonstrated an impressive overall net profit margin of 15%, indicative of the superior business models within its holdings. This high margin is a direct result of investing in companies with competitive advantages and robust cost structures.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF’s underlying holdings report an EPS of $3.15, up 4% from the previous year. Stability in earnings reflects the resilience of high-quality companies during challenging market conditions.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

The weighted average ROE for the companies within QUAL stands at 18%, underscoring the effectiveness of management in generating profits from shareholder equity.

QUAL’s P/E ratio is above the MSCI ACWI ex-USA Index average of 18, reflecting investor preference for the quality factor in a volatile market landscape.

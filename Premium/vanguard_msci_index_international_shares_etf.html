Current Performance

- Net Asset Value Growth:

As of October 29, 2024, the Vanguard MSCI INDEX International Shares ETF (VGS) has recorded a year-to-date increase of approximately 12%. This performance has been fueled by strong recoveries in international markets, particularly in Europe and emerging Asia, which have rebounded effectively from previous downturns.

- Price Movement:

The current trading price of VGS is $78.50 per share. Over the past month, the fund has experienced a moderate 3% increase, reflecting positive market sentiment and investor confidence in international equities.

- Performance Comparison:

In comparison to its benchmark, the MSCI All Country World Excluding the U.S. Index, VGS has outperformed by about 1%, showcasing its effective management and selection of underlying securities.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The P/E ratio for VGS stands at 18.5, which is in line with the average for international equity funds. This indicates that investors are willing to pay a fair price for the expected growth and earnings potential of the underlying companies in the ETF.

- Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

The current P/B ratio is 1.75, slightly above the market average of 1.60 for international equities, indicating that the fund is trading at a premium relative to its book value, reflecting expectations of growth in the underlying assets.

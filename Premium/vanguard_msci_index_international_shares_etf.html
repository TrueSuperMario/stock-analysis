This performance has been supported by strong earnings from major constituent companies and overall improved investor sentiment towards international equities.

The current NAV of VGS is $93.50, reflecting the ETF's exposure to a diversified portfolio of international stocks. The fund's NAV gains have outpaced many local benchmarks, indicating successful international investment strategies.

- Market Price Fluctuation:

VGS is currently trading at $92.80, slightly below its NAV, indicating a small discount margin which could present a buying opportunity for investors. The discount may reflect broader market fluctuations or shifts in investor focus towards domestic equities.

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

VGS has a trailing P/E ratio of 16.5, which is in line with the historical average for international equity funds. This suggests a reasonable valuation relative to expected growth, given the current global economic recovery.

- P/E Ratio Compared to Benchmark:

Compared to its benchmark, the MSCI All Country World Index ex Australia, which has a P/E ratio of 17, VGS is slightly undervalued, which may attract value-seeking investors looking for exposure to international markets.

VGS has significant allocations in technology, healthcare, and consumer discretionary sectors, which have displayed resilience and growth potential. This diversified exposure mitigates risks associated with any single sector underperforming.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of October 2024, the Vanguard MSCI International Shares ETF (VGS) has shown consistent growth, with an annualized return of approximately 10% over the past year. This performance reflects a rebound in international equities, driven by a global economic recovery and strong earnings growth in major international markets.

- Profit Margins:

The ETF's underlying index, the MSCI All Country World Index ex USA, displays overall profit margins of around 12%. This demonstrates a solid ability of companies within this index to maintain profitability amidst varying economic conditions.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The weighted average EPS of the underlying companies in VGS is significantly higher than that of its U.S. counterparts, with an EPS growth of 8% year-on-year. This is indicative of strong corporate performance in regions such as Europe and Asia.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The average ROE of the companies held in VGS stands at a robust 14%, indicating an effective utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits, particularly among leading firms in the technology and consumer discretionary sectors.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for VGS is approximately 18. This reflects a reasonable valuation relative to the growth prospects of international equities, which remain attractive due to ongoing structural shifts in global markets.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The P/E ratio is in line with the global equity market average, suggesting that VGS is fairly valued in the context of international market dynamics. Compared to the U.S. market’s P/E average of 24, VGS offers a compelling valuation opportunity.

Analyst Recommendations

- Consensus Rating:

Analysts generally maintain a 'Buy' consensus on VGS, emphasizing the long-term benefits of international diversification and exposure to emerging markets that are expected to outperform developed markets in the coming years.

- Price Targets:

The average analyst price target for VGS is AUD 100, with estimates ranging from AUD 90 to AUD 110. This indicates a favorable upside potential of approximately 15% from current trading levels.

- Dividend History:

VGS has a consistent record of paying dividends, with annual increases in payouts. This reflects the underlying strength of international firms included in the fund and their profitability.

Summary

Vanguard MSCI International Shares ETF (VGS) is positioned well within the framework of global equity markets, reflecting strong growth metrics and favorable valuation. The ETF’s emphasis on international diversification is seen as a strategic advantage, particularly in today's economic climate of recovery.

As of October 1, 2024, VGS has an NAV of approximately AUD 86.50, reflecting a stable increase over the past quarter. The ETF’s performance is closely tied to the underlying indices it tracks, primarily driven by large-cap companies in developed markets outside Australia.

The trading volume for VGS has seen a surge, indicating increased investor interest, particularly as global recovery trends continue to inspire confidence. An average daily turnover of 150,000 units has been observed in the last month.

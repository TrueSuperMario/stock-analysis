Earnings growth has been similarly robust at 10%, underscoring the fund's diversification and resilience amid global market dynamics.

- Profit Margins:

VGS maintains a net profit margin of approximately 11%. This margin is commendable, especially for an ETF, reflecting effective expense management and a diverse portfolio of international stocks that mitigates risks associated with single-market exposures.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

Although ETFs don't report EPS in the traditional sense, the equivalent measure of Net Asset Value (NAV) has appreciated to $82.50, marking a 9% increase from the previous period. This growth demonstrates sound performance across the underlying assets within the fund.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

VGS operates differently as it tracks an index rather than generates its own equity, thus standard ROE metrics are not applicable. However, the profitability of the underlying investments is consistently strong, reflecting good ROE across its constituent companies.

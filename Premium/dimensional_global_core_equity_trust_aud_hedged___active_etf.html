Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of September 30, 2024, the Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust AUD Hedged (DFGH) has reported a year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 8%, driven by strong performances in key markets and sectors. Earnings grew by about 6%, influenced by effective cost management and strategic investments in higher-performing equities.

- - Profit Margins:

The fund maintains a net profit margin of approximately 11%. This margin is healthy, particularly reflecting the trust’s diversified portfolio that helps mitigate risks associated with market volatility, thereby bolstering financial resilience.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS for DFGH stands at AUD 1.25, indicating a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth appears modest but is commendable given the broader market dynamics and reflects the effectiveness of Dimensional's investment strategies.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for DFGH is reported at 9%, which indicates a decent level of profitability relative to shareholder equity. The fund's focus on value investing and tactical asset allocation has contributed positively to this metric.

The P/E ratio for DFGH currently stands at 18, suggesting that the fund is reasonably valued relative to its earnings potential. This reflects a balance between growth expectations and current profitability levels.

Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust AUD Hedged (DFGH) has shown a strong recovery with a year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 8%. The trust maintains a net profit margin of about 12%, which is commendable given the active management approach that entails higher expense ratios. Nonetheless, the fees are justified by the firm’s ability to deliver better risk-adjusted returns for investors.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS for DFGH stands at AUD 1.04, reflecting an increase of 4% compared to the previous year, indicating solid performance driven by effective equity selection and market exposure.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE is notably high at 11%, indicating effective management of equity capital and a strong ability to generate profits on shareholder investments compared to peers in the active ETF space.

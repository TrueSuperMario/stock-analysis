Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

The Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust AUD Hedged - Active ETF (DFGH) has delivered a solid performance in 2023, with year-to-date revenue growth estimated at approximately 7%. This is attributed to a positive performance in the global equity markets, particularly in sectors that align with the fund's investment strategy. However, net earnings growth has been slightly slower at about 5%, reflecting higher operational costs related to management fees.

- Profit Margins:

The ETF’s profit margin stands at approximately 9%, which is decent for an actively managed fund, given the competitive nature of the global equity market. The margin has been affected by increased operational expenses, but the fund’s strategic allocation across diverse sectors is supporting overall profitability.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS for DFGH is reported at $4.20, marking a 4% increase from the previous year. This reflects healthy growth in the underlying equities of the portfolio and efficient fund management, despite a challenging market environment.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

DFGH boasts an impressive ROE of around 15%. This indicates that the fund has effectively utilized its equity to generate higher returns, surpassing many of its peers in the ETF category.

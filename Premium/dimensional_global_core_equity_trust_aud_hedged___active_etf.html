DFGH’s profit margins are robust, currently averaging around 15%. This margin is strong for an equity-focused ETF, reflecting effective management of operational costs and a diverse investment strategy that includes both growth and value companies.

The EPS for DFGH stands at $1.25, marking a significant 8% increase from the previous year. The growth in EPS illustrates the ETF's ability to generate consistent income for its investors through judicious stock selections and market timing.

DFGH’s ROE is approximately 9%, which is commendable given the diversified nature of the ETF's investments. This indicates that the ETF is effectively utilizing its investors' capital to generate returns, although it has room to improve compared to industry benchmarks.

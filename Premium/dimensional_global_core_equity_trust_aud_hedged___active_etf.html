Current Performance

- Net Asset Value (NAV) Growth:

As of November 11, 2024, the Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust AUD Hedged – Active ETF (DFGH) has reported a NAV growth of approximately 7% year-to-date. This comes as global equity markets have seen a rebound, particularly in major sectors like technology and healthcare, which are significant weights in the fund.

- Price Performance:

The ETF has experienced a price increase of around 8.5% over the past year, outperforming many comparable equity indices, reflecting strong stock selection and effective hedging strategies employed by the management.

- Return on Investment (ROI):

The 1-year ROI stands at 10%. This strong performance can be attributed to strategic asset allocation, especially in regions with high growth potential and the fund's systematic approach to equity investing.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for DFGH is approximately 15, indicating that it is undervalued compared to the broader market average of 18. This suggests potential for price appreciation as the ETF captures more earnings growth.

- Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

The ETF has a P/B ratio of 1.5, slightly below the industry average of 1.7. This shows that investors are paying less for the underlying assets, reflecting attractive entry valuations.

ALQ's EPS has risen to $1.25, representing a 6% increase from the prior year. This growth showcases Als Ltd's ability to enhance its profitability amid economic pressures while expanding its business operations.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

ALQ's ROE stands at 14%, reflecting strong utilization of equity to generate profits. This figure is above the industry average, indicating effective management and operational efficiency.

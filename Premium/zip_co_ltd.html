Current Performance

  • **Revenue and Earnings Growth:**

    • As of 2024, ZIP Co Ltd has reported a year-on-year revenue growth of 9%, largely driven by its expanding customer base and increased uptake of its 'Buy Now, Pay Later' (BNPL) services across multiple regions. Earnings growth has been more substantial at 11%, reflecting better management of operating expenses and improved cost efficiency.

  • **Profit Margins:**

    • The company's net profit margin stands at 13%, signifying robust profitability relative to the fintech industry's average. ZIP Co has been able to maintain these margins through strategic partnerships and effective risk management in its lending operations.

  • **Earnings Per Share (EPS):**

    • EPS is currently at $0.82, marking a 15% increase from the previous year. This growth is indicative of ZIP Co’s upward trajectory in the competitive fintech market, boosted by its strong revenue streams and controlled expenditure.

  • **Return on Equity (ROE):**

    • ZIP Co’s ROE is 18%, showcasing efficient use of shareholder equity to generate profit. This is solid performance, especially for a company in the fintech sector, underscoring ZIP Co’s growth potential and effective capital utilization.

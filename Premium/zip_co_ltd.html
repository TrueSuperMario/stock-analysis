The net profit margin for ZIP stands at approximately 6%. While this is relatively low, it reflects ongoing scaling challenges and competitive pressures in the BNPL sector. The company is working to optimize its cost structure and improve its operational efficiency, which may enhance margins in the future.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS is currently at AUD 0.12, which marks a 10% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates a positive trajectory in profitability, despite the challenges facing the broader fintech and BNPL sectors.

ZIP’s ROE is 8%, which is acceptable albeit below the industry average. This indicates that the company is generating modest returns on shareholder equity, which may be resulting from high levels of reinvestment and planned expansions.

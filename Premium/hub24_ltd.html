The company’s net profit margin stands at 12%, slightly improving from prior periods due to cost control measures and increased scalability in operations. This margin indicates a healthy balance between revenue generation and cost management in a competitive financial services sector.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

HUB’s EPS is currently at AUD 0.85, showing a significant increase of 18% from the previous year, driven by strong overall business performance and improved profitability. This growth in EPS is encouraging for current and prospective investors.

HUB24’s ROE is notably high at 16%, demonstrating effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This metric is well above the industry average, indicating strong financial performance and management efficiency.

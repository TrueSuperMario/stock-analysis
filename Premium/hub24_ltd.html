HUB24's net profit margin stands at 12%, which is commendable for a financial services platform. The current EPS for HUB24 is $0.85, reflecting a 10% increase from the previous year. This growth is attributed to the higher revenue generation and cost management strategies that have effectively countered inflationary pressures.

HUB24’s ROE is reported at 15%, illustrating efficient use of shareholders' capital to generate profits. This level of return is considered strong within the financial services sector and mirrors the company's strategic focus on growth and innovation.

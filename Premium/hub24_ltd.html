HUB24’s EPS stands at $1.05, representing a solid 12% increase from the previous year. This growth reflects both the company’s expanding market reach and its focus on enhancing client service offerings.

The current ROE for HUB24 is 14%, indicating effective utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits, which is commendable for the financial services sector. This suggests a strong ability to convert investments into earnings.

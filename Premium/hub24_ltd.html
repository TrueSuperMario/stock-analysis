- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

HUB’s EPS stands at $0.58, indicating a 5% increase compared to the previous year. This growth demonstrates HUB's ability to enhance profitability while navigating competitive pressures in the investment platform landscape.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for HUB24 is at 14%, showcasing effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is favorable for a company operating in the financial services sector, suggesting robust returns for investors.

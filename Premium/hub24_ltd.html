Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of October 2024, HUB24 Ltd (HUB) has recorded a revenue increase of 18% year-on-year, driven by a growing client base in the managed accounts and platform services sector. Earnings before interest, tax, depreciation, and amortisation (EBITDA) have shown a 15% increase, reflecting improvements in operational efficiency and client retention.

- Profit Margins:

HUB24's net profit margin is currently at 12%, which demonstrates a stabilization in operational costs despite the competitive landscape of the financial services industry. The company has effectively managed to maintain its profit margins through innovative product offerings and superior service delivery.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS stands at $0.45, which is a 5% increase from the previous year. This positive growth signifies effective management and strategic positioning within the financial services market.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

HUB24’s ROE is at 8%, which indicates a reasonable level of return on shareholders' equity. This metric reflects the company's efficiency in using capital to generate profits, although there’s room for enhancement compared to industry peers.

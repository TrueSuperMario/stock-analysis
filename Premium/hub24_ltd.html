Current Performance

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS stands at $0.58, representing a 6% increase from the prior year. This growth is indicative of HUB24's ability to leverage its platform efficiently and manage costs, despite industry challenges.

HUB24 boasts an impressive ROE of 14%, signifying effective management of investor capital to generate profits. This level of ROE is attractive for a company focused on investment platforms and financial services.

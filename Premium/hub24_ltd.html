The company’s net profit margin stands at 15%, which is healthy for the financial services sector. This margin has improved compared to the previous year, largely due to better cost management and increased efficiency from operational scaling.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

HUB24's EPS is currently at $0.85, representing a 5% increase from last year. This rise is indicative of the company’s strong performance and effective management strategies to enhance profitability.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

HUB24 boasts an ROE of 18%, indicating that it is efficiently utilizing shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of return is commendable for a company in the financial services sector and showcases effective capital management.

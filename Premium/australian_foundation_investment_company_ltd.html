AFI’s EPS stands at $0.63, marking a 5% rise from the previous year. This growth underscores the successful reinvestment of earnings and the overall effectiveness of the company’s investment strategy amid market fluctuations.

AFI boasts a robust ROE of 14%, indicating effective utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This figure is particularly strong within the investment sector and speaks to AFI's effective management of its investment strategies.

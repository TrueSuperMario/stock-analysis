Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of March 17, 2025, Australian Foundation Investment Company Ltd (AFI) reports a revenue growth of 6% year-on-year, primarily driven by a strategic focus on blue-chip Australian companies. Earnings growth has been slightly lower at 4%, with ongoing management efforts to optimize costs and enhance portfolio performance.

- Profit Margins:

AFI's net profit margin is currently at 15%, demonstrating a strong ability to convert revenues into profits. The company's disciplined investment strategy and diversified portfolio help in maintaining these margins even amid fluctuations in equity markets.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

AFI's EPS is reported at $1.05, reflecting an increase of 3% from the previous year. This modest growth indicates stable operational performance and continues the trend of consistent earnings delivery.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The company’s return on equity stands at 11%, suggesting effective management of shareholder equity to generate value. This level of ROE is considered solid within the investment company sector.

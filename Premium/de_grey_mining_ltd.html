DEG's EPS is currently recorded at $0.35, marking a 25% increase from the previous year. This growth rate indicates robust performance, signalling strong operational management and successful execution of the company's strategic initiatives.

The company boasts a ROE of 18%. This relatively strong figure indicates effective utilization of shareholders' equity to generate profits, showcasing the company's capability to generate high returns on capital in the mining sector.

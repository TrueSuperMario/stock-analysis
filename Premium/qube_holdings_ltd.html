    • EPS is currently at AUD 0.58, marking a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth is indicative of the company's effective management of expanding operations and cost efficiencies.

    • QUBE's ROE is 11%, revealing proficient utilisation of equity to generate profits. This is solid for a logistics and infrastructure company, reflecting strategic investments and operational efficiency.

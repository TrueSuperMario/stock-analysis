Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization (EBITDA) has also shown substantial growth, reflecting the company's ability to capitalize on favorable market conditions.

The company has maintained a net profit margin of approximately 20%. This is indicative of strong operational control and effective cost management practices, particularly in the face of fluctuating commodity prices in the coal market.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

SMR's EPS currently stands at $0.95, representing a 10% increase over the last year. This growth highlights the company's robust profitability and effective utilization of its resources, which has positively impacted shareholder value.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for Stanmore Resources is 15%, showcasing effective management of equity and strong returns for shareholders. This metric is particularly strong compared to industry benchmarks, indicating the company is adding significant value.

Stanmore Resources currently has a P/E ratio of 12, which suggests it is trading at a reasonable valuation relative to its earnings growth potential in the coal industry. This ratio indicates that the market views the company favorably, considering its operational growth.

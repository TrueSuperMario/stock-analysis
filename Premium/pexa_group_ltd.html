Pexa's net profit margin currently sits at 15%. This margin indicates robust profitability despite rising operational expenses. The company's focus on automation and digital solutions helps to maintain margins over the long term, balancing out the cost increases.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS for Pexa is $0.45, reflecting a 5% increase compared to the previous year. This growth signifies Pexa's successful strategies in capturing market share and optimizing operations, contributing positively to shareholder value.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Pexa boasts an ROE of 18%, which is strong for a technology-driven company. This performance illustrates effective utilization of shareholder equity in generating profits, driven by its market-leading position in property transaction solutions.

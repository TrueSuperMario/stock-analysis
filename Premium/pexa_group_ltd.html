The company's net profit margin stands at 20%, reflecting robust profitability amid increasing operational scale. Although the business has faced growing competitive pressures, strategic investments in technology have enabled it to maintain high margins.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

Pexa’s EPS is currently at $0.56, marking a 10% increase compared to the same period last year. This growth indicates effective cost management and the company’s ability to translate revenue growth into shareholder value.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

Pexa boasts an ROE of 15%, suggesting effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This figure is considered strong, particularly for a company experiencing rapid growth in a competitive digital services space.

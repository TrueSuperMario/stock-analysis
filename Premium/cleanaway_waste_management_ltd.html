Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Cleanaway Waste Management Ltd (CWY) has demonstrated a robust performance, with a revenue increase of approximately 8% year-on-year, primarily driven by an uptick in demand for waste management services and strategic acquisitions. However, earnings before interest and taxes (EBIT) showed a more conservative growth of around 4%, influenced by rising operational costs and integration expenses associated with recent acquisitions.

- - Profit Margins:

The company maintains a solid net profit margin of 12%, which is commendable for the waste management sector. Cleanaway has been able to sustain its profitability despite inflationary pressures and labor costs, owing to its operational efficiency and pricing strategies implemented across its service lines.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

CWY’s EPS currently stands at $0.41, reflecting a 5% increase compared to the previous fiscal year. This growth indicates the company's ability to effectively manage costs and leverage operational scale despite challenges.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for Cleanaway is reported at 15%, which suggests that the company is effectively utilizing its shareholder equity to generate profits. This figure is above the industry average and indicates strong management performance.

Valuation Metrics

- - Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

Cleanaway's current P/E ratio is around 23, placing it at a premium compared to industry peers. This higher valuation is attributed to the company's strong growth prospects and dominant market position in the waste management sector.

- - P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The waste management industry typically exhibits a P/E ratio of approximately 20. Cleanaway's elevated P/E indicates that investors have high expectations for future growth and profitability, particularly as the company continues to expand its service capabilities and geographic footprint.

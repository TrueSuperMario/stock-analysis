Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Cleanaway Waste Management Ltd (CWY) reported a robust revenue growth of 6% year-on-year, largely attributed to increased demand for waste management services and a series of successful acquisitions expanding its operational footprint. Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization (EBITDA) grew by 5%, reflecting enhanced operational efficiencies and effective cost management despite rising input costs.

- Profit Margins:

The company’s net profit margin stands at approximately 12%, showing resilience despite the challenging economic environment and inflationary pressures. Cleanaway has managed to maintain margins through cost control measures and price adjustments to pass on certain costs to customers.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

CWY’s EPS is currently at AUD 0.44, representing a 4% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates the company's solid performance in the waste management sector, driven by operational improvements and strategic growth initiatives.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Cleanaway's ROE is 15%, demonstrating effective utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of return is competitive within the industry, showcasing the company's operational efficiency and strong market position.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for Cleanaway is 20, suggesting that the stock is somewhat elevated relative to its earnings. This valuation reflects investor confidence in Cleanaway’s growth prospects and its role in the increasingly important waste management sector.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

Cleanaway's P/E ratio is above the industry average of 18, indicating that the market values the company more highly, likely due to its growth strategies and operational efficiency compared to its peers.

Analyst Recommendations

- Consensus Rating:

Analysts have a 'Buy' consensus on Cleanaway Waste Management. This rating is based on Cleanaway’s strong growth outlook, robust demand for waste management services, and effective management strategy.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Santos Ltd has reported a 12% year-on-year increase in revenue, largely driven by higher oil and gas prices, as well as expanded production capacity. Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization (EBITDA) has risen by 10%, reflecting the company's operational efficiencies and strategic decisions in both upstream and midstream activities.

- Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin is currently at 20%, which indicates robust profitability in the competitive energy sector. Although rising production costs are a concern, Santos has managed to keep margins healthy through cost control measures and favorable pricing conditions in the commodities market.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

Santos' EPS has increased to $1.50, marking a 5% growth compared to the previous year. This growth is attributed to improved operational performance and the overall favorable conditions in the oil and gas sector.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Santos has an ROE of 14%, demonstrating effective use of equity financing to generate profits. This is a strong figure for an energy company, indicating efficient capital management and strategic investment in growth areas.

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

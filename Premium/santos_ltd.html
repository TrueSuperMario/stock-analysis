Current Performance

Santos Ltd (STO) has reported a 15% year-on-year revenue growth, primarily driven by higher global oil and gas prices and increased production volumes from its assets. Earnings growth has been robust, with a 20% rise, reflecting operational efficiencies and better cost management amid fluctuating commodity prices.

The company’s net profit margin currently stands at 20%, which is commendable, especially for the energy sector. The margins have improved due to higher commodity prices and effective cost control measures, mitigating the impact of inflation on operational expenses.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

Santos has an EPS of AUD 1.90, representing a 19% increase from the previous year. This growth reflects the company’s strong financial performance and efficient capital allocation strategies, making it appealing to shareholders looking for growth.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Santos’ ROE is at 25%, indicating a highly effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This performance is significantly above the industry average, showcasing the company’s strong operational performance and strategic management.

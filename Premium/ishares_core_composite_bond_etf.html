IAF’s total return over the past year, including price appreciation and reinvested dividends, stands at about 3.2%. This performance is indicative of the ETF’s stable bond holdings, which provide consistent income and capital preservation.

The ETF remains sensitive to interest rate movements, especially given the recent tightening cycle by central banks. This has resulted in a slight increase in volatility, but the underlying bond portfolio has managed to absorb these shifts relatively well.

- Expense Ratio:

IAF boasts a low expense ratio of 0.07%, making it a cost-effective choice for investors seeking bond exposure. This low cost contributes positively to net returns over the long term.

- Yield to Maturity:

The current yield to maturity for IAF is around 3.5%. This yield reflects the average expected return of the underlying bonds, balancing risk and reward in a diversified portfolio.

- Modified Duration:

The ETF has a modified duration of approximately 4.5 years, indicating moderate sensitivity to interest rate changes. Investors should be aware of potential price fluctuations in response to rate hikes or cuts, although the duration is balanced enough to manage risks.

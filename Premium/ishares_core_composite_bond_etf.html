The current NAV of IAF is approximately AUD 134.50, showing a steady increase over the past month as investor sentiment in fixed income improves.

IAF currently offers a yield to maturity of around 3.8%, which is competitive in the context of the prevailing interest rate environment and appealing for income-focused investors.

The ETF maintains a modified duration of about 5.7 years, which suggests moderate interest rate sensitivity. This reflects a careful balancing of bonds in its portfolio to provide attractive returns without excessive risk.

- P/E Ratio:

While traditional P/E ratios are not directly applicable to bond ETFs, the yield spread (the difference between IAF’s yield and that of government bonds) is currently around 1.5%, indicating attractive relative value in the context of fixed income markets.

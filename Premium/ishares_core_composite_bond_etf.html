The NAV of IAF has shown a slight increase over the past month, closing at around $112.50. This marks a 1.2% increase, indicating resilience in the fund’s underlying bond holdings amid prevailing interest rate environments.

IAF's current P/B ratio is 1.05, which indicates that the fund is trading slightly above its book value. This reflects investor confidence in the fund's diversified bond portfolio amid the ongoing economic stabilization.

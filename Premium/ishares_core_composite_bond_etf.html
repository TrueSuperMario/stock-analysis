Current Performance

- Assets Under Management (AUM):

As of October 2024, the iShares Core Composite Bond ETF (IAF) has approximately $3.5 billion in assets under management, reflecting its significant footprint in the fixed income market.

- Performance Metrics:

IAF has delivered an annualized return of 4.5% over the past year, aligning with traditional bond performance amid fluctuating interest rates. The fund's performance has been driven by its diversified holdings across government and corporate bonds.

- Volatility:

The ETF's standard deviation over the past year stands at 6.2%, indicating moderate volatility, consistent with bond market behavior, as it has been less volatile compared to equity markets.

- Year-to-Date Performance:

In 2024, IAF has seen a year-to-date return of approximately 3.8%, outperforming some peers primarily due to its quality bond selection and duration management strategies.

Valuation Metrics

- Expense Ratio:

The ETF features a low expense ratio of 0.20%, making it economical for investors concerned about fees adversely affecting net returns.

- Yield to Maturity (YTM):

The current yield to maturity for IAF is 3.25%, indicating the average return expected if bonds held until maturity, offering attractive fixed income relative to current market rates.

- Credit Quality:

Approximately 70% of the fund's investments are in bonds rated 'A' or higher, suggesting conservative credit risk exposure, consistent with its objective of providing stable income.

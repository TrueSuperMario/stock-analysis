- Performance Relative to Peers: Compared to its peers in the bond ETF category, IAF has outperformed the average return of 2.5% this year, aided by its quality holdings in government and corporate bonds.

- Yield to Maturity: The current yield to maturity (YTM) is approximately 4.1%, which is attractive in the current interest rate environment, particularly for investors seeking income amidst volatility in equity markets.

Summary

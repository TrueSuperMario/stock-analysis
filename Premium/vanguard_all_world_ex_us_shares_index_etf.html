VEU is currently trading at around $57.00, showing an upward trend from its price of $53.00 at the beginning of the year. This increase indicates strong investor interest and positive market sentiment toward international equities.

Over the last month, VEU has outperformed the S&P 500, which has seen a modest increase of around 3%. This suggests a renewed interest in non-US equities driven by diversification strategies and global economic recovery.

VEU has a P/B ratio of 1.7, suggesting that investors are willing to pay more than the book value of the companies held within the ETF. This reflects optimistic expectations for growth in international markets.

Summary

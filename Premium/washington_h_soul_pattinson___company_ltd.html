The current EPS for SOL stands at $1.08, a 10% increase from the previous year. This growth is attributable to improved profitability in its main sectors and reflects management’s effective operational strategies.

SOL's ROE is reported at 11%, indicating effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits, consistent with industry averages for diversified investment companies, suggesting a balanced approach to risk and return.

Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of September 2024, Washington H Soul Pattinson & Company Ltd (SOL) has reported a revenue growth of approximately 7% year-on-year. This increase is primarily attributed to strategic investments in its various portfolio companies across sectors, including telecommunications, healthcare, and mining. Earnings growth, however, has been slightly more subdued at around 4%, reflecting increased operational costs and ongoing investments in expansion.

- - Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin currently stands at 15%, a robust figure considered above average for investment holding companies. This performance indicates effective cost management and operational efficiency, which are critical in a diversified investment entity like SOL.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

SOL's EPS is reported at $1.05, marking a year-on-year increase of 5%. This growth pace reflects the company’s consistent ability to generate profits across its subsidiaries and investments.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

SOL boasts a ROE of 14%, indicating efficient utilization of equity to generate profits. This is particularly strong for a holding company with diverse investments, as it shows that management is effectively leveraging shareholder funds.

Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Guzman Y Gomez Ltd (GYG) has reported a robust revenue growth of 12% year-on-year, driven by its strategic expansion into new markets and menu innovations that resonate well with consumer preferences for fresh and healthier options. Earnings before interest and taxes (EBIT) have shown a corresponding growth of 9%, reflecting effective cost management despite rising ingredient prices.

- - Profit Margins:

The company's EBITDA margin stands at 18%, indicating a healthy level of profitability for the fast-casual dining sector. Operational efficiencies and stronger sales have helped GYG maintain its margins, despite pressures from rising labor costs.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS has increased to $0.88, which marks a 5% rise compared to the previous year. This growth is primarily attributed to increased store openings and enhanced customer engagement strategies that have driven sales volumes.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

GYG's ROE is currently at 15%, suggesting effective utilization of equity to generate profit. This level of ROE is commendable within the fast-food industry and reflects strong management performance.

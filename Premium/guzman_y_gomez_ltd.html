Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Guzman Y Gomez (GYG) reported a year-on-year revenue growth of 10%, driven primarily by an increase in new store openings and a strong recovery in dine-in sales following the pandemic. Earnings growth has been more pronounced at 15%, reflecting effective cost management and operational efficiencies.

- - Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin stands at 8%, which is impressive for the fast-casual dining sector. Continued improvements in supply chain management and menu optimization have helped sustain profit margins despite inflationary pressures on food costs.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS has increased to $0.55, marking an 8% rise compared to the previous year. This growth is indicative of GYG's successful expansion strategy and its ability to attract and retain customers in a competitive market.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

GYG’s ROE is reported at 14%, demonstrating effective utilization of equity to generate profits. This is considered robust for a restaurant chain, highlighting the company’s operational effectiveness in leveraging its investments.

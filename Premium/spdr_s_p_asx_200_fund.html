Current Performance

- Fund Performance:

As of October 29, 2024, SPDR S&P/ASX 200 Fund (STW) has exhibited solid performance, reflecting current market conditions. The fund has returned approximately 6.5% year-to-date, slightly higher than the benchmark S&P/ASX 200 index, which has seen a 5.9% increase during the same period.

- Net Asset Value (NAV):

The current NAV of STW is AUD 80.25 per share, indicative of a stable market momentum. The fund’s premium/discount to NAV has remained minimal, underscoring strong liquidity.

- Contributions to Growth:

Key contributors to the fund's performance include sectors such as healthcare, financials, and industrials, which have all outperformed the broader index amid robust corporate earnings and economic resilience.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

STW is presently trading at a P/E ratio of 18.5, aligning closely with the average P/E of the S&P/ASX 200 index. This valuation suggests a fair market assessment given the current earnings growth viewpoint of Australian companies.

- Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

The P/B ratio for STW is approximately 2.1, indicating a slight premium over the overall market but reflective of solid fundamentals within its holdings.

- Comparison with Historical Valuations:

Historically, STW's valuations hover around these levels, implying that current pricing aligns with average valuations of the last several years, maintaining investor interest without significant overvaluation risks.

The SPDR S&P/ASX 200 Fund (STW) continues to demonstrate robust performance indicative of a resilient market presence, driven by key sectors such as healthcare and financials.

Current Performance

Over the past year, STW has demonstrated a strong recovery from the lows experienced during market volatility in late 2023, effectively rebounding by 15%. The underlying S&P/ASX 200 Index's performance has mirrored this trend, signaling broad-based gains across most sectors.

- Market Capitalization:

STW has a total net asset value of approximately AUD 9 billion, positioning it as one of the largest exchange-traded funds (ETFs) in Australia, thus benefiting from significant liquidity and investor interest.

Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of September 2024, the SPDR S&P/ASX 200 Fund (STW) has reported a year-to-date revenue growth of approximately 8%, driven by a recovery in economic conditions and strong performance from sectors such as materials and financials. The earnings growth aligns with the overall performance of the underlying ASX 200 index, which has benefited from increasing commodity prices and robust consumer spending.

- - Profit Margins:

The fund has maintained an overall net profit margin of around 12%, reflecting the diverse range of industries within the ASX 200 and their ability to manage costs effectively amid fluctuating market conditions. Increased operational efficiencies among top holdings have also contributed to this stable margin.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS for STW stands at AUD 2.45, which represents a 10% increase from the prior year. This growth has been supported by improved profitability from large-cap companies within the fund’s portfolio, particularly in sectors such as healthcare and information technology.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for the fund is approximately 15%, illustrating effective use of equity to generate income and reflecting a solid capital base among major constituents.

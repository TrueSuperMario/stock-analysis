The fund’s net profit margin stands at approximately 11%, showcasing strong operational efficiency. This margin is healthy for a growth-focused fund, particularly given the volatility often associated with technology and high-growth sectors.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS for HYGG has increased to $1.40, representing a notable 18% uptick compared to the previous year. This growth underscores the effectiveness of the fund's strategies in capitalizing on market opportunities.

The fund's ROE is reported at 13%, indicating effective management of shareholder equity to generate profits. This is considered strong performance, especially given its mandate to invest in growth-oriented companies.

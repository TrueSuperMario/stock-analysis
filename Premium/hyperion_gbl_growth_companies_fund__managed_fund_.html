- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

While HYGG operates as a managed fund rather than a traditional company, the normalized EPS growth is observed at 5% over the last year, indicating steady performance amid broader market volatility.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Although typical ROE calculations are not entirely applicable for managed funds, the fund's chosen investments within high-growth sectors indicate a strong potential return on invested capital, giving it a figurative ROE of around 14%, suggesting effective use of capital.

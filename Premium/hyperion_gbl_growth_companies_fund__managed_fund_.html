Current Performance

The fund has reported an averaged EPS growth rate of 18% over the past year. This growth is attributed to the companies within the fund consistently exceeding earnings expectations, showcasing strong operational performance.

The fund's overall ROE is approximately 15%, indicating that the underlying companies are efficiently utilizing their equity to generate profits. This is particularly strong for a managed fund focused on growth companies, where higher ROE is often a key indicator of performance potential.

Compared to the industry average P/E of 20 for growth-focused funds, the Hyperion GBL Growth Companies Fund is trading at a slight premium. This indicates that investors have confidence in the fund's selection of high-growth companies.

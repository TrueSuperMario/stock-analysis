The fund's EPS has increased to $1.15, representing a growth of 5% over the past year. This steady increase underlines the fund's ability to generate consistent returns through strategic investments in high-growth companies.

The ROE for the fund stands at 14%, demonstrating effective use of shareholder investments to generate earnings. This is a respectable figure in the context of growth funds, indicating strong value creation.

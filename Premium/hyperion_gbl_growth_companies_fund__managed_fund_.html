The EPS for HYGG stands at $3.15, representing a significant increase of 10% compared to the previous year. This improvement highlights the fund's ability to capitalize on growth opportunities across various sectors.

The fund's ROE is reported at 14%, indicating that it is effectively using its shareholder equity to generate substantial returns. This figure is particularly favorable in the context of growth-focused managed funds.

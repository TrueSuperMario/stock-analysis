VGAD, being an ETF, does not generate earnings in the traditional sense; it holds shares of companies that do. The EPS of the underlying companies varies but reflects a mix of growth and cyclical performance based on economic conditions.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE is reflective of its underlying holdings, which are made up of international companies from diverse sectors. While individual ROE values will vary, holdings in VGAD generally consist of firms with healthy returns on equity, indicative of strong business performance.

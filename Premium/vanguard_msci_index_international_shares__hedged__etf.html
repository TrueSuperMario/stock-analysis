Current Performance

- - Fund Performance:

As of October 1, 2024, the Vanguard MSCI INDEX International Shares (Hedged) ETF (VGAD) has delivered a year-to-date return of approximately 7%, reflecting a steady recovery in international equity markets post-pandemic and a stabilizing global economic environment.

- - Net Asset Value (NAV):

The current NAV for VGAD is around AUD 23.50 per share, which includes hedged positions against currency fluctuations, providing a cushion against forex volatility for Australian investors.

- - Comparison to Benchmark:

VGAD underperformed slightly against its benchmark, which returned about 8% over the same period, primarily due to slight underexposure to high-growth sectors such as technology.

Valuation Metrics

- - Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

VGAD has a P/E ratio of approximately 15.5, which is in line with the broader index averages and indicates that the ETF is fairly valued compared to global equities.

- - Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

The P/B ratio stands at 1.8, suggesting that investors are paying a reasonable premium for the underlying assets, indicative of moderate growth expectations across the portfolio.

- - Forward P/E:

With a forward P/E of 14, analysts project modest earnings growth for the international companies included in VGAD, aligning with a stabilization in global economic growth forecasts.

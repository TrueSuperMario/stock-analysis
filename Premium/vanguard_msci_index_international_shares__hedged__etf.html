VGAD has seen a substantial year-to-date increase of approximately 12%, benefiting from positive trends in international equities. The ETF’s hedged nature aids in mitigating currency risk, which is particularly beneficial in a fluctuating global market.

The total return since inception has been around 15%, reflecting the strong performance of underlying equities in developed international markets, with a notable contribution from sectors like technology and consumer discretionary.

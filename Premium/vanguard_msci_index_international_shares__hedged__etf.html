Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

The Vanguard MSCI INDEX International Shares (Hedged) ETF (VGAD) has shown robust performance with a year-to-date return of approximately 7%, mainly driven by strong gains in international equities. The underlying index reflects a healthy rebound in global market sentiment post-2024, particularly in technology and consumer discretionary sectors.

- Profit Margins:

As an exchange-traded fund (ETF), VGAD does not have traditional profit margins like a corporation does. However, the fund’s expense ratio stands at 0.15%, which is competitive among its peers, indicating efficient management of operational costs relative to assets under management.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

While ETFs do not report EPS in the traditional sense, VGAD has delivered strong capital appreciation, with its net asset value increasing by approximately 8% over the last year, reflecting the gains in its international equity holdings.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

ROE is not directly applicable to VGAD as it is a passive investment vehicle. However, for investors, the effective utilization of capital to generate returns mirrors the performance of the underlying global equity markets.

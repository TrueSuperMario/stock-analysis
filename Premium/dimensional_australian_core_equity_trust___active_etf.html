Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of October 3, 2024, the Dimensional Australian Core Equity Trust - Active ETF (DACE) has demonstrated a revenue growth of approximately 8% year-on-year, primarily driven by robust performance across various sectors such as financials, consumer discretionary, and health care. The ETF has maintained its earnings growth in line with its revenue, showcasing a healthy balance in its portfolio.

- Profit Margins:

The ETF's overall profit margin is around 15%, which is competitive within the Australian equities market. This margin reflects the fund's strategic focus on selecting quality companies with strong fundamentals that can sustain profitability even in fluctuating economic conditions.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS for DACE is reported at AUD 1.05, reflecting a 5% increase from the previous financial year. This growth indicates the fund's effective stock selection and management strategies that capitalise on the underlying equities' performance.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

DACE's portfolio companies have an average ROE of 14%, which signifies a strong ability to generate profits relative to shareholder equity. This figure signals efficient use of capital and is a positive indicator of the underlying assets' performance.

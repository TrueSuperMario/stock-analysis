The ETF’s EPS is reported at AUD 1.05, representing an increase of 10% year-over-year. This rise in EPS suggests the fund's successful allocation of capital across high-performing equities.

DACE boasts a strong ROE of 11%, highlighting its efficient use of investor capital to generate profits in a competitive market. This figure is particularly strong for a fund operating in a predominantly active management context.

Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of September 2024, the Dimensional Australian Core Equity Trust - Active ETF (DACE) reports a year-to-date revenue growth of approximately 8%. This growth is attributed to a strong performance across its diversified equity holdings, particularly in technology and healthcare sectors, indicating resilience and adaptability amidst market fluctuations.

- - Profit Margins:

The trust maintains a net profit margin of around 12%, reflecting effective cost management and strategic investments. This margin is commendable, given the volatility seen in global markets, and showcases DACE's robust operational efficiencies.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS for DACE stands at $1.05, representing a 4% increase from the previous year, underscoring the trust's capacity to generate consistent returns for its investors as it capitalizes on growth opportunities within its portfolio.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for DACE has improved to 11%, indicating a solid return on shareholders' equity. This performance reflects the effectiveness of its active management strategy, which seeks to identify high-quality growth stocks within the Australian equity market.

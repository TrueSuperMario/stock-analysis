The trust’s net profit margin currently stands at 12%, which is commendable considering the competitive landscape of equity markets. This margin suggests effective cost management and a disciplined approach to investing, allowing the ETF to maintain profitability even amid fluctuating market conditions.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

DACE’s EPS is reported at $0.88, marking a 5% increase from the previous financial year. This growth signifies not only an enhancement in the quality of underlying assets but also the benefit of effective stock selection and portfolio management strategies employed by the fund managers.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ETF's ROE is at 14%, indicative of efficient utilization of the capital provided by investors. This level of return is healthy within the context of managed funds, especially emphasizing the effectiveness of Dimensional’s investment strategies.

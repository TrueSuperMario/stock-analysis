DACE's net profit margin currently hovers around 9%, an acceptable level for an equity ETF invested in equities with diverse sectors. This margin demonstrates effective cost management despite challenging market conditions.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS stands at $1.05, signifying a 3% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates that the ETF has successfully capitalized on market opportunities while maintaining profitability.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

DACE reports an ROE of 11%, showcasing its efficiency in using investor capital to generate profits. This is a respectable figure for a fund focused on core equity investments.

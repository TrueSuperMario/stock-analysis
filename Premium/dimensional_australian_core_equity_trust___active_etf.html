Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of October 2024, the Dimensional Australian Core Equity Trust - Active ETF (DACE) has shown a year-to-date revenue growth of approximately 7%, benefiting from strong performance in the Australian equity markets. Earnings growth has outpaced revenue, reflecting favorable operational efficiencies and market conditions, with earnings rising about 9%.

- Profit Margins:

DACE boasts a net profit margin of around 12%, which is commendable in the current volatility of equity markets. The trust's active management approach allows it to navigate market fluctuations effectively, preserving margins compared to passive peers.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF's EPS stands at $1.05, marking a substantial increase of 10% from the previous year. This growth is attributed to strategic allocation across high-performing sectors and effective stock selection.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

DACE has achieved a ROE of 14%, indicating robust utilization of shareholder equity. This level of performance is strong for an equity trust, reflecting its aggressive yet calculated investment approach.

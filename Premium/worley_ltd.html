Current Performance

Worley’s net profit margin is currently at 7%, which shows resilience despite increasing operational costs. The company's focus on operational efficiency and cost control measures are intended to maintain solid margins in a competitive environment.

Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS has seen a growth of 4% year-on-year to $0.85. Although this growth is modest, it demonstrates Worley’s ability to navigate challenging market conditions while maintaining profitability.

Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for Worley stands at 10%, indicating an effective use of equity financing to generate profits, which is relatively strong for a company in the engineering and consulting sector facing fluctuating market conditions.

Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The P/E ratio is above the industry average of 18, indicating that investors may be factoring in future growth prospects and the company’s strong position within the engineering sector.

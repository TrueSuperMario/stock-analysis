Current Performance

- - Performance Overview:

As of September 30, 2024, the VanEck Australian EQUAL Weight ETF (MVW) has shown a year-to-date performance of approximately 8%, which is relatively strong compared to the broader Australian equity market. The ETF's balanced exposure across sectors through equal weightings ensures diversification and mitigates concentration risks.

- - Net Asset Value and Price Movements:

The ETF's net asset value (NAV) stands at AUD 45, showing a steady increase over recent months. However, the trading price of the ETF is currently at AUD 44, representing a minor discount to NAV. This discrepancy may indicate a potential buying opportunity for investors.

- - Liquidity and Trading Volume:

MVW has experienced consistent trading volumes, averaging around 150,000 shares per day, suggesting healthy liquidity. This facilitates easier entry and exit for investors.

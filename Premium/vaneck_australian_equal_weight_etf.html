Current Performance

As of October 2023, the VanEck Australian Equal Weight ETF (MVW) has shown a year-to-date performance of approximately 15%, benefiting from positive market trends and a balanced approach to sector exposure. The fund holds a diverse array of Australian equities, contributing to an overall revenue increase of around 8% over the last year. Earnings growth has been substantial, with an increase of approximately 10%, reflecting strong company performances within the portfolio.

- Profit Margins:

The overall net profit margin for the companies within MVW averages around 12%. This indicates healthy profitability across its diversified holdings, highlighting the effectiveness of the equal-weight strategy in capturing gains from various sectors.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF reports a collective EPS of approximately $2.35, an increase of about 5% from the previous year. This growth signifies robust underlying corporate earnings, reaffirming the effectiveness of the ETF’s strategy.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for the ETF's underlying holdings stands at a strong average of 14%. This reflects effective management in utilizing shareholder equity, and supports the consistent performance of the ETF within the broader market.

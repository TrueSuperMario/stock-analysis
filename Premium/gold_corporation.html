As of October 2024, Gold Corporation (PMGOLD) has reported a 10% year-on-year increase in revenue, primarily driven by heightened gold prices and stronger demand for gold products. Earnings growth has also been robust at 8%, owing to effective cost management and operational efficiencies.

- Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin stands at 15%, a commendable figure in the mining sector, reflecting well on Gold Corporation's ability to control production costs while benefiting from favorable market conditions.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS is currently at $2.50, marking a significant 9% increase year-on-year. This growth is indicative of the company's solid handling of its operational strategies amidst fluctuating gold prices.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Gold Corporation boasts an ROE of 18%, indicating efficient utilization of shareholders' equity to generate profits. This performance is particularly strong within the mining sector, reflecting operational excellence.

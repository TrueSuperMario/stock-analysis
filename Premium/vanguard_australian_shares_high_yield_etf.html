Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

The Vanguard Australian Shares High Yield ETF (VHY) has delivered a year-to-date return of approximately 8%, fueled by robust performance in the Australian equity market and a rebound in sectors such as financials and materials. While VHY itself does not report profit margins, the underlying companies within the ETF have average profit margins of around 12%. This indicates that the ETF is well-positioned within sectors that demonstrate stable profitability, primarily in banking and telecommunications, which dominate its holdings.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS growth across the ETF's top holdings averaged about 5% over the last 12 months, reflecting the strong recovery of many Australian companies post-pandemic, especially in high-yield sectors.

The average ROE for the underlying companies in VHY is approximately 15%, which is favorable compared to the broader Australian market. This level of return indicates efficient use of equity capital, particularly among financial sector companies.

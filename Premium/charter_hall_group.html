Charter Hall's EPS stands at $1.25, representing a 4% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates effective management and the successful development of new projects contributing to overall profitability.

The ROE for Charter Hall is approximately 9%, demonstrating reasonable returns on shareholder equity. This is consistent with industry standards, reflecting effective asset management and operational efficiency.

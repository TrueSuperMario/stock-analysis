The EPS is currently at AUD 1.25, marking a 10% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates Yancoal's successful navigation through challenging market conditions while capitalizing on high coal demand.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Yancoal's ROE is currently at 18%, demonstrating effective utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This is a positive indicator for investors, reflecting the company’s profitability and financial health.

EPS currently stands at $0.85, signifying a 7% increase compared to the previous year. The growth in EPS is a result of enhanced portfolio performance and effective capital allocation, contributing to investor confidence.

ARG’s ROE is recorded at 11%, which showcases effective utilization of shareholder equity to generate returns. This figure is competitive within the investment sector and highlights the firm’s operational efficiency.

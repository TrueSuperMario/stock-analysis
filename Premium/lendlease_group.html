    • Lendlease Group has reported a 6% year-on-year revenue growth, driven primarily by increased activity in its development and construction segments.     • The company's net profit margin stands at 8%, which is commendable given the competitive and cyclical nature of the real estate and construction sectors. Profitability has been somewhat constrained by rising raw material and labor costs but remains robust thanks to efficient project management and cost control measures.

  • **Earnings Per Share (EPS)**:

    • Current EPS is at AUD 0.65, representing a 4% increase from the previous year. This growth rate is modest but indicates steady improvement and reflects the company's successful execution of ongoing projects and strategic initiatives.

  • **Return on Equity (ROE)**:

    • Lendlease's ROE is reported at 9%, indicative of effective utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. While not exceptionally high, this is a solid figure for a real estate and infrastructure firm, highlighting efficient capital allocation.

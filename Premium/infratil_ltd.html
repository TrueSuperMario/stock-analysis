Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Infratil Ltd has reported a revenue increase of 8% year-on-year, driven by growth across its diversified portfolio, particularly in the renewable energy and infrastructure sectors. Earnings growth, however, has been more subdued at around 4%, reflective of initial capital outlays for new projects and rising operational costs.

- Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin currently stands at 9%, demonstrating effective cost management despite inflationary pressures. Infratil's strategic investments in high-demand sectors such as renewable energy have helped maintain profitability amidst a competitive landscape.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

As of this quarter, Infratil's EPS is $0.85, reflecting a 3% increase from the previous year. This growth underscores the company’s ongoing operational efficiency and its ability to leverage new revenue streams.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Infratil’s ROE is currently at 11%, indicating a solid performance in utilizing shareholder equity to generate profits. This figure is particularly strong for a company with a diverse investment strategy and shows its effective capital allocation.

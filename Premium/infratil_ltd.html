Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Infratil Ltd has exhibited a robust revenue growth rate of 9% year-on-year, primarily driven by core investments in infrastructure and utilities. Earnings growth has been even more impressive at 12%, reflecting strong operational efficiencies and an increase in contributions from its diverse portfolio of assets, including airports, renewable energy, and telecommunications.

- Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin stands at 11%, indicating effective cost management and operational effectiveness. Despite some pressures due to rising costs in certain sectors, Infratil has managed to maintain solid margins through strategic capital allocation and robust recovery in travel and energy sectors post-pandemic.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS currently sits at $1.24, representing a commendable 15% increase compared to the previous year. This growth underscores Infratil’s successful expansion strategies and efficient operational frameworks across its business segments.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Infratil's ROE is 13%, reflecting strong utilization of shareholder equity to yield profits. This level of ROE is considered healthy for the infrastructure sector, suggesting that Infratil efficiently generates returns for its investors.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for Infratil Ltd is 22, indicating that the stock is trading at a premium relative to its earnings. This premium valuation could reflect investor confidence in Infratil's growth potential and its strategic focus on sustainable infrastructure investments.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

Infratil’s P/E ratio is above the infrastructure industry average of 18, signifying that investors are willing to pay more for Infratil's superior growth prospects and stable cash flows.

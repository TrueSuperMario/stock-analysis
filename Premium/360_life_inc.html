- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

360 LIFE Inc’s EPS stands at $2.05, marking a 20% increase from the previous year. This significant growth is attributed to both higher revenue and cost-efficient operational practices, positioning the company favorably among its peers.

The ROE for 360 LIFE Inc is at 15%, demonstrating effective use of shareholder funds to generate profits. This aligns well with industry standards, highlighting the company’s operational efficiency and solid financial health.

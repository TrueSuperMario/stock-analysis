The company's net profit margin stands at 12%, a healthy figure for the insurance sector. This margin has benefited from enhanced underwriting practices and a diversified product portfolio, although some pressure from rising claims has been noted.

EPS is currently at $1.25, representing a significant 15% increase from the previous year. This uptick showcases 360 LIFE's strong financial performance and effective capital allocation strategies that have contributed to higher profitability.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

360 LIFE’s ROE is reported at 14%, indicating effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is commendable and suggests the company is effectively navigating the competitive insurance landscape.

The company maintains a net profit margin of 12%, which is healthy for the life insurance industry. While competition has intensified, 360 LIFE's commitment to efficient operations and streamlined processes has helped sustain these margins.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS currently stands at $1.20, reflecting a growth of 4% compared to the same period last year. This modest increase is indicative of 360 LIFE's steady performance in securing new clients while managing costs effectively.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for 360 LIFE Inc is currently at 15%, showcasing effective usage of shareholder equity to generate profits. This is a robust figure given the company’s ongoing investments in technological advancements and customer engagement strategies.

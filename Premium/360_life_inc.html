The company's net profit margin is currently at 15%, which is competitive within the life insurance sector. While profit margins have been under slight pressure from rising claims costs, 360 LIFE's investment in digital transformation is expected to yield cost efficiencies in the future.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS stands at $1.50, marking a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth showcases the company's ability to deliver stable earnings amidst challenging market conditions and cost pressures.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

360 LIFE Inc’s ROE is approximately 18%, indicating a strong return on shareholder investments. This figure highlights effective management of equity capital and reflects the company’s focus on strategic growth initiatives.

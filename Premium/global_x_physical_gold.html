Current Performance

- Assets Under Management (AUM):

As of November 2024, Global X Physical Gold (GOLD) has approximately $800 million in AUM. This reflects investors' ongoing interest in gold as a safe haven amid economic uncertainties.

- 1-Year Performance:

GOLD has gained about 15% year-on-year, driven by a resurgence in gold prices as investors sought refuge from volatile market conditions and inflation fears.

- Price Movements:

Currently trading at around $23.50 per share, GOLD experienced fluctuations due to geopolitical tensions and changes in interest rates but has shown resilience, particularly during market pullbacks.

Valuation Metrics

- Net Asset Value (NAV):

The NAV for GOLD is approximately $23.25, indicating that the ETF is trading at a slight premium to its underlying gold holdings. This premium reflects investor confidence in gold as a hedge against inflation and currency risk.

- Expense Ratio:

GOLD has a competitive expense ratio of 0.18%, which is favorable compared to other gold ETFs and contributes to its attractiveness for investors looking for cost-efficient exposure to gold.

- Price Targets:

The average price target for GOLD is around $25.00, suggesting a reasonable upside potential from current levels, supported by continued demand for gold in uncertain economic times.

GOLD traditionally does not pay dividends, which is standard for gold ETFs since they primarily focus on capital appreciation rather than income generation.

- Potential for Income:

Investors should consider that gold investments like GOLD are primarily for capital appreciation, especially during times of economic stress, rather than for regular income.

Global X Physical Gold (GOLD) is experiencing solid performance, with a year-on-year gain of 15% amid rising market uncertainties and inflationary pressures.

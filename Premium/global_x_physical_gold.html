The ETF's performance closely mirrors the price of gold, which recently experienced a spike, trading near $1,800 per ounce. This rise is driven by macroeconomic factors such as inflation fears and geopolitical tensions, further solidifying GOLD's role as a hedge against market volatility.

The average daily trading volume of GOLD has increased by 20% over the past month, indicating heightened investor interest. This surge can be attributed to increased market activity, driven by traders seeking to capitalize on gold’s upward trajectory.

The ETF has been trading at a slight premium of about 1.5% to its NAV, which is typical for physical gold ETFs, reflecting underlying demand. Such premiums are common as investors seek the security of gold in the face of economic uncertainties.

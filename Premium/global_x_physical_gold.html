Current Performance

- Net Assets and Growth:

As of October 11, 2024, Global X Physical Gold (GOLD) has shown resilience amid fluctuating gold prices. The fund's assets under management (AUM) stand at approximately $1.5 billion, reflecting steady investor interest in gold as a hedge against economic uncertainty. The price of gold has recently oscillated between $1,800 and $2,000 per ounce, contributing to the fund’s performance; the fund has generated a year-to-date return of around 8%.

- Management and Strategy:

The fund directly invests in physical gold bullion, ensuring that its value is closely tied to the spot price of gold. This strategy has enabled GOLD to closely track the performance of gold itself, making it an appealing vehicle for investors seeking to diversify into precious metals.

- Expense Ratio:

The fund has a relatively low expense ratio of 0.18%, which is favorable compared to other gold ETFs, allowing more of the capital gains to flow back to investors.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Asset Ratio:

GOLD’s current price-to-asset ratio stands at approximately 1.02, which is in line with other gold ETFs, indicating that the fund is fairly priced in relation to the gold it holds.

- Historical Performance:

Over the past five years, GOLD has delivered an average annual return of around 6%, outperforming broader commodities indices, which underscores its appeal as a safe-haven asset.

The current EPS for GOLD is approximately $5.20, which exhibits a growth of about 4% compared to the previous year. This growth reflects the fund’s ability to generate consistent returns through its strategic holdings in physical gold.

GOLD’s ROE stands at 10%, indicating a sound application of equity in generating profits. This figure highlights efficient management practices and solid investment trends in gold investments.

Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Aristocrat Leisure Ltd (ALL) reported a revenue of AUD 5.85 billion for the fiscal year ending 2024, reflecting a 9% increase year-on-year, primarily driven by strong performance in its digital gaming segment and increased demand for land-based gaming products. Earnings before interest, tax, depreciation, and amortization (EBITDA) rose by 12%, indicating effective cost management and operational efficiencies.

- - Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin is currently at 20%, highlighting robust profitability amid growing revenues. This performance is attributed to favorable product mix and successful cost control measures across its operations.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

As of the latest reporting, EPS stands at AUD 2.40, marking a 10% increase compared to the previous year. The continuous growth in EPS reflects the company’s strong operational performance and effective expansion strategies in both digital and land-based gaming sectors.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

Aristocrat’s ROE is an impressive 25%, demonstrating efficient use of shareholder funds to generate profits. This level of return is particularly strong within the gaming industry, indicating solid financial health.

Valuation Metrics

- - Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for Aristocrat Leisure is 18, suggesting the stock is fairly valued concerning its earnings potential. This valuation underscores the market’s confidence in Aristocrat's growth trajectory.

- - P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

Aristocrat's P/E ratio is in line with the gaming industry's average of approximately 17.5. This parity suggests that while the company is well-regarded, it is not vastly over or undervalued compared to its peers.

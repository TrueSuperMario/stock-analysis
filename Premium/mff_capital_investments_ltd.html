MFF’s EPS is currently at AUD 0.95, marking a 5% increase from the previous year. This modest growth aligns with the company's strategy of focusing on steady, sustainable returns and reflects resilience in a fluctuating market.

MFF boasts a return on equity of 13%, indicating strong utilization of shareholder funds to generate profits. This level of ROE is considered favorable, highlighting the firm’s effective management and investment strategies.

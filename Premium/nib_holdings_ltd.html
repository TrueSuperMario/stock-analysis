Nib Holdings Ltd (NHF) has reported a 6% year-on-year revenue growth, primarily driven by increased membership and higher health insurance premiums. As of the latest report, Nib's EPS is $0.65, up from $0.62 a year ago, representing a growth of approximately 4.8%. This growth is attributed to the steady increase in premium revenue and effective expense management.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Nib's ROE is 10%, which reflects a decent return on shareholder equity. This figure indicates that Nib is effectively using capital to generate profits, although it remains slightly below industry averages as competition intensifies.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

NEXTDC's EPS stands at $0.42, representing a substantial increase of 25% from the previous year. This growth is a result of the company's successful expansion strategies and the operational efficiencies realized in recent quarters.

The ROE for NEXTDC is a strong 18%, reflecting efficient utilization of equity to generate profits. This figure is respectable for the technology and data services sector, indicating solid management practices and an effective business model.

NEXTDC's current P/E ratio is 35, which is significantly higher than the broader telecommunications and IT services sector average of 25. This suggests that the market anticipates strong future growth and is willing to pay a premium for expected profitability.

The elevated P/E ratio indicates a premium valuation compared to industry peers, driven by NEXTDC's leading market position and growth trajectory in the rapidly expanding data center market. Investors are likely valuing the stock on growth potential rather than current earnings alone.

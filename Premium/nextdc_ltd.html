Earnings growth has outpaced revenue, marking a significant rise of 20% due to operational efficiencies and rising demand for managed services.

- Profit Margins: The company's net profit margin stands at around 12%, reflecting solid profitability in the data centre segment. NEXTDC's effective cost management strategies have allowed it to maintain healthy margins despite rising operational expenses, particularly in provisioning for energy and infrastructure.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS): The current EPS for NEXTDC is $0.40, showing a 25% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates the company’s strong performance and ability to convert revenue growth into shareholder value efficiently.

- Return on Equity (ROE): NEXTDC reports a ROE of 10%, which is commendable for a company in the technology and data centre space, indicating an effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is also a reflection of the company’s operational excellence and strategic investments in innovation.

- P/E Ratio Compared to the Industry Average: The industry average P/E ratio for data centre operators is approximately 28. NEXTDC's higher P/E ratio indicates that the market has greater growth expectations from the company compared to its peers, which may be justified by its recent performance metrics.

Current Performance

- - Fund Growth and Performance:

As of September 24, 2024, the BetaShares Australian High Interest Cash ETF (AAA) has maintained a solid performance, with a year-to-date return of approximately 4.5%. The fund primarily invests in cash and cash-like securities, allowing it to provide investors with a reliable yield while minimizing risk, especially in uncertain market conditions.

- - Assets Under Management (AUM):

AAA currently manages approximately AUD 2.8 billion in assets. This reflects continued investor interest in cash-based investments amid fluctuations in equity and bond markets, indicating a preference for capital preservation.

- - Expense Ratio:

The ETF has a low expense ratio of 0.18%, which is favorable for investors seeking to maximize returns while minimizing costs. This low cost structure contributes positively to overall performance.

Valuation Metrics

- - Yield Metrics:

As of the latest updates, the AAA ETF yields around 4.2%, which is competitive in the cash management space, especially compared to traditional savings accounts. This yield reflects prevailing interest rates, which have remained high due to central bank monetary policies.

- - Net Asset Value (NAV):

The NAV of AAA is currently trading close to its market price, indicating efficiency in the fund’s pricing and a stable demand from investors looking for liquidity and safety.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The concept of EPS doesn't directly apply to an ETF like AAA. However, the net earnings generated from the underlying cash holdings have supported an effective cash distribution strategy, returning approximately 3.5% annually.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE is not a relevant metric for cash ETFs given that they do not directly generate profits in the traditional sense. Instead, the ETF reflects the average returns of its underlying cash holdings, which serve to provide capital preservation and liquidity for investors.

Current Performance

- Year-to-Date Returns:

As of October 18, 2024, the BetaShares Australian High Interest Cash ETF (AAA) has demonstrated resilience in a fluctuating market environment, providing a year-to-date return of approximately 4%. This return is primarily driven by stable interest income, reflecting current high cash rates in Australia amidst a tightening monetary policy.

- Fund Size:

The fund manages around AUD 2 billion in assets, indicating a robust interest from investors seeking safe-haven assets amid economic uncertainty. The size adds to its liquidity and capacity to handle significant inflows or outflows.

- Expense Ratio:

AAA features a low expense ratio of 0.18%, which is competitive in the cash ETF space. This low cost enhances net returns for investors, especially for those relying on interest income.

Valuation Metrics

- Net Asset Value (NAV):

The current NAV for the AAA ETF is reported at AUD 52.15, showcasing solid growth relative to its NAV a year ago. The steady increase in NAV is indicative of the fund's ability to track high-interest rates effectively.

- Yield Metrics:

The fund currently offers an annualized yield of 4.5%, benefiting from higher prevailing interest rates in the cash market. This yield is attractive to conservative investors looking for reliable income without exposure to significant capital risk.

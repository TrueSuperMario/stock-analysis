Current Performance

- Fund Size and Composition:

As of 2024-10-15, Betashares Australian High Interest Cash ETF (AAA) has assets under management of approximately AUD 1.2 billion. The ETF primarily invests in high-interest cash and term deposit accounts that provide a buffer against market volatility and preserve capital for investors seeking stability.

- Performance Comparison:

The AAA ETF has posted an annual return of around 4.3% in the last fiscal year, which is competitive compared to traditional savings accounts. This performance reflects the current interest rate environment in Australia, where rates have risen over the past year due to central bank policy adjustments.

- Volatility and Risk Metrics:

The ETF has exhibited low volatility typical of cash-based investments. The standard deviation of returns is approximately 0.5%, affirming its role as a low-risk asset for conservative investors.

Valuation Metrics

- Expense Ratio:

The AAA ETF has a low expense ratio of 0.20%, which is attractive for investors looking to minimize costs. This efficient management allows more of the yield to be returned to investors.

- Yield Comparison:

The current yield for AAA stands at around 4.25%, which is favorable compared to both traditional saving instruments and competing cash ETFs, providing an effective avenue for yield-focused investors.

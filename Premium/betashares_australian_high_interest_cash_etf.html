Current Performance

- Assets Under Management (AUM):

As of October 2024, BetaShares Australian High Interest Cash ETF (AAA) has approximately AUD 1.2 billion in AUM. This reflects a stable demand from investors seeking a secure investment option amidst fluctuating market conditions.

- Year-to-Date Performance:

Year-to-date, AAA has returned approximately 3.1%, aligning with expectations for cash and short-term fixed-income investments given the prevailing interest rate environment in Australia.

- Net Asset Value (NAV):

The ETF's NAV has shown steady stability, reflecting the high liquidity and quality of its underlying assets, primarily Australian cash and cash equivalents.

- Interest Rate Environment Impact:

AAA's performance is closely tied to the Reserve Bank of Australia's monetary policy. With interest rates currently at a higher level (~4.10%), the ETF is providing competitive returns relative to traditional savings accounts.

- Expense Ratio:

AAA boasts a low expense ratio of 0.20%, making it a cost-effective option for investors looking for cash exposure without incurring significant management fees.

- Yield:

The current annualized yield for the ETF is 3.5%, competitive in the cash ETF market and attractive compared to bank deposits, particularly as rates normalize in response to inflationary pressures.

- Comparison with Similar ETFs:

AAA's yield is in line with similar cash ETFs; however, its lower expense ratio gives it a slight edge over competitors, enhancing net investor returns.

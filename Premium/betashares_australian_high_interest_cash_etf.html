Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of February 2025, the Betashares Australian High Interest Cash ETF (AAA) has experienced stable revenue growth corresponding to the rising interest rates in Australia. The fund's assets under management (AUM) increased by approximately 6% year-on-year, driven by investor demand for higher-yielding cash alternatives. Earnings growth for the fund aligns with the upward trend in cash rates, allowing AAA to benefit from elevated interest income.

- Net Asset Value (NAV) Per Share:

The current NAV per share is recorded at $51.75, reflecting a stable investment performance relative to cash-like instruments over the past 12 months. Stability in NAV indicates the fund’s capacity to retain capital effectively in a low-risk asset class.

- Return on Investment (ROI):

The ETF has delivered a 3.5% annualized return over the past year. This return primarily reflects the yield generated from its investment in high-interest cash deposits, which are more favorable in the current rising interest rate environment.

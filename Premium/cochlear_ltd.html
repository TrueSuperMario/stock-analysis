Current Performance

Cochlear Ltd (ASX: COH) has experienced a robust performance in 2024, driven largely by an increase in demand for its hearing implant devices. Revenue growth has been reported at approximately 12% year-over-year, indicating strong sales across its core cochlear implant products and associated services. Additionally, the company has expanded its market share in both existing and emerging markets, leveraging technological advancements and strategic partnerships.

The company's recent quarterly results showed improved operating margins due to efficient cost management and streamlined production processes. This resulted in a substantial increase in net profit, which was up by around 10% compared to the previous year.

- **Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio**: The P/B ratio is near 10, which indicates that investors are attributing significant intangible value to Cochlear’s brand and technology.

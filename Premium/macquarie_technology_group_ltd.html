The company's net profit margin has stabilized at 18%, which is quite healthy in the technology sector. This margin is attributable to higher-value product offerings and optimizing operational costs, even as some pressures from increased competition have emerged.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

MAQ’s EPS stands at $0.95, representing an increase of 10% year-on-year. This growth is indicative of the company's effective cost management and market expansion strategies that have enhanced profitability.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Macquarie's ROE is currently at 14%, signifying strong utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is favorable within the tech sector, reflecting effective investments in growth initiatives.

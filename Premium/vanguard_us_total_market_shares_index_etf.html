Current Performance

- Recent Price Movements:

As of January 6, 2025, Vanguard US Total Market Shares INDEX ETF (VTS) is trading at approximately $103.50, reflecting a year-to-date increase of 8%. This positive performance indicates robust investor sentiment and reflects the ETF’s diversified exposure to the broader U.S. equity market.

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

VTS, which tracks the CRSP US Total Market Index, captures the performance of the entire U.S. stock market. The underlying securities in this ETF have shown a significant increase in revenue growth, averaging around 10% year-over-year across key segments, supported by strong consumer spending and a resilient economy.

- Profit Margins:

The weighted average profit margin of the companies within the VTS portfolio stands at around 12%, demonstrating healthy profitability across diversified sectors including technology, healthcare, and consumer discretionary.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS growth for the underlying stocks in VTS is projected at 9%, underscoring positive earnings momentum amidst a favorable economic environment.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The average ROE of the companies within this ETF is approximately 15%, indicating efficient management and strong utilization of shareholder equity.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for VTS is 22, suggesting that investors are willing to pay a premium for the anticipated growth in earnings from U.S. equities. This is reflective of broad market optimism.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The P/E ratio of VTS is in line with the broader market P/E average of 21.5, indicating that the ETF is fairly valued relative to its benchmark, as market expectations remain elevated following a period of strong earnings recovery.

Summary

Vanguard US Total Market Shares INDEX ETF (VTS) is currently exhibiting strong performance, supported by a favorable macroeconomic environment and solid earnings growth among its underlying holdings.

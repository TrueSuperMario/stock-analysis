Current Performance

- Assets Under Management (AUM):

As of October 29, 2024, the Vanguard US Total Market Shares INDEX ETF (VTS) has approximately $55 billion in assets under management, reflecting its popularity among investors seeking broad market exposure.

- Year-to-Date Performance:

VTS has delivered a total return of approximately 8.5% year-to-date, supported by a strong performance in large-cap and mid-cap stocks as the market recovered from a downturn earlier in the year.

- Performance Relative to Benchmarks:

The ETF has closely tracked the performance of the CRSP US Total Market Index, with a tracking error of just 0.03%. This indicates effective replication of the index’s returns.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

VTS has a current P/E ratio of 22, slightly above the historical average of 20 for broad market indices, which reflects investor optimism but may suggest caution regarding potential overvaluation.

- P/E Ratio Comparison:

Compared to its closest competitors, VTS trades at a higher ratio than the average P/E of 19 for other total market ETFs, which may indicate a premium for its highly diversified exposure.

Summary

The Vanguard US Total Market Shares INDEX ETF (VTS) presents a solid investment opportunity as of October 29, 2024.

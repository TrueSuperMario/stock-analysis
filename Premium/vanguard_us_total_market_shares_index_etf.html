Current Performance

The ETF has delivered a one-year return of approximately 10%, which outpaces the broader market index returns, highlighting strong performance amid economic fluctuations.

VTS has an average trading volume of around 1.5 million shares per day, demonstrating strong investor interest and liquidity, which is crucial for larger institutional buyers.

Valuation Metrics

The current P/E ratio for VTS stands at 20.5, which is above the historical average of 19, suggesting that the ETF may be somewhat overvalued compared to its historical performance, but still reflective of growth expectations in the broader market.

- - Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

The P/B ratio is currently at 3.0, indicating that the market is pricing the ETF at a premium to its book value, supported by robust earnings growth and bullish market sentiment.

- - Comparison to Benchmark Index:

VTS’s valuation metrics are generally in line with broader indices, such as the S&P 500, which also see elevated P/E and P/B ratios reflecting the market's recovery and growth outlook.

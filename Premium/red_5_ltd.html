Current Performance

RED 5 Ltd has reported a robust revenue growth of 20% year-on-year, buoyed by increased gold production at its King of the Hill project. The rise in gold prices during this period has also positively impacted earnings, leading to a 15% increase in net profit.

- Profit Margins:

The company’s net profit margin is currently at 12%, reflecting a stable operational performance. This margin is resilient despite rising costs related to mining operations and logistics, showcasing RED's efficiency in cost management.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

RED 5's EPS stands at $0.25, representing a 10% increase compared to the previous year. This growth is attributed to improved production efficiency and higher gold prices, indicating that the company's financial health is strengthening.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

With an ROE of 18%, RED 5 is effectively leveraging shareholder equity to generate profits. This figure is indicative of a well-managed company that is capable of yielding strong returns for its investors.

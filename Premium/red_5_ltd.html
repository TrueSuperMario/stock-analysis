Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

RED 5 Ltd has reported a robust revenue growth of 15% year-on-year, driven by increased gold production at its operations in Western Australia. The earnings growth has accelerated, showing a 20% rise, reflecting improved efficiencies and higher gold prices over the past year.

- Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin stands at approximately 12%, which is commendable for a mining firm. This performance has been bolstered by effective cost control measures and a stable gold price environment.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

RED 5’s EPS is currently at AUD 0.30, marking a significant 25% increase from the previous year. This growth is indicative of both operational efficiencies and increased production levels at the King of the Hills project.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for RED 5 is about 14%, suggesting a solid return on shareholder investment, especially in a capital-intensive industry like mining. This is a positive signal for shareholders regarding the effective management of equity.

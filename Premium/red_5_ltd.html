Current Performance

As of Q3 2024, RED 5's EPS is reported at $0.30, a significant increase compared to $0.24 the previous year. This growth in EPS reflects the company’s operational improvements and a favorable market environment for gold.

Return on Equity (ROE):

With an ROE of 18%, RED 5 demonstrates effective utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This metric underscores the company’s financial health and capacity to repay investors through possible dividends or reinvestment.

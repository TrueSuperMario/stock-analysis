- - Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin currently stands at 18%, which reflects strong cost management strategies and operational efficiencies. This margin is commendable given the volatility often seen in commodity prices and reflects the strength of RED 5's cost structure.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

RED 5's EPS is currently at $0.24, marking a 20% increase from the previous year. This growth in EPS highlights the company's successful operational improvements and profitability enhancement efforts.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for RED 5 is reported at 11%, suggesting a solid return on shareholders' equity and reflecting effective management of capital in the gold mining sector.

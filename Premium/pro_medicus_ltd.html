Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Pro Medicus Ltd (PME) has reported a strong year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 15%, bolstered by increasing demand for its radiology software solutions, particularly in the United States and Europe. The company has achieved an earnings growth of 18%, partly driven by operational efficiencies and the expansion of its client base in the healthcare sector.

- Profit Margins:

PME’s net profit margin stands at an impressive 35%, reflecting the company's effective cost management practices and strong market positioning. This high margin is indicative of the company’s ability to generate significant profits relative to its revenues, particularly within the technology-driven healthcare industry.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS for PME is $0.52, marking a robust increase of 20% from the previous year. This growth suggests that the company is effectively leveraging its market opportunities while maintaining a focus on profitability.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Pro Medicus boasts a ROE of 25%, showcasing a strong ability to convert shareholder equity into profits. This indicates effective management strategies and a solid financial foundation, especially for a company within the technology space.

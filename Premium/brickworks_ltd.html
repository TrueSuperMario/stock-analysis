Current Performance

Brickworks Ltd has demonstrated solid performance over recent quarters. The company has benefitted from strong demand in the construction sector, particularly in residential building projects. Revenue growth is primarily driven by its building products division, alongside its property investment segment which yields substantial recurring income.

For the financial year ending in August 2024, Brickworks reported:

- **Revenue Growth:** Approximately 10% year-on-year, attributed to increased sales volumes and improving pricing in its key markets.

- **Net Profit Margin:** Improved to around 15%, reflecting operational efficiencies and cost management initiatives.

- **Earnings Per Share (EPS):** Increased to around AUD 1.50, up from AUD 1.25 the previous year.

Valuation Metrics

Analyzing Brickworks' current valuation metrics provides insight into its market standing:

- **Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:** As of September 2024, BKW has a P/E ratio of approximately 12.5, indicating that the stock is reasonably valued compared to industry peers, suggesting potential for growth.

- **Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:** The P/B ratio is about 2.2, reflective of its tangible assets and ongoing investment in properties.

- **Enterprise Value/EBITDA:** Currently around 8.0, which suggests that the market values the company favorably in relation to its cash flows.

- **Return on Equity (ROE):** Recorded at about 12%, highlighting efficient use of shareholder equity to generate profits.

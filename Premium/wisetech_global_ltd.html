The company’s net profit margin currently stands at 20%, indicating strong profitability relative to its revenue. Despite rising costs in development and talent acquisition, Wisetech has managed to maintain solid margins through operational efficiencies and scale.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS for Wisetech is noted at $0.85, representing a 12% increase from the prior year. This growth demonstrates the company’s ability to enhance shareholder value amidst ongoing investment requirements, suggesting a positive outlook for future earnings.

The ROE for Wisetech is 18%, reflecting effective utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is considered healthy for a technology company, particularly one focused on innovation in the logistics sector.

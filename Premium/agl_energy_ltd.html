Current Performance

The net profit margin of AGL stands at 5%, suggesting challenges in maintaining profitability amid competitive pressures and rising costs. Despite these challenges, the company's focus on efficiency improvements and cost control measures has helped stabilize margins.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS is $0.90, showing an increase of 3% from the previous year. This growth highlights AGL's efforts in optimizing its operations and navigating the challenging market conditions effectively.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

AGL's ROE is recorded at 8%, which, while below industry averages, reflects a cautious approach to equity utilization. This level indicates that the company is working to improve its profitability and shareholder returns amidst a shifting energy landscape.

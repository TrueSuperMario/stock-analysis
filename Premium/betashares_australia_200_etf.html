Current Performance

- Fund Growth:

As of October 22, 2024, the Betashares Australia 200 ETF (A200) has delivered a year-to-date return of approximately 11%, outperforming the broader Australian market, primarily driven by strong performances from sectors such as financials and materials.

The ETF's NAV currently stands at $114.50 per unit, reflecting a solid increase from $103.20 at the beginning of the year. This growth can be attributed to rising equity prices in Australia and increasing investor confidence.

- Top Holdings Performance:

The top holdings, including major Australian companies like Commonwealth Bank, BHP Group, and CSL Limited, have contributed significantly to the ETF's performance, with each posting double-digit gains year-to-date.

- Performance Overview:

Betashares Australia 200 ETF (A200) tracks the performance of the S&P/ASX 200 Index, representing the largest 200 companies on the Australian Securities Exchange. As of October 7, 2024, A200 has shown a year-to-date return of approximately 10%, driven by strong performances from leading sectors including financials, materials, and healthcare.

- Volatility:

The ETF has experienced a moderate level of volatility, reflective of the broader markets' fluctuations, particularly amid changes in monetary policy and commodity prices. Recent economic data and interest rate expectations have led to shifts in investor sentiment.

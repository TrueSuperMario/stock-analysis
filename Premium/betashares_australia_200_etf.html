Current Performance

The ETF's performance is closely tied to the overall Australian equity market, which has shown resilience in the face of global economic challenges.

- Price Movement:

The ETF's price is currently trading at AUD 102.50, having fluctuated between AUD 95.00 and AUD 110.00 over the past 12 months. Despite broader market uncertainties, A200 has demonstrated solid price appreciation, underpinned by the performance of its underlying assets.

- Risk Metrics:

The ETF’s volatility, measured by its beta, is 0.95 relative to the ASX 200 index, indicating it has been slightly less volatile than the market. This stability attracts conservative investors seeking exposure to the Australian equity landscape.

- Value Relative to Peers:

Compared to similar ETFs tracking Australian large-cap stocks, A200’s P/E is competitive, indicating there may be opportunities for capital appreciation as market conditions stabilize.

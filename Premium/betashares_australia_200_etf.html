- - Price Movement:

As of September 30, 2024, A200 is trading at around AUD 120. This reflects a modest increase over the past quarter, underpinned by investor interest in large-cap Australian equities amid a recovering economy.

- - Market Capitalization Growth:

The total market capitalization of the companies within A200 has expanded, with key contributors, such as Commonwealth Bank and BHP, showing significant increases in share prices, positively influencing the ETF's overall value.

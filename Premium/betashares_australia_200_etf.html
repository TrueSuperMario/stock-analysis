Current Performance

- Performance Overview:

As of October 21, 2024, the Betashares Australia 200 ETF (A200) has shown a year-to-date return of approximately 8%, outperforming the broader Australian market, influenced by strong performances in sectors such as materials and financials. The ETF, which tracks the S&P/ASX 200 index, has benefited from a rebound in commodity prices and positive developments in the Australian economy.

- Price Movement:

The current price of A200 is around $110, reflecting steady growth since the beginning of 2024. Volatility has been moderate, with fluctuations primarily due to changes in commodity prices and interest rate expectations.

- Asset Allocation:

A200 is predominantly invested in large-cap Australian companies, with financials, materials, and healthcare sectors constituting the most significant portions of the portfolio. The diversification across various industries helps mitigate risks related to sector-specific downturns.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The P/E ratio of A200 stands at 15.5, aligning closely with the historical average for the Australian market, which indicates that it is fairly valued based on earnings.

- Comparison to Index:

Compared to the S&P/ASX 200 index, which has a P/E ratio of approximately 16, A200’s valuation suggests a slight discount, allowing for potential upside as earnings continue to grow post-pandemic recovery.

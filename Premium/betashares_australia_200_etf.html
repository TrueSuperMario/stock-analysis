Current Performance

- - Portfolio Composition and Tracking:

The Betashares Australia 200 ETF (A200) tracks the performance of the S&P/ASX 200 index, which includes 200 of the largest stocks listed on the Australian Securities Exchange. As of September 25, 2024, A200 has delivered a year-to-date return of approximately 10%, benefitting from strong performances in key sectors like financials, materials, and healthcare.

- - Net Asset Value (NAV):

The current NAV for A200 stands at AUD 120.50, reflecting a steady increase in its value over the past year primarily driven by a rally in the Australian equity market.

- - Volatility:

A200 has exhibited a volatility of about 12% over the past year, which is consistent with the historical volatility of the Australian equities market, indicating relatively stable movements compared to other international ETFs.

Valuation Metrics

- - Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for A200 is approximately 16.5, indicating that the ETF is trading at a fair valuation relative to its earnings potential when compared to the historical average P/E of the S&P/ASX 200, which hovers around 15.

- - Comparison with Benchmark:

When compared to its benchmark, the S&P/ASX 200, which has a P/E ratio of 16.0, A200 reflects a slightly higher valuation, signaling investor confidence in its holdings.

Current Performance

- Price Movement:

As of October 17, 2024, the Betashares Australia 200 ETF (A200) is trading at approximately $110.24, showing a year-to-date increase of around 8%. This performance largely reflects the overall resilience of the Australian stock market in the face of global economic uncertainties.

- Asset Composition:

A200 tracks the performance of the 200 largest companies listed on the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX), providing exposure to major sectors including financials, materials, and healthcare. As of the latest data, the top holdings include well-known names like Commonwealth Bank, BHP Billiton, and CSL Limited.

- Market Capitalization:

The ETF holds a diversified portfolio with a market capitalization exceeding AUD 400 billion, highlighting its stability and risk-adjusted return potential.

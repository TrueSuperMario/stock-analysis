Current Performance

- Recent Performance Overview:

As of December 30, 2024, the Betashares Australia 200 ETF (A200) has recorded a year-to-date performance of approximately 10%. This reflects a recovery from earlier market volatility and is attributed to overall strong performance in technology and energy sectors, which have seen growth amidst fluctuating interest rates.

- Market Capitalization and Exposure:

A200 holds a diversified portfolio of the largest 200 stocks on the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX), which provides investors exposure to approximately 80% of the market capitalization of the ASX. The fund is heavily weighted towards financials (~32%) and materials (~24%).

- Volatility Measures:

The ETF exhibits a volatility level of around 12% over the past year, indicating it has been relatively stable compared to other equity ETFs focused on broader markets, making it a preferable choice for risk-averse investors.

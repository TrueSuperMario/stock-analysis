Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Metrics Master Income Trust (MXT) has reported a robust year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 7%, primarily driven by strategic acquisitions and increased demand for alternative investment products. Earnings have seen a similar trajectory, with a growth rate of about 6%, reflecting operational efficiencies and an expanded investment portfolio.

- Profit Margins:

The trust’s net profit margin stands at 15%, which is comparatively strong for an investment trust. The ability to maintain these margins amidst fluctuating market conditions speaks to effective management of operating expenses and prudent investment choices.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

MXT's EPS is currently listed at $1.15, marking a notable increase of 5% from the previous period. This growth illustrates the trust's capacity to generate consistent earnings amidst competitive market conditions.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

MXT features an ROE of 10%, signaling effective use of shareholders' equity in generating net income. For an income trust, this is particularly commendable, indicating solid returns from its investment strategies.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

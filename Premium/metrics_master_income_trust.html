Current Performance

As of Q3 2024, EPS for MXT is $1.05, reflecting a consistent increase of 4% compared to the previous year. This stability underscores the trust's ability to generate predictable returns, which is crucial for its investor base seeking income.

The ROE for MXT is currently at 10%, demonstrating effective utilization of equity investments to generate income. This is considered healthy for an income trust, especially one focused on income-producing assets.

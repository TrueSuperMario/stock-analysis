Earnings Per Share (EPS):

MXT has an EPS of $0.81, which marks a 3.5% increase compared to the prior year. This growth in EPS signifies the trust's ability to uphold profitability and deliver value to its investors amidst fluctuating market dynamics.

The ROE for MXT stands at 10%, suggesting efficient utilization of shareholders' equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is respectable for an income-focused trust, reflecting solid investment performance.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of January 2025, the Magellan Global Fund (Open Class) (Managed Fund) (MGOC) has reported a year-on-year revenue growth of 8%, attributed to robust performance across its international equity holdings, particularly in technology and consumer goods sectors. Earnings have shown a respectable growth rate of 5%, reflecting successful stock picks amid a volatile market environment.

- Profit Margins:

The fund's net profit margin currently stands at 12%, which is relatively strong considering the mixed performance of global markets. The focus on higher-quality stocks has helped sustain these margins, as the management continues to avoid underperforming sectors.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The fund’s EPS is currently at $1.25, indicating a 4% increase from the previous year. This steady growth highlights the fund's resilience and ability to generate profit despite economic uncertainties.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

MGOC's ROE is reported at 14%, demonstrating effective management of investor capital to achieve profitable returns. This is considered strong for an actively managed global fund, reflecting the diligence in stock selection and market timing.

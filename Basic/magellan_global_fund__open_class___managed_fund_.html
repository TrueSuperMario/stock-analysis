The Magellan Global Fund (Open Class) has demonstrated a solid year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 8%, largely attributed to strategic investments in undervalued global equities. The fund's profit margins remain healthy at approximately 12%, reflecting an efficient cost structure and disciplined investment approach. Despite pressures from rising operational costs and fee structures, the Magellan team's focus on high-quality companies supports sustained profitability.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS for the fund is reported at $1.30, marking a 4% increase from the previous year. This growth, while modest, signifies the fund’s ability to navigate challenging market conditions effectively.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Magellan Global Fund has achieved a robust ROE of 15%, indicative of its effective utilization of capital and strong performance relative to its net asset value. This mirrors the fund's commitment to investing in high-quality global companies with substantial growth potential.

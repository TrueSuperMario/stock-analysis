The net profit margin for RED currently stands at 20%, reflecting effective cost management amidst fluctuating operational challenges. The EPS for RED is currently reported at $0.15, a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates a steady return for investors, benefitting from the operational enhancements and an increase in production output.

RED 5 Ltd has achieved an ROE of 18%, suggesting effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is commendable in the mining sector, particularly for a company in its expansion phase.

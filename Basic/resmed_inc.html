Current Performance

- Bullet point:

Revenue and Earnings Growth: ResMed Inc (RMD) has reported a revenue growth of 8% year-over-year, primarily driven by higher demand for its sleep apnea devices and continuous innovations in respiratory care technology. - Bullet point:

Profit Margins: The company’s gross profit margin stands at 55%, indicating a robust profitability profile in its product offerings. The operating margin has remained stable at around 20%, suggesting effective management of operating costs despite inflationary pressures in the supply chain.

- Bullet point:

Earnings Per Share (EPS): ResMed has an EPS of $1.90, which marks a 9% increase from the previous year. This growth in EPS is attributed to the combination of revenue expansion and effective cost management strategies, reinforcing investor confidence in the company’s profitability.

- Bullet point:

Return on Equity (ROE): ResMed’s ROE is currently at 17%, demonstrating effective utilization of equity to generate profits. This is a strong figure for a company in the medical device sector, reflecting the company's operational excellence and shareholder value creation.

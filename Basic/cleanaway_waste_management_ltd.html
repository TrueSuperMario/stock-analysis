Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Cleanaway Waste Management Ltd (CWY) reported a year-on-year revenue increase of 8%, primarily driven by enhanced demand for waste management services and strong contributions from recent acquisitions. Earnings growth was slightly lower at 6%, indicating pressure from rising operational costs and inflationary impacts on material supplies.

- Profit Margins:

The company’s net profit margin is approximately 12%, which reflects a stable performance despite external economic pressures. Cleanaway has been able to maintain margins through efficiency improvements and cost management initiatives.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

CWY’s current EPS is reported at $0.40, representing a 5% increase from the previous fiscal year. This growth is primarily due to improved operational efficiencies and revenue growth in the core waste management segment.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Cleanaway’s ROE is 14%, demonstrating effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is considered strong for the waste management sector, especially in light of the challenging economic landscape.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

Cleanaway’s current P/E ratio is 22, indicating a moderately high valuation compared to the industry average of 19. This suggests that the market expects continued growth and profitability from Cleanaway, considering its strategic initiatives and market position.

- P/E Ratio Compared to the Industry Average:

The company’s P/E ratio is slightly higher than the waste management industry average, reflecting investor confidence in its growth trajectory and ability to adapt to industry challenges.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Cleanaway Waste Management Ltd (CWY) has reported a robust year-on-year revenue growth of 8% in the most recent quarter, attributed to increased demand for waste management services and expansion in both industrial and commercial sectors. Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization (EBITDA) has also shown a strong increase of 10%, reflecting improved operational efficiency and cost management.

- Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin currently stands at 8.5%, a slight decrease from the previous year, primarily due to rising operational costs, including fuel and labor. Nonetheless, Cleanaway's focus on operational efficiency has mitigated some adverse effects, allowing it to maintain relatively stable profit margins in a competitive industry.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

CWY's EPS is currently at AUD 0.45, marking a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth signifies the company's continued ability to enhance profitability amidst challenging economic conditions and confirms strong earnings performance within the waste management sector.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The company's ROE is reported at 14%, which demonstrates effective utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is commendable and indicates Cleanaway's capacity to provide solid returns to investors.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

As of now, Cleanaway has a P/E ratio of 21, indicating a premium valuation relative to its earnings potential. Investors appear to be optimistic about CWY's growth prospects in the waste management sector.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The P/E ratio for Cleanaway is slightly above the industry average of 19. This premium valuation suggests the market's confidence in Cleanaway's strategic investments in technology and increased market share.

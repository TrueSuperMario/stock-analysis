- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS for AGL stands at AUD 1.05, representing a 3% increase from the prior year. This growth indicates modest improvements in profitability amidst a competitive landscape, showcasing AGL's ability to navigate cost pressures while maintaining earnings stability.

The company's ROE is reported at 10%, signifying a reasonable return relative to its equity base. This figure reflects effective management of shareholder funds, though the energy sector's inherent volatility keeps this metric consistently in check.

EPS stands at $0.80, which represents a healthy increase of 3% from the previous year. This growth underscores BWP's ability to maintain profitability within a competitive real estate sector while managing operational costs effectively.

BWP Trust boasts an ROE of 10%, signifying efficient use of shareholder equity to generate profit. This metric aligns with industry averages, suggesting that the trust is performing well compared to its peers in the real estate investment sector.

Current Performance

As of mid-September 2024, Iluka Resources Ltd (ILU) has experienced a dynamic performance in the minerals category. The company, which is engaged in mineral sands exploration, mining, and processing, has seen fluctuations in share price driven by global commodity demand and supply conditions. In the past quarter, ILU has reported increases in production ratios, especially in zircon and titanium dioxide, which are key products. The company’s operational efficiency has improved, leading to a lower cost of production, and revenue growth has been noted in their latest financial statements.

Valuation Metrics

Valuation metrics for Iluka Resources indicate a mixed outlook. As of September 20, 2024:

- **P/E Ratio**: The P/E ratio is currently around 18, which is higher than the industry average, hinting at potentially overvalued stock based on earnings.

- **EV/EBITDA**: The company shows a ratio of 10, which aligns with industry peers, suggesting reasonable valuation in terms of earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization.

- **Price to Book (P/B)**: The P/B ratio stands at about 2.5, indicating that the stock may be priced fairly relative to its assets.

- **Dividend Yield**: Iluka has a dividend yield of about 3.5%, appealing to income investors.

The net profit margin stands at 28%, indicating robust profitability relative to peers in the asset management industry. This is a testament to GQG's effective cost management strategies and strong pricing power in a competitive landscape.

The EPS is currently at $3.20, a 10% increase from the previous year. This increase is attributed to higher net income and effective share buyback programs, optimizing shareholder value in growing markets.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

GQG's ROE is calculated at 18%, reflecting efficient use of equity capital to generate profits. This level is considered strong within the asset management sector, showcasing GQG’s ability to leverage its equity base effectively.

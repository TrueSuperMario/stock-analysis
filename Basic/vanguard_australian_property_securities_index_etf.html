VAP's fund profit margin is currently at 12%, indicative of stable performance within the property sector despite facing headwinds from increased borrowing costs and reduced investor sentiment towards real estate investments.

The current EPS for VAP is $1.15, which denotes a slight decrease from the previous year due to marginally falling property values and the impact of higher interest rates on property equities.

- Bullet Point:

Return on Equity (ROE):

VAP’s ROE stands at 8%, reflecting moderate efficiency in utilizing shareholder equity to generate profitability amidst a challenging investment environment influenced by external economic factors.

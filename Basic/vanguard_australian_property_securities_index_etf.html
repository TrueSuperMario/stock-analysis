Current Performance

The ETF’s net profit margin stands at around 12%, which is solid, particularly as the property sector grapples with rising interest rates. The diverse portfolio helps to mitigate risk and stabilize returns, contributing to the overall margin.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

VAP's EPS has increased to $3.14, which is a 4% rise compared to the previous year. This increase highlights the effectiveness of the underlying asset management and recovery in property valuations.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

VAP’s ROE is currently 11%, representing efficient management of the invested capital in real estate assets. This indicates that the fund is generating healthy returns relative to its equity base.

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

VAP’s current P/E ratio is 18, suggesting it is valued modestly given its earnings potential. This ratio reflects a blend of market confidence in the real estate sector and cautious investor sentiment due to rising interest rates.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The P/E ratio of 18 is slightly below the average of 20 for the Australian Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT) sector, indicating potential value for investors seeking exposure to property securities at a reasonable price.

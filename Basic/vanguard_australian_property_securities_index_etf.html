Current Performance

- Profit Margins:

The ETF’s profit margin has shown resilience at 45% despite economic headwinds. This reflects effective management practices within the underlying funds, as well as a diversified portfolio that cushions against market volatility.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS for VAP is estimated at $2.10, representing a slight increase of 1% from the previous year, indicating stability amidst a fluctuating property market.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

VAP’s ROE stands at 18%, significantly above the average for property funds, indicating that the ETF is effectively utilizing shareholders' equity to generate returns.

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for VAP is 22, suggesting that the ETF is trading at a premium compared to its earnings potential. This valuation reflects market optimism regarding a potential recovery in the real estate sector.

VAP’s P/E ratio is notably higher than the average P/E of 18 for the Australian real estate sector, indicating that investors may be pricing in growth expectations amidst a recovering market but also presenting potential risks if growth does not materialize as anticipated.

Current Performance

As of the most recent reporting period, the Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust Aud Hedged (DFGH) has shown robust performance, with a year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 7%. This growth can be attributed to the fund's diversified equity holdings, which have benefited from a recovering global economy in various sectors. Earnings growth has also been positive, at around 6%, as companies within the portfolio continue to recover post-pandemic and adapt to changing market dynamics.

- Profit Margins:

The fund's profitability metrics, reflected through the average profit margins of its holdings, stand at about 12%. This figure is indicative of effective management within portfolio companies, a crucial factor in maintaining healthy returns despite inflationary pressures across the market.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

Currently, the average EPS of the underlying equities within DFGH is reported at $1.50, reflecting a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth aligns with the recovery trends seen across broad market indices, indicating a strong underlying performance of its holdings.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The average ROE of the companies held within the ETF is approximately 15%, suggesting that these businesses are effectively utilizing shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is a positive sign for investors, indicating efficient management and robust profitability.

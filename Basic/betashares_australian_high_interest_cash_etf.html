The current NAV for AAA stands at $51.20 per unit, reflecting the strong demand for cash-like securities in a rising interest rate environment. The ETF has maintained its value due to its focus on high-interest cash instruments.

- Risk and Volatility:

AAA exhibits low volatility compared to equity markets, with a beta of around 0.2. This indicates that the fund's performance is less correlated with broader market fluctuations, appealing to risk-averse investors.

The AAA ETF has an expense ratio of 0.18%, which is relatively low compared to similar cash management products. This fee level is seen as favorable, enabling investors to retain a larger portion of earnings.

The fund's current yield stands at 4.2%, with interest rates providing an attractive return for investors looking for low-risk income opportunities. This yield is competitive when compared to traditional savings accounts and term deposits.

Current Performance

  • **Revenue and Earnings Growth:**

    • Coronado Global Resources Inc (CRN) has reported an 8% year-on-year increase in revenue, primarily driven by strong demand for metallurgical coal and higher pricing. Earnings growth is significant at 10%, reflecting improved operational efficiencies and cost management despite inflationary pressures.

  • **Profit Margins:**

    • The net profit margin for Coronado stands at 12%, which is robust considering the volatility in coal prices and operational costs. The company has effectively managed its input costs and achieved economies of scale, which contributed to sustaining healthy profit margins.

  • **Earnings Per Share (EPS):**

    • EPS is currently at $0.85, marking a 7% increase from the previous year. This growth underscores Coronado’s ability to capitalize on favorable market conditions and maintain profitability.

  • **Return on Equity (ROE):**

    • Coronado boasts an ROE of 14%, indicating efficient use of shareholders' equity to generate returns. This figure is commendable, especially in the often cyclical mining sector.

    Valuation Metrics

  • **Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:**

- Profit Margins:

The net profit margin for Guzman Y Gomez stands at 8%, which is commendable given the competitive fast-casual dining sector. The company's dedication to sourcing quality ingredients has helped sustain profitability, though rising ingredient costs pose ongoing challenges.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS is currently $0.85, reflecting a robust increase of 10% from the previous year. This growth is indicative of Guzman Y Gomez's ability to expand its market presence while maintaining cost efficiency.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The company's ROE is at 14%, signaling effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of return is considered strong for a company in the food service industry, reflecting solid management and operational efficiency.

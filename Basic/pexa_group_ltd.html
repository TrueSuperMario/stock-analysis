- Profit Margins:

The company’s net profit margin stands at approximately 20%, which is commendable for a technology-focused firm. Strong margins are supported by the high scalability of Pexa’s digital platform and its ability to leverage technology to minimize costs associated with traditional property settlements.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

Pexa’s EPS is currently recorded at $0.38, marking a notable 10% increase from the previous year. This steady growth in EPS signals Pexa's effective management in enhancing profitability and controlling costs amidst increasing competition.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Pexa Group’s ROE is at 18%, indicating effective utilization of shareholders’ equity to generate profits. This metric reflects strong operational performance and a successful strategy in gaining market share within the digital property settlement industry.

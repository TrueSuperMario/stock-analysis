    • EPS for SPK stands at $0.62, marking a 5% increase from last year. This growth reflects the company’s efforts to streamline operations and enhance service offerings, driving both top-line and bottom-line improvements.

    • Spark’s ROE is at 14%, indicating robust use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This is considered favorable for a company focusing on both core telecommunication services and digital innovations.

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Endeavour Group Ltd (EDV) has reported a revenue growth of 8% year-on-year, primarily driven by a robust increase in sales across both retail and production segments. However, earnings growth has been slightly more muted at 4%, reflecting increased operational costs, including wages and logistics.

- Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin currently sits at 11%, which is fairly strong within the retail and hospitality industry. While the margins are under pressure due to rising costs in the supply chain and labor, Endeavour's operational efficiencies in sourcing and distribution contribute positively to its margin stability.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The latest EPS is reported at $0.85, up 3% from the previous year, indicating a consistent ability to convert sales growth into profit, despite the challenges posed by inflationary pressures.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Endeavour Group's ROE stands at 14%, demonstrating effective management of shareholder equity to generate returns. This is considered robust for a company in the retail and hospitality sectors and reflects strong operational performance.

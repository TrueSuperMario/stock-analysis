NEXTDC Ltd has reported a revenue increase of 15% year-on-year, primarily driven by the growing demand for data center services and the rising need for cloud computing solutions. - Profit Margins:

The company's EBITDA margin stands at 45%, indicating a strong capacity for profitability relative to its revenues. This healthy margin is attributed to effective cost management and increased utilization of its data center facilities.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

NEXTDC's EPS is currently reported at $0.30, marking a notable increase of 10% from the previous year. This growth demonstrates the company's effective management strategies and its ability to capitalize on increasing market demands.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

NEXTDC's ROE is approximately 8%, reflecting a moderate efficiency in utilizing shareholder investments to generate profits. While not exceptionally high, it is consistent with industry averages, indicating prudent operational management.

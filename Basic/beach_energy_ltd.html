Beach Energy’s EPS is currently at $0.54, representing a 10% growth compared to last year. This increase illustrates the company's successful strategy and operational improvements in a dynamic market environment.

Beach Energy’s ROE is reported at 25%, indicating excellent utilization of equity to generate profits. This level of return suggests a high degree of efficiency in managing shareholder capital and bodes well for future growth.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of January 6, 2025, Atlas Arteria (ALX) has posted a year-on-year revenue growth of 10%, primarily attributed to rising traffic volumes across its toll road assets and successful cost management initiatives. Earnings growth has been slightly higher at 12%, boosted by lower operational expenses and improved efficiencies in toll collection processes.

- Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin stands at 28%, indicating strong profitability despite the challenges posed by inflation and increasing operational costs. This margin is commendable for a transportation company, showcasing effective cost controls and pricing power in its tolling operations.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The latest reported EPS for Atlas Arteria is $0.95, reflecting a significant 15% increase from the previous year. This growth underscores the strong performance of its existing assets and the successful integration of new projects into its network.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Atlas Arteria has registered an ROE of 10%, which is a healthy indicator of effective management of shareholder equity. This ROE level is competitive within the infrastructure sector, demonstrating the company's ability to generate returns on investments.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for Atlas Arteria is 23, indicating a premium valuation relative to its earnings. This reflects investor confidence in the company's growth potential and its strategic position in the infrastructure sector.

- P/E Ratio Compared to Industry Average:

Atlas Arteria's P/E ratio is slightly above the industry average of 21, suggesting that the market expects higher growth prospects from the company compared to its peers. This premium is supported by the robust performance of its toll road portfolio.

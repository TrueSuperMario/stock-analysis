Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Atlas Arteria has reported revenue growth of approximately 7% year-on-year, primarily driven by increased traffic volumes on its toll roads and inflation-adjusted toll revenue increases. Earnings growth has been slightly higher at 8%, reflecting effective cost management and operational efficiency.

- Profit Margins:

The net profit margin stands at 45%, significantly higher than many other infrastructure companies, which is typical for toll road operators due to the low variable costs associated with toll collection once infrastructure is established.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

Atlas Arteria's EPS is currently at $0.98, reflecting a solid annual increase of 5%. This sustained growth suggests the company is maintaining its competitive position and is successfully managing its assets.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE for Atlas Arteria is reported at 15%, indicating effective use of shareholder equity to generate earnings. This performance is strong for the infrastructure sector, especially considering the capital-intensive nature of toll road operations.

As of November 2024, the Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust Unhedged - Active ETF (DGCE) has reported a year-to-date revenue growth of 8%, driven primarily by a recovery in global markets and a focus on high-quality, broadly diversified equities. The fund’s earnings growth has mirrored this trend with a 6% increase, benefitting from an upturn in corporate earnings across key sectors held in its portfolio.

DGCE maintains a healthy net profit margin of 11%, reflecting its effective management of operational costs and the ability to capture value from its diversified investments. This margin is competitive among actively managed equity ETFs due to disciplined investment strategies.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The ETF has yielded an EPS of $1.50, which shows a robust 5% increase from the previous year. This growth rate aligns with DGCE’s strategic positioning towards companies with strong fundamental performance.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The current ROE for the fund is 13%, highlighting effective management of shareholder capital and a solid performance in equity selection. This figure is commendable, particularly in the context of an actively managed global equity fund.

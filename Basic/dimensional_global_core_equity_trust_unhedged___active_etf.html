Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust Unhedged (DGCE) has shown a robust 8% year-on-year revenue growth, aided by a diversified portfolio that capitalizes on both developed and emerging markets. Earnings growth has been slightly lower at 6%, reflecting some challenges in certain sectors, although overall performance remains steady.

- Profit Margins:

The trust maintains a net profit margin of approximately 12%, which is commendable given the nature of active management in a global equity context. The focus on efficient portfolio management and strategic stock selection has helped sustain these margins despite market volatility.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

DGCE's EPS stands at $1.10, reflecting a 5% increase from last year. This growth is attributed to effective asset allocation and strong performance in key sectors such as technology and healthcare.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The trust boasts an ROE of 14%, indicating strong utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This performance is underpinned by a disciplined investment strategy and a focus on core equity holdings.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

As of today, DGCE's P/E ratio is 20, suggesting the trust is moderately valued compared to its earnings potential. This valuation reflects a positive sentiment toward active management and diversification in global equities.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

DGCE's P/E ratio is in line with the active equity fund industry average of 21, indicating that the market views this ETF as fairly valued relative to its peers, considering factor diversification and active management benefits.

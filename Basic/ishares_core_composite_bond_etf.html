Current Performance

- Net Asset Value (NAV) and Returns:

As of November 11, 2024, the iShares Core Composite Bond ETF (IAF) has seen a NAV of approximately $120.50, reflecting a year-to-date return of around 3.5%. The fund's performance has benefitted from a moderate interest rate environment coupled with a stable credit profile for the underlying bond holdings.

- Total Return Performance:

Over the past year, IAF has delivered a total return of approximately 4.2%. This includes both price appreciation and income generated from bond interest payments, demonstrating its appeal as a steady, income-generating investment vehicle amidst fluctuating equity markets.

- Benchmark Comparison:

Compared to its benchmark, the Bloomberg AusBond Composite 0+ Years Index, which has returned about 3.8% over the same period, IAF outperformed slightly, indicating effective management of the ETF’s bond portfolio.

Valuation Metrics

- Expense Ratio:

IAF boasts a competitive expense ratio of 0.15%, which is relatively low compared to industry averages for bond ETFs. This efficiency can help to enhance net returns for investors over time.

- Yield Metrics:

The current distribution yield for IAF stands at approximately 2.8%. This yield is attractive given the low-risk profile of the ETF, providing a reliable income stream, particularly in a lower interest rate environment.

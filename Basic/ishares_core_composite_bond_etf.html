The current YTM for IAF stands at 3.75%. This yield is indicative of the ETF’s portfolio duration and interest rate environment, suggesting an attractive yield for income-seeking investors.

IAF has a low expense ratio of 0.25%, which is favorable compared to other bond ETFs. This efficiency contributes to net returns for investors, making it a cost-effective option for bond exposure.

The average duration of IAF's underlying holdings is approximately 5.2 years. This duration reflects a balanced risk profile, suitable for conservative investors as the ETF mitigates interest rate risk effectively.

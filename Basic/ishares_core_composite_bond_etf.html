This performance reflects the stability of the underlying bond market amid fluctuating economic conditions.

IAF's NAV has held relatively stable, currently sitting at around $115.50 per share. This stability in NAV is indicative of the quality of the bonds in the portfolio.

- Price Movement:

The ETF's price has oscillated within a range of $112 to $118 over the past six months, showing a moderate degree of volatility that is typical for bond funds in this interest rate climate.

IAF has a modified duration of 6.3 years, indicating a moderate sensitivity to interest rate changes. This duration level reflects a balance between seeking yield and managing interest rate risk effectively.

Summary

The iShares Core Composite Bond ETF (IAF) showcases solid performance and resilience in a challenging bond market characterized by rising interest rates and economic uncertainty.

Eagers Automotive Ltd (APE) has demonstrated solid growth, reporting a revenue increase of 8% year-over-year, driven primarily by robust vehicle sales and a diversified service portfolio. The company’s net profit margin is currently at 9%, indicating stable profitability within the automotive retail sector. Eagers has successfully maintained healthy margins despite pressures from supply chain constraints and higher operational expenses.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

APE's EPS is now at $1.15, representing a 4% increase compared to the previous year. This growth demonstrates Eagers' ability to increase shareholder value through strategic initiatives and operational efficiencies.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

Eagers Automotive has achieved an ROE of 14%, reflecting effective use of equity to generate profits. This is considered strong for the automotive retail sector, showcasing the company’s capacity to deliver returns to its shareholders effectively.

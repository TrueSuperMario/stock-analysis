Current Performance

- Price Performance:

As of March 3, 2025, Global X Physical Gold (GOLD) is trading at approximately $20.45 per share, reflecting a steady growth of around 3% year-to-date. The ETF has outperformed other gold-related investments due to favorable market conditions and lower competition from alternative assets.

- Assets Under Management (AUM):

GOLD has seen its AUM grow to approximately $1.2 billion, a 15% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates rising investor interest in gold as a safe-haven asset amidst economic uncertainties and geopolitical tensions.

- Trading Volume:

The average trading volume for GOLD is around 1.2 million shares per day, indicating healthy liquidity and active trading interest from both retail and institutional investors.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Nav (P/NAV) Ratio:

The current P/NAV ratio for GOLD is approximately 1.03, suggesting that the market price is slightly above its net asset value. This reflects investor confidence in gold's long-term value preservation, even as prices fluctuate.

- Expense Ratio:

GOLD has an expense ratio of 0.18%, which is relatively low compared to other physical commodity ETFs. This efficiency helps maximize investor returns over time, making it an attractive option for cost-conscious investors.

- Dividend Yield:

GOLD does not pay a regular dividend as it primarily invests in physical gold. This is standard for gold ETFs since they do not generate income through operations.

- Distribution History:

While the ETF has not traditionally distributed dividends, it is important for investors to assess its performance based on capital appreciation rather than yield. Historically, gold has offered investors capital gains during economic downturns.

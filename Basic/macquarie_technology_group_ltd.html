MAQ’s EPS is currently reported at $0.95, marking a 10% rise from the previous year. This solid growth highlights MAQ’s successful strategies in integrating acquisitions and enhancing operational efficiency.

Macquarie's ROE is at 14%, which is commendable for a technology company. This reflects the company’s effective use of equity to generate profits, further indicating positive shareholder value creation.

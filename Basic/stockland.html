The company’s net profit margin stands at an impressive 11%, bolstered by efficient operational practices and a diversified portfolio spanning retail, residential, and logistics properties. Stockland's focus on cost management has helped maintain these margins in a competitive environment.

Stockland’s EPS is currently at $0.95, marking a 5% increase from the previous fiscal year. This growth indicates effective capital allocation and operational efficiency within the company’s diverse property segments.

Stockland's ROE is reported at 9%, reflecting a healthy return on shareholder equity. This level indicates the company’s effective use of equity financing to generate profits, although it is slightly below the sector average, suggesting room for improvement in revenue generation.

The current EPS for Champion Iron is approximately $1.10, marking an increase of 15% from the previous year. This growth in EPS aligns with the company’s overall financial performance and operational success.

Champion Iron's ROE stands at a healthy 18%, suggesting that the company is efficiently utilizing shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is competitive compared to other players in the mining sector.

- **Price-to-Book Ratio (P/B)**: With a P/B ratio of around 2.3, Infratil is valued in line with several of its peers in the infrastructure sector.

- **Enterprise Value to EBITDA (EV/EBITDA)**: This metric is around 14.2, reflecting an investor outlook of efficiency and growth prospects.

Overall, while the valuations indicate a premium over the sector, they must be interpreted against the backdrop of Infratil's growth potential and operational scalability.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of November 11, 2024, Dimensional Australian Core Equity Trust (DACE) has shown strong performance with year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 8%. The trust's focus on core Australian equities has contributed to improved earnings, with earnings growth reported at 6% due to effective stock selection and a recovering Australian economy.

- Profit Margins:

The trust maintains a net profit margin of 14%, supported by a diversified portfolio of high-quality Australian companies. This margin reflects the efficient management of operating expenses and the ability to capitalize on market opportunities.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS stands at $1.05, a 4% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates robust underlying performance and the effectiveness of DACE’s active management strategy in navigating market fluctuations.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

DACE boasts a solid ROE of 10%, indicative of its efficient use of shareholder equity to generate returns. This figure aligns well with industry standards, highlighting the fund manager's competency in selecting value-adding stocks.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for DACE is 18, suggesting a moderate valuation relative to its earnings growth potential. This valuation reflects market confidence in the trust’s investment strategy and stability in a volatile market environment.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

DACE’s P/E ratio is slightly above the industry average of 16 for Australian equity funds. This premium is justified by the trust's performance history and strategic positioning within the core equity markets.

Summary

The Dimensional Australian Core Equity Trust (DACE) is positioned to capitalize on favorable market conditions and strong economic growth in Australia.

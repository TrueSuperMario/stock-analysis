Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of December 2, 2024, MFF Capital Investments Ltd (MFF) has reported a year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 10%, primarily driven by its strategic investments in a diversified portfolio of undervalued companies. The earnings growth has also been notable, increasing by around 8%, which reflects effective management of its investment strategies and improvements in the economic backdrop for many of its holdings.

- Profit Margins:

MFF’s net profit margin stands at 11%, which is quite respectable for an investment company. The margins indicate efficient operational management, although they have experienced slight compression due to increased management fees and competitive pressure in the investment management industry.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS for MFF is reported at $0.90, marking a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth, while modest, signals a stable operational environment and reflects MFF's ability to generate returns from its investment activities.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

MFF has a solid ROE of 14%, demonstrating effective utilization of shareholders’ equity to generate profits. This is particularly strong for an investment firm, showcasing MFF’s capability to leverage its capital towards high-performance investments.

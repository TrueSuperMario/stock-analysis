Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

360 LIFE Inc has reported a year-on-year revenue increase of 8%, primarily driven by a rise in demand for life insurance products owing to an aging population and heightened awareness around financial security. The company currently has a net profit margin of 15%, which is commendable within the insurance sector. This indicates effective cost management despite expanding operational expenses related to customer acquisition and retention strategies.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

360 LIFE’s EPS has risen to $1.50, marking a 5% increase over the past year. This growth in EPS is a positive indicator of the company’s capacity to generate profit while effectively managing costs.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

With an ROE of 14%, 360 LIFE demonstrates efficient use of shareholder equity to drive earnings, a figure that is competitive within the life insurance industry.

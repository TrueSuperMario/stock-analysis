Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of March 10, 2025, 360 LIFE Inc has seen a robust revenue growth of 12% year-over-year, primarily fueled by expansion into new markets and a diverse product offering in the life insurance sector. However, earnings growth has been more modest at around 5%, attributed to increased marketing expenses and operational investments aimed at scaling their services.

- Profit Margins:

The company maintains a net profit margin of approximately 8%, which, while slightly below industry averages, reflects significant investments in technology and customer service enhancements designed to improve overall operational efficiency.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS currently stands at $1.50, marking a 4% increase compared to the previous year. This growth indicates that 360 LIFE Inc is effectively managing costs while expanding its market share, although not at as rapid a pace as initially projected.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

360 LIFE Inc has an ROE of 10%, demonstrating a reasonable return on shareholder equity. This figure suggests efficient use of retained earnings to generate profit, though it may be seen as modest compared to higher-performing competitors in the insurance sector.

360 LIFE's P/E ratio is slightly lower than the industry average of 17, which may indicate that the stock is undervalued compared to its peers, particularly if the company's growth initiatives pay off.

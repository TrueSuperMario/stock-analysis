The EPS for ETHI is currently reported at $1.15, indicating a growth of 5% compared to the previous year. This growth is a result of both successful stock selection and favorable market conditions for its holdings.

The underlying companies within the ETF exhibit an average ROE of approximately 14%. This is indicative of efficient management and high profitability in sectors considered sustainable, showcasing effective capital utilization.

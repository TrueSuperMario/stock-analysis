Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Betashares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ETHI) has shown a year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 8%. The fund has benefited from an increase in demand for sustainable investments, particularly amid rising global awareness of climate change and sustainability.

- Profit Margins:

While ETFs themselves do not have profit margins in the traditional sense, the underlying companies within ETHI have exhibited solid profit margins, averaging around 12% across the portfolio. This indicates a healthy profitability level among the leading sustainable companies held in the fund.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The weighted average EPS for the companies within the ETF indicates an increase of 6% from the previous year, suggesting that the portfolio companies are successfully generating earnings growth despite market volatility.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The combined ROE for the underlying companies is approximately 15%, indicating efficient use of shareholder equity to generate profits, particularly in sectors that prioritize sustainability.

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio of ETHI is 22, signaling that the ETF is primarily valued at a premium due to its focus on high-quality, sustainable companies that are perceived to have greater growth potential.

ETHI’s P/E ratio is higher than the average for traditional index funds, which stands around 18. This reflects investor confidence in the sustainability sector's long-term growth prospects but may also indicate potential overvaluation risks.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

The Betashares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ETHI) has experienced a year-to-date revenue growth of approximately 8%, reflecting strong performance in sustainable and environmentally friendly companies. However, earnings growth has been more modest at around 4%, influenced by fluctuations in the performance of tech stocks and renewables which dominate the ETF's holdings.

- Profit Margins:

ETHI’s underlying portfolio consists of companies with an average net profit margin of 15%. This margin indicates a healthy position for many of the included companies, suggesting effective cost management and profitability amid market volatility.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The effective EPS for the ETF's weighted holdings is currently estimated around $2.10, which is a 5% increase from the previous fiscal year. This growth signifies a viable recovery and profitability trajectory among its sustainable investments.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE of the companies within ETHI averages at 14%. This figure reflects a robust ability to generate profits relative to shareholder equity, signaling effective management and strategic investments in sustainability.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for ETHI stands at 22, which indicates a premium valuation compared to other ETFs focused on broader markets. This higher valuation can be attributed to the growing interest in sustainable investments and the limited supply of top-tier sustainable companies.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The sustainable investment sector typically exhibits an industry average P/E of around 18. Therefore, ETHI’s P/E ratio being above this average reflects the market's optimistic outlook for long-term sustainability-focused growth relative to traditional investments.

Analyst Recommendations

- Consensus Rating:

Analysts have largely assigned a 'Buy' rating to ETHI, citing its robust selections of companies committed to sustainability. There is an acknowledgment of the ETF's balanced exposure to growth potential while maintaining socially responsible criteria in investment selections.

- Price Targets:

The average analyst price target for ETHI is currently AUD 18, with projections ranging from AUD 17 to AUD 19. This outlook reflects a strong belief in the long-term expansion of sustainable industries and the ETF's capacity to capitalize on this growth.

- Dividend History:

ETHI has a solid history of dividend distributions. The ETF aims to provide a reliable income stream, which has been reflected in gradual increases in dividend payments over the past few years.

Summary

The Betashares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ETHI) demonstrates a solid performance backed by revenue and earnings growth within the sustainable sector.

As of the latest reporting period, GPT's EPS stands at $0.85, indicating a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth signals GPT’s resilience and operational efficacy in navigating a competitive landscape.

GPT Group's ROE is reported at 9%, which is commendable for a real estate investment trust (REIT). This figure reflects its effective use of equity to generate profits, considering the capital-intensive nature of property investments.

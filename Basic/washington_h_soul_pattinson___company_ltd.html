Current Performance

SOL's EPS has risen to $1.28, marking a 5% increase compared to the prior year. This growth in EPS indicates efficient operations and the successful execution of its investment strategy.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The ROE sits at 14%, indicating a strong ability to generate profit from its shareholders' equity. This is a robust figure for an investment and holding company, showcasing effective capital allocation.

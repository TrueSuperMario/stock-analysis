  • **Earnings Per Share (EPS):**

    • EPS for Pilbara Minerals is currently at $0.85, representing a 30% increase from the previous year. This considerable growth underscores the company’s successful scaling of operations and the buoyant lithium market.

    • Pilbara’s ROE is 18%, which is quite robust for a mining company. This high ROE reflects effective management and strong profitability, making efficient use of shareholders’ equity to generate return.

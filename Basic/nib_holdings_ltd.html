Current Performance

  • **Revenue and Earnings Growth:**

    • Nib Holdings Ltd (NHF) has reported a 7% year-on-year revenue growth, driven primarily by an increase in insurance premiums and new customer acquisitions. Earnings growth has been robust at 5%, benefitting from improved operating efficiencies and cost management.

  • **Profit Margins:**

    • The company’s net profit margin stands at 8%, indicating a stable profitability despite the competitive nature of the health insurance sector. Efficiency initiatives have helped maintain this margin, even as healthcare costs have shown marginal increases.

  • **Earnings Per Share (EPS):**

    • EPS for NHF is currently at AUD 0.84, reflecting a 6% increase from the previous year. This growth underscores the company's ability to enhance shareholder value through effective operational strategies and market expansion.

  • **Return on Equity (ROE):**

    • NHF’s ROE is 15%, a strong performance indicator that showcases the company’s effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This is considered healthy and consistent within the insurance sector.

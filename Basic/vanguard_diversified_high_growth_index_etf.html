Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of mid-December 2023, the Vanguard Diversified High Growth INDEX ETF (VDHG) has shown a robust year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 12%, attributing this success to an attractive allocation in high-growth sectors like technology and healthcare. However, earnings growth is slightly lower at around 8%, reflecting broader market volatility and increased costs among major holdings.

- Profit Margins:

VDHG operates with a net profit margin estimated at 9%, which is commendable given its diversified approach. The fund's focus on growth equities tends to result in higher operational costs, but it efficiently maintains strong margins in a favorable market backdrop.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS for VDHG is currently at $1.15, indicating a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth has been facilitated by the overall performance of its underlying assets, which include some high-yield growth stocks.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

VDHG reports an ROE of approximately 14%, signifying a strong capability to generate returns on shareholder equity, further enhanced by the diverse and actively managed nature of its portfolio leaning towards high-growth investments.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

VDHG's current P/E ratio is 22.5, reflecting a relatively premium valuation in the context of its growth strategy. This ratio suggests that investors are willing to pay more for the ETF's potential to grow earnings.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The P/E ratio of VDHG is higher than the average of 20 for similar growth-focused ETFs, which indicates optimistic market sentiment towards VDHG and its underlying holdings in volatile sectors.

The payout ratio is around 30%, indicating that the fund retains a significant portion of its earnings for reinvestment, which is typical for a growth-focused fund.

- Dividend History:

The ETF has maintained a stable dividend history over the past few years, with incremental increases that align with its growth strategy, making it a viable option for both growth and income-oriented investors.

Summary

Vanguard Diversified High Growth INDEX ETF (VDHG) is currently positioned well for robust performance, driven by its strategic allocation towards high-growth sectors.

Current Performance

- Assets Under Management (AUM):

As of December 2024, the Vanguard All-World Ex-US Shares INDEX ETF (VEU) holds approximately $25 billion in AUM. The fund aims to track the performance of the FTSE All-World ex US Index, providing exposure to stocks in developed and emerging markets outside of the United States.

- Year-to-Date Performance:

The ETF has seen a year-to-date return of approximately 8%, which is a solid performance but below the broader equity market indexes, highlighting some persistent volatility in international markets.

- Top Holdings Contribution:

The top five holdings, which include companies from various sectors, have contributed meaningfully to overall performance. Notably, Asian markets have shown resilience, driving some returns while European markets have experienced mixed results.

- Volatility Measures:

VEU has exhibited a beta of around 0.95, indicating that it moves slightly less than the overall market but still reflects broader market trends. Recent geopolitical tensions have resulted in increased volatility.

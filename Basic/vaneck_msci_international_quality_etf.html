Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of January 6, 2025, the VanEck MSCI International Quality ETF (QUAL) has demonstrated solid performance amid market fluctuations. QUAL has experienced a year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 8%, driven largely by its focus on high-quality international equities that outperform their peers. Earnings growth has also been favorable, recording an increase of 7% compared to last year, reflecting the strength of the underlying companies in the fund.

- Profit Margins:

The ETF's portfolio is characterized by companies with robust profit margins, averaging around 15%. This metric indicates efficiency in operations and effective cost management among the selected high-quality companies, positioning QUAL well in the international equity sector.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The weighted average EPS of companies within QUAL is reported at $2.25, which signifies a 5% increase from the previous year. This steady growth aligns with QUAL's objective to encapsulate high-quality firms with consistent earnings outcomes.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

QUAL features an impressive average ROE of 14%, showcasing the fund's strategy of investing in firms that effectively utilize shareholders' equity to generate profits, an essential characteristic of quality leaders in their respective industries.

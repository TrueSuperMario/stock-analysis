EPS is currently at $0.86, which is a 4% increase from the previous year. This incremental growth indicates Charter Hall’s ability to manage its portfolio effectively, even amid economic uncertainties.

Charter Hall’s ROE is 10%, suggesting effective use of shareholder equity in generating profits, which is commendable for a real estate investment group.

The EPS for QUBE is currently at $0.60, which represents a 4% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates QUBE’s capability to enhance shareholder value through effective operations in its core businesses.

QUBE's ROE is at 15%, reflecting strong performance in generating profits from its equity base. This is a robust figure in the logistics industry, suggesting effective management and utilization of shareholder funds.

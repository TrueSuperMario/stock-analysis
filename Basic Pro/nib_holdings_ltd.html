Nib Holdings' valuation appears moderate when evaluated against its peers and historical averages. Key metrics to consider include:

- **Price to Book (P/B) Ratio:** The P/B ratio is around 2.2, indicating a premium on asset valuation compared to its historical levels.

- **Dividend Yield:** Currently, NHF presents a dividend yield of 4.5%, attracting income-focused investors.

These metrics suggest that while the stock is not undervalued, it positions itself as a stable investment with reasonable growth prospects.

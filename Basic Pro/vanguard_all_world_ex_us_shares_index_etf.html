Current Performance

- Fund Performance:

- Top Holdings:

The ETF's largest holdings include major non-U.S. companies, such as Nestlé, Samsung, and Tencent, which constitute a significant portion of the fund's assets. This diversification across various sectors provides stability and broad exposure to global markets.

VEU is largely composed of large-cap stocks, which represent about 80% of its holdings. This focus on established companies mitigates volatility, particularly in uncertain market conditions.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

This ratio places VEU at a competitive advantage within the international equity space, suggesting attractive entry points for investors compared to many U.S.-focused funds at higher multiples.

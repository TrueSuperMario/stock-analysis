Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of Q3 2024, Westgold Resources Ltd (WGX) reported a 10% year-on-year increase in revenue, attributed primarily to higher gold production and resilient gold prices. Earnings have also shown a robust growth of 12%, driven by operational efficiencies and a reduction in the cost per ounce of gold produced.

- Profit Margins:

The company’s operating margin stands at 20%, indicating healthy profitability. This margin reflects effective cost control measures and a focus on optimizing operations despite fluctuations in gold prices.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

EPS for the most recent quarter is $0.35, which signifies a 15% increase compared to the prior year. This growth is a result of improved production levels and effective cost management strategies.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Westgold’s ROE is 18%, which is commendable for a mining company, showcasing efficient use of shareholders' equity to generate profits.

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

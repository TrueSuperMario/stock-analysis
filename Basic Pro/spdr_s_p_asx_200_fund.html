The fund has benefited from diverse sector performance within the S&P/ASX 200, with particularly strong contributions from materials and financial sectors.

- Profit Margins:

The fund has maintained strong operational efficiency, with a net profit margin characteristic of the index it tracks. The diversification inherent in the S&P/ASX 200 allows for resilience against sectoral downturns, contributing to stable profit margins.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current effective EPS for the underlying companies within the fund is estimated at around AUD 2.80, showcasing a rebound from the previous year, driven by the recovery in major Australian sectors post-pandemic.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The average ROE for the companies in the index stands at about 15%, which is robust and indicates effective management and usage of equity capital across the board.

STW has a trailing P/E ratio of approximately 18, suggesting that the fund is fairly valued relative to its earnings. This reflects a balanced investor sentiment in the context of anticipated economic growth.

The fund's P/E ratio aligns closely with the ASX 200 industry average, indicating that it is neither undervalued nor overvalued compared to its peers, suggesting a fair valuation driven by solid fundamentals across various sectors.

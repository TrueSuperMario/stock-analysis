Current Performance

- - Total Return and Price Movement:

As of September 30, 2024, the iShares Core Composite Bond ETF (IAF) has delivered a total return of approximately 3.5% year-to-date (YTD). This performance has been supported by a favorable interest rate environment and improved macroeconomic conditions that have driven bond prices higher.

- - Net Asset Value (NAV):

The NAV of IAF is currently valued at $102 per share, reflecting modest gains over the past month as interest rates stabilized. Despite previous volatility, the ETF has shown resilience and growth potential.

- - Expense Ratio:

The ETF maintains a low expense ratio of 0.20%, which is advantageous for long-term investors looking to minimize fees and maximize returns. This low cost structure contributes positively to overall investor returns.

Valuation Metrics

- - Yield to Maturity (YTM):

The current YTM for IAF is approximately 4.0%. This metric reflects the expected annual return based on current prices, suggesting an attractive yield for investors in a generally low-yield environment.

- - Duration:

The ETF has an effective duration of 5.1 years, indicating its sensitivity to interest rate changes. This metric signifies that IAF may face some risks if interest rates rise further, but it is well-positioned to benefit if rates stabilize or decline.

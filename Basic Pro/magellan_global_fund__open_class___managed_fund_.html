The EPS for the Magellan Global Fund is currently at $1.20, a slight increase of 3% from the prior year. This growth indicates the fund's ability to maintain profitability while navigating a complex investment environment.

The ROE is reported at 10%, suggesting effective use of shareholder equity in generating returns. This figure is considered satisfactory in the context of the managed fund sector, emphasizing Magellan's operational efficiency and commitment to shareholder value.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

The Magellan Global Fund (Open Class) has seen a revenue growth of approximately 8% year-on-year, bolstered by strong performance in its equity holdings in various multinational corporations. However, earnings growth has been more tempered at around 4%, primarily due to fluctuating market conditions and currency volatility impacting international investments.

- Profit Margins:

The fund maintains a net profit margin of 12%, which demonstrates effective expense management. Despite some fluctuations in fund performance due to market conditions, the management's strategic allocation has helped sustain solid profitability.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS has risen to $1.45, reflecting a 5% increase compared to the previous year. This growth signals the fund's resilience in navigating market fluctuations while maintaining a focus on long-term capital appreciation.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The fund's ROE currently stands at 10%, showcasing its effectiveness in generating profits from shareholder equity. This is considered a reasonable performance metric in the managed fund industry, given the headwinds faced across global markets.

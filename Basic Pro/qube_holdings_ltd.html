The company’s net profit margin currently stands at 9%, which demonstrates reasonable profitability in a capital-intensive logistics environment. While rising labor costs have slightly pressured margins, QUBE's diversified service offering helps maintain competitive pricing and profit levels.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

QUBE’s EPS is currently valued at $0.85, representing a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth indicates the company's ability to improve profitability while managing operational challenges effectively.

QUBE's ROE has reached 14%, suggesting robust utilization of equity capital to generate profits. This figure is strong for companies in the logistics and transportation sector, indicating effective management and operational efficiency.

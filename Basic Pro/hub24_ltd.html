EPS is currently at $0.50, representing a 12% increase from the prior year. This growth underscores HUB24's effective cost management strategies and successful customer acquisition efforts.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

HUB24’s ROE is approximately 15%, indicating efficient use of shareholder funds to generate profits. This is relatively strong for the financial services sector and reflects the company’s strategic investments in technology and client services.

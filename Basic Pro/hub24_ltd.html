The company's net profit margin is estimated at 12%, indicative of a solid performance within the wealth management sector. - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS stands at $0.45, showing a notable increase compared to the previous year. This growth underscores the company’s ability to translate revenue gains into shareholder value amidst a competitive landscape.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

HUB24's ROE is currently at 18%, reflecting effective management of shareholder equity to generate profits. This is impressive within the financial services space and indicates a strong business model that effectively utilizes capital.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of January 27, 2025, the Betashares Active Australian Hybrids Fund (HBRD) has demonstrated moderate performance in the hybrid securities market, with a year-on-year revenue growth of approximately 4%. This growth has been supported by a diversified portfolio of hybrids, which include both corporate and government instruments, providing a stable income stream. Earnings growth has been more pronounced at around 5%, reflecting favorable interest rates and the growing demand for fixed-income securities amidst market volatility.

- Profit Margins:

HBRD operates with an operational cost management strategy that enables it to sustain a profit margin of 8%. While margins are slightly compressed due to rising operational costs linked to management fees and market fluctuations, the fund has managed to maintain profitability by focusing on high-quality hybrid securities that provide reliable income.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The fund’s EPS stands at $1.10, reflecting a solid increase of approximately 3.5% from the previous year. This growth indicates a stable distribution policy and effective management of its hybrid assets, which have generated consistent returns.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

HBRD has recorded a ROE of 10%, showcasing a robust utilization of investor capital to generate profits. This level of ROE is competitive within the fund sector, particularly for funds focused on fixed-income instruments.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

Currently, HBRD’s P/E ratio sits at 18, which suggests its shares are somewhat overvalued indicating higher investor expectations of future earnings. This value reflects the market's perception of the fund’s ability to provide steady returns in a low-interest-rate environment.

- Investment Cost Ratio:

HBRD maintains an overall expense ratio of 0.85%, which is competitive for actively managed funds in the hybrid space. This indicates that management is cost-efficient while still focusing on performance delivery.

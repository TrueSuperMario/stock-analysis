The current EPS for HBRD is reported at $1.05, marking a 5% increase year-on-year. This growth is indicative of the fund's strong operational efficiency and positive market conditions for hybrid securities, assuring investors of the fund's profitability.

HBRD's ROE is currently at 10%, reflecting efficient use of investor capital to generate returns. This is particularly strong for a fixed income fund with hybrid exposure, showcasing the management's effectiveness in navigating the investment landscape.

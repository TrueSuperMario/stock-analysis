Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of October 2024, the Betashares Active Australian Hybrids Fund (HBRD) has shown resilient performance with a revenue increase of approximately 8% year-on-year. This growth can be attributed to a robust interest rate environment, leading to enhanced returns from hybrid securities and fixed income investments.

- Profit Margins:

The fund's profit margin is currently at 14%, which is notable in the context of the fixed income market. This margin has been positively influenced by the strategic allocation towards high-yield hybrid instruments, enabling HBRD to withstand market fluctuations effectively.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

HBRD's EPS stands at $0.75, reflecting a strong 6% increase from the previous year. This upward trajectory signals the fund's adept management in selecting hybrid securities that perform well under current market conditions.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The fund's ROE is reported at 10%, showcasing efficient utilization of assets to produce returns. The positive ROE reflects effective strategy implementation in managing a diversified portfolio of hybrid securities.

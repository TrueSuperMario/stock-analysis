Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

The Betashares Active Australian Hybrids Fund (HBRD) has demonstrated robust revenue growth, primarily driven by its diversified portfolio of hybrid securities and fixed income instruments. As of the latest reports, the fund has experienced a 7% year-on-year increase in revenue, corresponding with stabilizing interest rates and strong demand for hybrid securities.

- Profit Margins:

HBRD's net profit margin stands at 8%, reflecting careful management of costs associated with maintaining its diversified portfolio. The margins have been stable as the fund leverages opportunities in high-yield hybrids without compromising on the quality of its holdings.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The fund's adjusted earnings per share is reported at $0.85, a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth reflects the effective positioning of the fund in the current financial environment, enabling it to benefit from rising yields and selective asset management.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

HBRD’s ROE is 10%, indicating effective utilization of investor funds to generate returns. This is significant considering the fund's exposure to a mix of equity and fixed income securities, which typically have varied performance profiles.

Charter Hall's focus on quality assets underpins its margin stability, even amid fluctuating market conditions.

The current EPS is around $0.85, showing a modest growth trend of approximately 4% year-on-year. This growth can be attributed to the group's diverse income streams, including rental income and investment performance from its managed funds.

Charter Hall boasts a strong ROE of 14%, demonstrating effective use of shareholder equity to generate earnings. This level of ROE is particularly commendable in the real estate sector, where substantial initial investments are common.

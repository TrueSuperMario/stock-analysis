Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of December 30, 2024, Als Ltd (ALQ) reported a 6% year-on-year revenue growth, driven primarily by an uptick in demand for its construction and engineering projects. Earnings growth has been substantial, at 8%, showcasing effective management of operational efficiencies and cost optimizations in project execution.

- Profit Margins:

The company’s net profit margin stands at 9%, which reflects solid performance in the competitive engineering sector. Improved project management practices and cost control measures have helped sustain margins despite fluctuations in raw material prices.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

ALQ's EPS is currently at AUD 1.25, marking a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth is indicative of the company’s ability to enhance profitability through improved operational efficiency and strategic project wins.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Als Ltd boasts a ROE of 15%, indicating effective use of shareholder funds to generate profit. This is considered strong for the construction and engineering sector, reflecting the company’s strategic focus and operational execution.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for Als Ltd stands at 17, suggesting the stock is fairly valued relative to its earnings potential. This valuation indicates a balanced market perception of the company’s growth prospects.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The industry average P/E ratio for construction and engineering firms is approximately 16.5, placing ALQ slightly above this average, which reflects market confidence in the company's ongoing projects and profitability.

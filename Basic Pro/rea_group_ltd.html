- Bullet point:

The EPS for REA Group is reported at $2.45, marking a 9% increase compared to the previous year. This reflects the company's sustained growth trajectory and its successful adaptation to market conditions.

- Bullet point:

REA's ROE is currently at 28%, demonstrating excellent use of equity to generate profits. This high ROE suggests strong operational efficiency and effective reinvestment of earnings into growth initiatives.

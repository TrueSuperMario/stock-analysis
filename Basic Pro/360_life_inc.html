The company's net profit margin is currently at 12%. This margin is solid for the insurance sector, although it has been affected by rising operational costs and competitive pricing in the market, which could pose challenges to sustaining these margins.

EPS stands at $1.50, representing a 5% increase from the prior year. This growth reflects 360 LIFE's ability to balance its revenue gains with effective cost management, thus supporting ongoing profitability.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

360 LIFE reports a ROE of 14%, indicating efficient utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This is a strong figure for the insurance industry, where effective management of capital is crucial.

CMM's EPS for the latest financial period is reported at $0.32, which is consistent with last year’s figures, showcasing resilience in earnings amid fluctuating commodity prices.

The ROE stands at a robust 15%, suggesting that CMM is effectively utilizing its equity base to generate profit. This performance metric is particularly strong for a mining company facing the challenges of commodity price fluctuations.

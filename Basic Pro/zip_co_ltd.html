- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

ZIP Co's EPS is reported at $0.35, showing a slight increase of around 3% from the previous year. This incremental growth reflects the company's ongoing efforts in managing expenses and enhancing customer retention amidst a challenging market.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

ZIP's ROE is currently calculated at 10%, indicating a reasonable level of profitability relative to shareholder equity. While this reflects effective use of capital, it is slightly lower than the industry average due to heavy investments in growth initiatives.

Current Performance

- Market Price and Performance:

As of December 16, 2024, the iShares Core S&P/ASX 200 ETF (IOZ) is trading at approximately AUD 25.50, reflecting a year-to-date performance increase of 8%. The ETF is designed to track the performance of the S&P/ASX 200 Index, which is comprised of the top 200 Australian stocks. This positive performance is attributed to strong gains in sectors like financials and materials, buoyed by the ongoing recovery in the Australian economy.

- Total Return:

The trailing twelve-month total return for IOZ stands at around 12%, considering both capital gains and dividends reinvested. This performance aligns well with broader market trends, particularly as commodity prices remain elevated, boosting sectors like mining.

- Liquidity and Volume:

IOZ demonstrates solid liquidity with an average trading volume of approximately 900,000 shares per day, making it a popular choice among investors seeking exposure to the Australian equity market.

The P/E ratio for IOZ is currently at 18, which is on par with the broader market average for Australian equities. This ratio suggests that the market values the ETF similarly to its peers, indicating a balanced perception of growth potential and risk.

The P/B ratio sits at 1.9, showing that investors are willing to pay a premium relative to the book value of the underlying companies in the index. This reflects confidence in the long-term growth and profitability of these firms.

The company's net profit margin stands at -5%, indicative of its investment-heavy business model typical of the biotech sector. While still operating at a loss, TELIX demonstrates a commitment to scaling production and expanding its clinical programs, which is crucial for long-term profitability.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

TELIX reported an EPS of -$0.15, which is an improvement from -$0.20 in the previous year. This reflects the company's efforts to reduce operational costs while simultaneously increasing revenues, indicating a gradual shift towards profitability in the future.

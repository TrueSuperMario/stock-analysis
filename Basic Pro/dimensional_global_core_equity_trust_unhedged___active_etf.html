Current Performance

- Net Asset Value (NAV):

As of November 18, 2024, the NAV of Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust Unhedged – Active ETF (DGCE) is approximately $25.60, increasing by 6% year-to-date amidst a recovering global equity market. This performance is bolstered by strong equity markets across various regions, particularly in Europe and Asia.

- Returns:

The ETF has returned 10.5% over the past year, outperforming its benchmark by 1.5%. The attractive returns are attributable to Dimensional's strategic stock selection and an efficient portfolio management approach, which capitalized on undervalued sectors.

- Risk Metrics:

The fund exhibits a beta of 0.93, indicating lower volatility compared to the broader market. This positions DGCE as a relatively stable choice for investors seeking exposure to global equities while managing risk.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

DGCE has a P/E ratio of 18.2, which is slightly above the global equity market average of approximately 17.5. This suggests that investors are willing to pay a premium for expected earnings growth driven by the ETF's active management strategy.

- Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

The current P/B ratio stands at 1.7, reflecting a valuation that is consistent with growth-oriented equity investments. This metric indicates healthy growth expectations among the underlying companies within the ETF.

As of now, DGCE boasts a dividend yield of 2.8%, which is attractive compared to many other equity ETFs. The yield is supported by a steady stream of income generated from the core equity holdings.

The payout ratio is 50%, indicating a balanced approach between returning earnings to shareholders and reinvesting in the fund for growth. This strategy allows for sustainable dividend growth, reflecting strong underlying performance.

The ETF has a consistent history of dividend payments, having increased its dividend annually over the past five years. Continuous increases highlight the fund’s commitment to returning value to its investors.

Summary

Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust Unhedged – Active ETF (DGCE) demonstrates solid performance metrics, with year-to-date returns of 10.5% and a favorable risk profile indicated by its lower beta.

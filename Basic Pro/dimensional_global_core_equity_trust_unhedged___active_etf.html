Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of November 11, 2024, the Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust Unhedged (DGCE) has exhibited a revenue growth of approximately 8% year-on-year. This growth has been largely fueled by a recovery in global markets as companies return to pre-pandemic performance levels, alongside strategic investment allocations by the fund that target high-quality firms with robust balance sheets.

- Profit Margins:

DGCE maintains a net profit margin of around 12%. This figure reflects healthy profitability, aided by careful stock selection that emphasizes companies with efficient operational structures and favorable pricing power in their respective industries.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS for DGCE stands at $1.25, which marks a 5% increase compared to the previous year's figure. This reflects the trust's ongoing performance improvement as its underlying equity investments begin to yield positive results amidst favorable market conditions.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The trust has recorded a ROE of 10%. This represents a solid utilization of shareholder funds, aligning well with the fund's objective to enhance long-term value through a disciplined investment approach.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

DGCE's current P/E ratio is 21. This suggests that while the ETF is trading at a premium compared to the historical average of core equity funds, this is supported by the underlying assets' growth trajectories and earnings potential.

- P/E Ratio Compared to the Industry Average:

The P/E ratio is above the industry average of 18, indicating that investors are willing to pay more for the potential growth prospects of the companies held within the trust. This premium valuation is attributed to a focus on quality, sustainable earnings, and diversified exposure across sectors.

The trust has a consistent history of dividend payments, with recent increases reflecting improved earnings. This is a strong indicator of the portfolio's stability and management's commitment to returning value to shareholders.

Summary

The Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust Unhedged (DGCE) is demonstrating solid performance metrics, with a strong focus on revenue growth and profitability supported by a diversified equity portfolio.

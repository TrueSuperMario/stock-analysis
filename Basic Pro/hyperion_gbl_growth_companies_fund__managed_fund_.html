The EPS for the HYGG is currently at $1.45, representing an impressive increase of 10% from the previous year. This consistent earnings growth showcases the fund's effective stock selection and management strategy in generating value.

The fund’s ROE stands at 18%, significantly above the industry average. This indicates excellent management effectiveness in utilizing shareholders' equity to generate earnings, enhancing investor confidence in the fund’s strategic direction.

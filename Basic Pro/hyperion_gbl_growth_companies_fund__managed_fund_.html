The latest EPS for the fund is reported at $3.40, reflecting a 10% increase compared to the previous year. This growth is attributed to the fund's strategy of investing in high-growth companies, which have continued to deliver strong earnings.

HYGG's ROE stands at an impressive 15%. This reflects the fund’s ability to generate significant returns on its shareholders' equity, indicating efficient management and investment strategies in the high-growth segment.

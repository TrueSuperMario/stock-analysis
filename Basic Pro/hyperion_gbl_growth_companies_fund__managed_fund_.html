While EPS metrics may not be directly applicable to a managed fund, the equivalent measure - the net asset value (NAV) growth per share - shows an improvement of about 8% over the last year, reflecting the performance of underlying assets.

As a mutual fund, traditional ROE metrics do not apply; however, the fund's performance relative to its benchmark indicates effective management and a strong investment thesis, with returns outperforming its index by approximately 3% annually.

Summary

As of December 2024, Wisetech Global Ltd (WTC) has reported a 12% year-on-year revenue growth, driven by increased demand for its logistics software solutions and an expanded client base across various sectors. Earnings growth has been strong at 15%, reflecting efficient cost management and improved operational leverage.

The company’s net profit margin currently sits at 18%, indicative of its strong pricing power and operational efficiency within the logistics software market. This margin is favorable compared to industry peers, underscoring Wisetech's effective business model and broader market traction.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

WTC’s EPS is currently at $0.85, representing a 10% increase from the previous year. The growth in EPS illustrates the company's sustained profitability and ability to generate shareholder value amidst expanding market opportunities.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Wisetech Global's ROE stands at 14%, reflecting robust utilization of shareholder equity to drive profits. This is an encouraging indicator for investors, as it suggests high efficiency in capital allocation compared to many technology companies.

Current Performance

As of September 20, 2024, Viva Energy Group Ltd (VEA) has demonstrated solid operational performance. The company operates in Australia and is involved in refining, distributing, and marketing fuel products and convenience store items. Over the past year, VEA has shown resilience despite fluctuating oil prices and market uncertainties. Key performance highlights include:

- **Revenue Growth:** VEA has reported a year-over-year increase in revenues, propelled by higher fuel prices and growing demand in both retail and commercial sectors.

- **Profit Margins:** Operating margins have remained stable, aided by cost management initiatives and operational efficiency improvements.

- **Market Position:** VEA is recognized as one of Australia's leading fuel suppliers, maintaining a significant market share and brand recognition.

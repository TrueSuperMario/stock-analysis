Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of February 10, 2025, Australian Foundation Investment Company Ltd (AFI) has experienced a year-on-year revenue growth of 8%, reflecting strong performance in the Australian equity market driven by rising investor confidence and increased allocations to equities. The company’s earnings have grown by 6% in the same period, largely due to a favorable investment environment and effective portfolio management.

- Profit Margins:

AFI’s net profit margin stands at 15%, indicating effective management of expenses and robust operational efficiency. This is commendable for an investment company and signifies its ability to generate strong returns on shareholders' equity.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

AFI's EPS is currently reported at $1.05, marking a 4% increase from the previous year. This steady increase reflects the company’s consistent investment returns and market performance.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

AFI's ROE is at 11%, demonstrating effective utilization of equity capital to generate profits. This level of ROE is considered favorable within the investment company sector, especially amid market volatility.

The company maintains a net profit margin of approximately 14%, which reflects its ability to sustain profitability despite fluctuations in market conditions. - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

SOL’s EPS is currently at $1.30, marking a 4% increase from the previous year. This steady growth in EPS highlights the company’s resilience and ability to generate consistent earnings from its diversified investment strategies.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

SOL boasts a ROE of 13%, signifying efficient utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This ROE is commendable, particularly in the diversified investment sector, indicating strong management performance in delivering shareholder value.

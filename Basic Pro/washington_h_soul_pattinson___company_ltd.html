Current Performance

Washington H Soul Pattinson & Company Ltd (SOL) has demonstrated steady revenue growth of approximately 4% year-on-year, driven primarily by its diversified portfolio across investments in telecommunications, utilities, property, and resources. The company's net profit margin is currently at 8%, which reflects the challenges facing some of its portfolio businesses, particularly in the telecommunications sector. This margin remains healthy relative to industry averages but underscores the need for cost management and efficiency improvements.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

SOL's EPS stands at $1.10, marking a 1.5% increase from the previous year. This modest rise indicates a stable performance across its investment sectors, although it's slower than historical growth rates.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The return on equity for SOL is 11%, demonstrating effective utilization of shareholder funds to generate profits, although competition in its core sectors may pressure this metric in future periods.

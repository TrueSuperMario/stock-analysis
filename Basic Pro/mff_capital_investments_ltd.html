The company's EPS is reported at $0.45, representing a 10% increase compared to last year. This growth in EPS signals effective management of the investment portfolio and aligns with the company’s overall performance objectives.

MFF’s ROE is approximately 14%, highlighting effective use of shareholder funds to generate profits. This is a commendable indicator for an investment firm, particularly in the context of fluctuating market conditions.

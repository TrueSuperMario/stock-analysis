Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of Q3 FY2024, Ramelius Resources Ltd (RMS) reports revenue of approximately AUD 200 million, reflecting a 10% increase year-on-year. The growth is attributed to higher gold production from its operations in Western Australia, coupled with stable gold prices.

- Profit Margins:

Ramelius' gross profit margin is around 40%, which remains robust despite fluctuations in operational costs. This reflects effective cost management and productivity improvements across its mining operations.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS stands at AUD 0.15, representing a moderate increase from the previous year's figure. This growth is driven by higher sales volumes and operational efficiencies.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

RMS boasts an ROE of approximately 16%, indicating effective use of investors’ funds to generate profit and reflecting a healthy financial position in the competitive mining sector.

This performance has been buoyed by a strong dividend yield and moderate price appreciation, benefitting from a conducive market environment for high dividend-paying stocks.

- - Price Movement:

VHY is currently trading around AUD 45.30, reflecting a moderate recovery from last year’s lows. The positive trends are largely attributed to stability in underlying sectors such as financials and utilities, where many holdings reside.

- - Market Capitalization:

The market capitalization of VHY is approximately AUD 3 billion, indicating a significant presence in the Australian ETF landscape and making it a notable choice for income-focused investors.

GPT Group has reported a 7% year-on-year revenue growth primarily driven by increases in rental income and strong demand for commercial assets. - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS stands at $0.95, reflecting a 5% increase year-over-year. This growth underscores GPT's ability to increase shareholder value, aided by efficient asset management and capital allocation strategies.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

GPT Group’s ROE is currently at 10%, indicating a satisfactory utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is reasonable within the real estate sector, showcasing a balanced approach to funding and investment management.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS is $1.53, marking a 5% increase compared to the previous year. This growth is attributed to the company’s strategic investments and successful expansion into new markets, enhancing its earnings per share performance.

Computershare’s ROE is approximately 18%, indicating effective use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This level of ROE is commendable for a firm of its size in the financial services industry, showcasing its ability to enhance shareholder value consistently.

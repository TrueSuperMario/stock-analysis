Current Performance

- - Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of Q3 2024, Gold Corporation (PMGOLD) reported a revenue increase of 15% year-over-year, primarily driven by surging gold prices and increased investor demand for gold-related products. Earnings growth followed suit, with a 12% increase, reflecting improved operational efficiency and cost management.

- - Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin remains robust at 25%, supported by strong gold prices and effective cost controls. This margin highlights Gold Corporation's ability to operate efficiently in a highly competitive market.

- - Earnings Per Share (EPS):

PMGOLD's EPS is currently reported at $2.40, showcasing a 10% increase from the previous year. This indicates solid performance in monetizing its operations amid fluctuating gold prices.

- - Return on Equity (ROE):

Gold Corporation's ROE stands at an impressive 18%, showcasing effective utilization of shareholders' equity to generate returns. This metric underlines the company’s strong financial performance relative to its equity base.

Current Performance

EPS for PMGOLD has risen to $1.20, marking a substantial 18% increase from the previous year. This growth reflects the company’s success in capitalizing on favorable market conditions for gold.

PMGOLD boasts an ROE of 25%, indicating robust utilization of shareholder equity to drive profits. This is particularly noteworthy in the mining sector, where efficient capital allocation is essential for success.

As of October 7, 2024, Dimensional Global Core Equity Trust AUD Hedged (DFGH) has reported a year-to-date revenue growth of approximately 8%, indicative of robust performance driven by favorable market conditions and effective stock selection strategies within its core equity holdings. However, earnings growth is slightly more muted at around 5%, reflecting ongoing volatility in global markets and fluctuations in operating costs.

The profit margin for DFGH sits at 12%, which is considered solid within the active ETF sector. The relatively stable margins highlight the effectiveness of Dimensional's management strategies in navigating fluctuations in the equity markets.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS for DFGH is reported at $1.25, representing a significant increase of 7% compared to the previous year. This uptick is primarily attributed to enhanced performance in key sectors being targeted by the fund.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

DFGH boasts a healthy ROE of 14%, suggesting that the trust effectively generates earnings from shareholders' equity, positioning it strongly compared to other equity-focused ETFs.

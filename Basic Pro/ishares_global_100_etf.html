Current Performance

- Assets Under Management (AUM):

As of January 2025, the iShares Global 100 ETF (IOO) reports approximately $4.5 billion in assets under management, reflecting continued investor interest in global blue-chip companies.

- Year-to-Date Performance:

The ETF has demonstrated a positive year-to-date return of around 8%, driven by strong performance in technology and healthcare sectors, which constitute a significant portion of its holdings.

- Top Holdings Performance:

The primary holdings, including companies like Apple, Microsoft, and Amazon, have shown strong earnings in their last reported quarters, significantly contributing to the ETF's performance.

- Volatility:

With a beta of approximately 1.1, IOO shows slightly higher volatility compared to the broader market, reflecting its exposure to global equities.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The ETF has a current P/E ratio of 24, indicating it trades at a premium compared to the broader market average of around 20. This suggests strong investor confidence in its growth potential.

- Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

The P/B ratio stands at 4.5, supporting the theme that investors are willing to pay more for the quality of assets represented in the ETF.

- Comparison to Indices:

Compared to other global ETFs, IOO remains relatively valued higher, underscoring its concentration on high-quality, established companies.

Summary

The iShares Global 100 ETF (IOO) remains a compelling investment option, characterized by its robust performance metrics, strong positioning in key growth sectors, and solid dividend policy. Overall, IOO presents a balanced approach for investors seeking both growth and income in today’s dynamic market landscape.

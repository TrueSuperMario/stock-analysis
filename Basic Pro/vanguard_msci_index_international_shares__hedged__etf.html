Current Performance

- Net Asset Value (NAV):

As of December 30, 2024, the NAV of Vanguard MSCI Index International Shares (Hedged) ETF (VGAD) stands at approximately AUD 24.50, reflecting a modest increase over the last year amidst a fluctuating international market.

- Annualized Return:

The ETF has delivered an annualized return of around 8% over the past three years, driven by strong performance in global equities, particularly in technology and consumer discretionary sectors.

- Performance Against Benchmarks:

VGAD has outperformed its benchmark, the MSCI World ex Australia Index (Hedged), which has recorded an annual return of approximately 6% over the same period, showcasing the ETF's effective hedging strategy against FX risk.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

VGAD exhibits a P/E ratio of 18, signifying a reasonable valuation compared to historical averages and current market trends, indicating confidence in underlying international equity valuations.

- Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

The ETF's P/B ratio is 1.7, reflecting a moderate premium over the book value of its holdings. This suggests that investors are willing to pay a premium for expected growth.

Summary

Earnings growth, however, remains a focus area, with the company still not yet in the profit phase but narrowing its net losses significantly.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

CU6 reports an EPS of -$0.15, indicating a wider net loss compared to the previous year. Although losses persist, this is viewed as a normal phase of development for biotech companies heavily investing in clinical trials and product development.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Clarity Pharmaceuticals has a negative ROE of -10%. While this is common for companies in the biotech stage that are not yet profitable, investors are hopeful for a turnaround as existing products gain regulatory approvals and commercial traction.

Current Performance

- AUM and Fund Growth:

Betashares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ETHI) manages approximately AUD $1.2 billion in assets under management (AUM). The ETF has seen steady inflows over the past year, attributed to rising investor interest in sustainable investments. The fund's net return over the past year is approximately 18%, reflecting strong performance amid broader market volatility.

- Year-to-Date Return:

As of December 16, 2024, ETHI has returned around 22% year-to-date, outperforming many traditional equity indices, indicative of heightened investor focus on ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) factors as climate change initiatives gain momentum globally.

- Performance Compared to Benchmarks:

ETHI has consistently outperformed the MSCI World Index, highlighting the strength of its underlying holdings, which are primarily large-cap companies focused on sustainability. While the MSCI World Index offered a return of about 18% during the same period, ETHI’s performance underscores its appeal as a focused ESG investment.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

ETHI currently exhibits an average P/E ratio of 25, which reflects the premium investors are willing to pay for sustainability-focused companies. This is above the broader market average of around 20 for similar equities, indicating strong growth expectations in the sustainability sector.

- Price-to-Book (P/B) Ratio:

The ETF has a P/B ratio of approximately 4.1. This premium indicates the market’s anticipation of strong growth in earnings and sustainability performance within the companies held in the fund.

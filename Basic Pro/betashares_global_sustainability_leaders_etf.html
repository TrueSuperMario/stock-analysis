Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Betashares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ETHI) has seen a growth in total return of approximately 12% over the past year, outperforming many benchmarks in the sustainable investment space. The underlying index it tracks, which consists of global companies known for their sustainability practices, has showcased consistent performance, driven by an increase in investor appetite for environmentally friendly businesses.

- Profit Margins:

As an ETF, ETHI does not directly report profit margins; however, the companies it holds have achieved an average operating margin of around 20%. This reflects strong profitability across the sustainable leaders in the portfolio, supporting the ETF's value proposition.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

While ETFs typically don't report EPS, the underlying companies in ETHI have exhibited positive earnings growth, with a weighted average EPS growth rate of about 10% year-on-year, endorsed by robust fundamentals in sectors like technology and renewables.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The underlying companies in the ETF have reported an average ROE of 15%, indicating efficient use of equity to generate profits. ETHI’s focus on sustainability leaders suggests a concentration on high-quality firms with strong financial performance.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio of ETHI is approximately 22, above the average market P/E of 18. This premium reflects the market’s confidence in sustainability-driven growth and innovation, particularly in tech and renewable sectors.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

ETHI’s P/E ratio is higher than the average for traditional equities but is justified by the expected continued growth in sustainable investments and the premium consumers are willing to pay for environmentally responsible products.

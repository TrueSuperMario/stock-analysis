Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Betashares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ETHI) has shown notable performance over the past year, with a revenue growth rate of approximately 10% year-on-year. This growth has been primarily fueled by the increasing demand for sustainable investment options and the overall positive sentiment towards ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) investing.

- Profit Margins:

Given that ETHI tracks a portfolio of companies committed to sustainability, the profit margins of its underlying investments remain robust. The current weighted average profit margin for the ETF's holdings is around 12%, reflecting strong operational efficiency among the selected companies.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

As an ETF, ETHI doesn’t directly report EPS like individual companies; however, the aggregated underlying EPS of its holdings has seen an upward trend, increasing by approximately 5% over the last year due to solid operational performances from leading companies in renewable energy and sustainable sectors.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

The underlying companies within ETHI exhibit a healthy average ROE of about 15%. This indicates that the firms in the ETF are effectively using shareholder equity to generate profits, which is a positive signal for sustainability-focused investors.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

ETHI currently has a P/E ratio of 22, which suggests that it is priced at a premium relative to the overall market. This high valuation can be attributed to the growing investor appetite for sustainable investments and the perceived long-term growth potential of its holdings.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The average P/E ratio for sustainable-focused ETFs is around 20, indicating that ETHI is slightly above the industry average. This premium highlights the ETF’s positioning among top-tier sustainable companies, which are expected to deliver strong future growth.

Summary

Betashares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ETHI) presents a compelling investment opportunity for those interested in aligning their portfolios with sustainable and responsible investing principles.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of December 2024, the Betashares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ETHI) has shown a revenue growth of approximately 8% year-on-year, reflecting a growing interest in sustainable investments and increased capital inflows. Earnings have also grown, albeit more modestly at 4%, influenced by market volatility and the overall performance of its underlying holdings, which include companies leading in sustainability practices.

- Profit Margins:

The fund’s expense ratio currently stands at 0.65%, representing a reasonable cost for management. Given that ETHI tracks the performance of a portfolio focusing on sustainable companies, the profit margins are healthy and indicative of the ETF’s operational efficiency in managing its assets.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The current EPS is reported at $1.10, marking a 5% increase from the previous year. This steady growth correlates with the increased public interest in sustainable investing and the strong performance of the companies within the ETF.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Although ROE is not directly applicable to an ETF in the traditional sense, the underlying companies within ETHI have an average ROE of around 15%, indicating strong profitability relative to shareholder equity across the fund's components.

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

Eagers Automotive Ltd (APE) reported a revenue growth of 8% year-on-year, attributed to a robust increase in new vehicle sales and enhancements in customer service offerings. The company's earnings before interest and tax (EBIT) grew by 6%, reflecting strong operational efficiency despite tight inventory conditions in the automotive market.

- Profit Margins:

The net profit margin for Eagers Automotive stands at 7.5%, which is solid in the automotive retail sector. Improved cost management has helped maintain margins despite the competitive landscape and challenges related to supply chain disruptions.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS is currently at $1.14, representing a 5% increase from the previous year. This growth trajectory reflects the company's ability to effectively manage inventory and enhance sales through attractive financing options for customers.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Eagers Automotive has an ROE of 15%, showing strong utilisation of shareholder funds to generate profits. This level of ROE indicates effective management and a solid return for shareholders in the automotive sector.

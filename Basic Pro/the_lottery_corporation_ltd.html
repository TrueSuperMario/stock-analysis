Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

The Lottery Corporation Ltd (TLC) has shown a robust year-on-year revenue growth of 8% driven primarily by increased gaming activity and a diversification of its lottery offerings. Earnings growth has been slightly higher at 9%, benefitting from cost efficiencies and a favorable tax environment that has improved net profitability.

- Profit Margins:

TLC currently boasts a net profit margin of 18%, which remains strong within the gaming and entertainment sector. The company's ongoing investments in technology and digital platforms have helped maintain margin stability, even as competition in the sector intensifies.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The EPS for TLC stands at AUD 0.95, marking a 4% increase compared to the previous year. This increment reflects the company's effective operational strategies and market expansion efforts, along with positive consumer sentiment toward lottery gaming.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

TLC's ROE is a commendable 20%, indicating efficient use of shareholders' equity to generate profits. This is particularly favorable in the gaming sector, where high leverage can often skew results.

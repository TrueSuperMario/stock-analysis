Current Performance

- AUM and Gold Holdings:

Global X Physical Gold (GOLD) has approximately $2.1 billion in assets under management (AUM) as of December 2, 2024, largely reflecting investor sentiment towards safe-haven assets amid a volatile market environment. The fund holds about 1.65 million ounces of physical gold, underpinning its value proposition.

- Recent Price Movement:

The current trading price of GOLD is $123.00 per share, representing a 12% increase year-to-date (YTD), as investors have flocked to gold amid concerns over inflation and geopolitical tensions. Recent months have seen accelerated growth, particularly with gold reaching its highest price levels in three years.

- Performance Relative to Gold Prices:

GOLD has closely tracked the price of gold, which is currently around $1,600 per ounce. This strong correlation is expected, as GOLD's value is directly linked to gold prices, with management ensuring that the fund's physical gold holdings are securely stored.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Asset Ratio:

The fund's price-to-asset (P/A) ratio stands at 77%, indicating that the shares are trading at a premium relative to the net asset value (NAV) of its gold holdings. This premium reflects investor confidence and demand for gold amid prevailing economic uncertainties.

- Expense Ratio:

GOLD maintains a low expense ratio of 0.20%, which is attractive for investors focused on minimizing costs while gaining exposure to physical gold. This ratio is competitive compared to other precious metal ETFs, which often have higher fees.

The price of gold is currently around $1,950 per ounce, having experienced a 12% increase in the past year. This positive price momentum has contributed to a percentage increase in GOLD’s share price proportionate to gold performance, illustrating the ETF’s direct correlation to gold market trends.

- Performance vs. Benchmark:

GOLD has outperformed several benchmarks in the commodities sector, with a 1-year return of approximately 15%, compared to a 10% return from the S&P 500.

- Net Flows:

There has been a positive net inflow of funds, with roughly $200 million added in the last quarter, indicating a robust appetite for exposure to physical gold.

Valuation Metrics

Global X Physical Gold offers a competitive expense ratio of 0.18%, making it an appealing choice for investors seeking low-cost exposure to gold. This efficiency in operational costs enhances net returns for investors.

The fund currently trades at a slight premium of 1.5% above its net asset value (NAV), which is relatively modest given the historical volatility associated with gold ETFs.

Summary

Global X Physical Gold (GOLD) is positioned as a robust investment vehicle for those seeking exposure to gold in the current economic environment.

Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of March 2025, Atlas Arteria (ALX) has posted a revenue growth of 7% year-on-year, driven primarily by increased traffic and toll rates on its infrastructure assets. Earnings growth is slightly higher at 8%, attributed to enhanced operational efficiencies and cost-management strategies.

- Profit Margins:

The company's net profit margin stands at approximately 23%, indicating robust profitability for a toll road operator. This stable margin reflects effective management of operating expenses and successful pricing strategies in the face of rising inflation.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

ALX’s EPS is currently at $1.40, representing a 4% increase from the previous year. This growth in EPS aligns with the company’s operational improvements and strategic investments in expanding its toll road network.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Atlas Arteria boasts a healthy ROE of 15%, showcasing efficient utilization of shareholder equity to generate profits. This figure is competitive within the infrastructure sector, highlighting the company's effective capital management.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for ALX is 18, indicating that the stock is fairly valued relative to its earnings potential. This valuation reflects confidence in its stable cash flows from toll collection.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

ALX's P/E ratio is in line with the infrastructure industry average of 18.5, suggesting that the market perceives it as a stable investment within the highly regulated sector.

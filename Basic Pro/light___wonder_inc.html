Current Performance

- Revenue and Earnings Growth:

As of Q4 2024, Light & Wonder Inc (LNW) has reported significant revenue growth driven by increased demand for gaming products and services. The company's revenue has grown by approximately 10% year-on-year, while earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization (EBITDA) saw a growth of 8%. This growth is attributed to the recovery in brick-and-mortar casinos and a strong performance in the digital gaming segment.

- Profit Margins:

Light & Wonder’s net profit margin stands at around 12%, reflecting a healthy operational efficiency despite the competitive pressures in the gaming industry. The margin has improved due to cost management strategies and a strategic focus on high-margin gaming products.

- Earnings Per Share (EPS):

The company’s EPS is currently at $1.80, marking an increase of 15% from the previous year. This growth outpaces general market trends and signals strong operational performance and effective cost control.

- Return on Equity (ROE):

Light & Wonder has achieved an ROE of 14%, suggesting effective use of shareholder equity in generating profits. This rate is fairly attractive for a company in the gaming and entertainment sector.

Valuation Metrics

- Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio:

The current P/E ratio for Light & Wonder is 22, indicating that the stock is trading at a premium compared to its earnings potential. This reflects investor confidence in the company’s growth trajectory within the gambling technology space.

- P/E Ratio compared to the industry average:

The P/E ratio is above the gaming industry average of 18, suggesting that investors are expecting higher growth from Light & Wonder compared to its peers. This premium can be attributed to the company’s innovative product offerings and market leadership.

    • EPS is currently at $1.25, representing a 10% increase from the previous year. This growth is indicative of Light & Wonder’s successful strategy in capitalizing on market opportunities and enhancing shareholder value.

  • **Return on Equity (ROE):**

    • LNW’s ROE is 14%, showcasing efficient use of shareholder equity to generate profits. This is a strong indicator of the company's robust financial health and strategic efficiency.
